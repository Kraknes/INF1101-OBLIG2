Nova Kapela-Batrina railway station () is a railway station on Novska-Tovarnik railway. Located between two settlements, Nova Kapela and Batrina. Railroad continued to Staro Petrovo Selo in one, in the other direction to Oriovac and the third direction towards to Ratkovica.
