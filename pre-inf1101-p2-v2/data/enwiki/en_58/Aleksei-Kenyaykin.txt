Aleksei Nikolayevich Kenyaykin (; born 23 August 1998) is a Russian football player. He plays for FC Orenburg. ==Club career== He made his debut in the Russian Professional Football League for FC Orenburg-2 on 18 August 2017 in a game against FC Anzhi-Yunior Zelenodolsk. On 4 July 2019, he joined FC Torpedo Moscow on loan for the 2019-20 season.
