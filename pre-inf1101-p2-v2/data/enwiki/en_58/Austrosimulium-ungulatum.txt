Austrosimulium ungulatum, known by the common name West Coast black fly or just sandfly, is a species of small fly of the family Simuliidae. It is endemic to New Zealand and is found in the South Island and Stewart Island. ==References== == External links == * Austrosimulium ungulatum discussed on RNZ Critter of the Week, 15 Feb 2019 * Citizen science observations Category:Simuliidae Category:Diptera of New Zealand Category:Endemic fauna of New Zealand Category:Insects described in 1925
