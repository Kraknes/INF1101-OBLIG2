Faunis kirata, the broad striped faun, is a butterfly in the family Nymphalidae. It was described by Lionel de Niceville in 1891. It is found in Peninsular Malaya, Sumatra and Borneo in the Indomalayan realm.Seitz, A., 1912-1927.
