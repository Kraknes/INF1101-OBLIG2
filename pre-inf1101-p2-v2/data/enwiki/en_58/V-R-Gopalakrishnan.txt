V. R. Gopalakrishnan was a Malayalam film director and story writer. ==Career== He wrote the story / screenplay / dialogues for 16 films. His noteworthy films are Dheem Tharikida Thom, Cheppu and Vandanam, all directed by Priyadarshan. Gopalakrishnan also directed 4 films, one of which was unreleased.
