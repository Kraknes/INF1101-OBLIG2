Paul Morris is an Australian former professional rugby league footballer who played in the 1990s. Primarily a er, he played for the Brisbane Broncos and was a foundation player for the North Queensland Cowboys. ==Playing career== Originally from Rockhampton, Morris made his first grade debut for the Brisbane Broncos in Round 13 of the 1994 NSWRL season, starting on the wing and scoring a try in their 10-29 loss to the Canberra Raiders. He would play three more games for them that season, coming off the bench in all three, before joining the North Queensland Cowboys.
