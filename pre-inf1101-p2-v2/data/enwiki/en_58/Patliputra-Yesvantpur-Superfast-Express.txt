The Patliputra - Yesvantpur Superfast Express is a Superfast train belonging to East Central Railway zone that runs between Patliputra Junction in Bihar and Yesvantpur Junction in Karnataka. It is currently being operated with 22351/22352 train numbers on weekly basis.It runs with LHB coaches with effect from 27 December 2019.EAST CENTRAL RAILWAY TO RUN TRAIN NOs. 22351/22352 PATALIPUTRA-YESVANTPUR-PATALIPUTRA WEEKLY EXPRESS UP TO PATALIPUTRA INSTEAD OF DANAPUR == Service== The 22351/Patliputra - Yesvantpur Express has averages speed of 56 km/hr and covers 2711 km in 48h 40m.
