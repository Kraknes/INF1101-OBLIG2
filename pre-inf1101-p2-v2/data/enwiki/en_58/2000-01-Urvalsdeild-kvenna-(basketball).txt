The 2000-2001 Urvalsdeild kvenna was the 43rd season of the Urvalsdeild kvenna, the top tier women's basketball league in Iceland. The season started on October 14, 2000 and ended on March 31 10, 2001. KR won its twelfth title by defeating Keflavik 3-0 in the Finals. ==Competition format== The participating teams first played a conventional round-robin schedule with every team playing each opponent twice "home" and twice "away" for a total of 16 games.
