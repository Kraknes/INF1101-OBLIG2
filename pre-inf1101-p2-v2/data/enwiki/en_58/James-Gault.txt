Sir James Gault Brigadier Sir James Frederick Gault KCMG (26 June 1902 - 14 January 1977) was a British brigadier and military assistant to General Dwight Eisenhower in the UK during World War II. James Gault was the son of Leslie Hamilton Gault (1855-1922) and Marion Anderson (1857-1939), one of five children. He was the grandson of Matthew Hamilton Gault. He was educated at Eton College and Trinity College, Cambridge."Gault, Brig. Sir James (Frederick), (26 June 1902-14 Jan. 1977)."
