Night of Decision () is a 1956 West German drama film directed by Falk Harnack and starring Carl Raddatz, Hilde Krahl and Albert Lieven.Bock & Bergfelder p. 383 It was shot in studios in Gottingen and on location in Belgium. The film's sets were designed by the art directors Walter Haag. ==Synopsis== After ten years as a prisoner in Siberia, a Belgian industrialist returns home to find that he has been declared dead and his wife has remarried. ==Cast== * Carl Raddatz as Rene Dobersin * Hilde Krahl as Claire Vernon * Albert Lieven as Albert Vernon * Ernst Schroder as Jacques Ardent * Gisela Tantau as Monique Dobersin * Harry Meyen as Philip Ardent * Alfred Schieske as Andre * Joseph Offenbach as Smozik * Hans Hessling as Jules * Fritz Remond Jr. as Professor Mareau * Maria Sebaldt as Penny * Paul Gunther as Matthieu * Gerd E.
