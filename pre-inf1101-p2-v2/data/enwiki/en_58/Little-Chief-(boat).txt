Little Chief is a 1926 boat plying St. Mary Lake, renamed from Rising Wolf in 1990 after a major restoration. It is a long boat which is wide, made of planked cedar on an oak frame. Its stem and keel are made of fir and it has a steel stem band. It is USCG-rated to carry 49 passengers and two crew members, and is registered at 13 gross tons and 10 net tons. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2016 as "Rising Wolf". With photos.
