Jack Sparkes (born 29 September 2000) is an English footballer who plays for Exeter City as a midfielder. ==Career== Sparkes began his career with Exeter City and made his professional debut for the Grecians on 12 August 2017 in a 1-1 away draw at Swindon Town. He then made his first appearance at Exeter's home ground St James Park the following week in a 1-0 win over Lincoln City. He scored his first goal for Exeter in an EFL Trophy tie against Yeovil Town on 29 August 2017. In October 2018, Sparkes joined National League South side Chippenham Town on a one-month loan deal.
