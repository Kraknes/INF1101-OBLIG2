The Aviator Monument (Polish: Pomnik Lotnika) is a monument made in honor of Franciszek Zwirko and Stanislaw Wigura, two aviation heroes of Poland. It is a replica of the original monument that was unveiled in 1932 in and destroyed by Nazis in 1944. The current monument was constructed in 1967.
