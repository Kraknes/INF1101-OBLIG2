"Me" is a song written, produced and performed by Paula Cole. It was released as the third and final single from her double-platinum album This Fire. Unlike her previous two singles, "Me" underperformed commercially. As it was not released commercially in the United States, it was not eligible to chart on the US Billboard Hot 100. It did peak at number 17 on the Billboard Adult Top 40 and entered the top 40 on Billboards Hot 100 Airplay chart. In Canada the song reached number 20 on the RPM Top Singles chart.
