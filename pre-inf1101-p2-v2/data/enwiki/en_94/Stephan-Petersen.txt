Stephan Petersen (born 15 November 1985) is a Danish footballer who plays as a winger for HB Koge. ==Career== Stephan Petersen can cover both edges of the midfield and attack. He is physically strong, quick, good head and a good run from his opponent He was signed from Danish Superliga side FC Nordsjaelland on 1 January 2010.
