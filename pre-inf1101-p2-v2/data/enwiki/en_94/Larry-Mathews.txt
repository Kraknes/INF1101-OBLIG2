Larry Mathews (born August 15, 1955, in Burbank, California) is an American actor, born as Larry Mazzeo, he is known for his role as Ritchie Petrie on The Dick Van Dyke Show. After the series ended in 1966, Larry Mathews left acting to pursue a more conventional childhood and graduated from the University of California, Los Angeles in 1976. He has also worked as an account executive and has appeared on numerous television programs to discuss his role on The Dick Van Dyke Show. As an actor, Mathews appeared in The Dick Van Dyke Show Revisited (2004), reprising the role of Ritchie.Larry Mathews IMDB.com.
