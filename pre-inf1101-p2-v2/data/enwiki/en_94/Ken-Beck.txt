Ken Beck (born 4 August 1940) is a retired Australian rules footballer who played with Hawthorn in the Victorian Football League (VFL). Originally from Stawell, Beck was a tall ruckman and made his league debut in 1962. He was a regular member of the team during the 1960s and was a reserve in their 1971 premiership side. ==References== *Holmesby, Russell and Main, Jim (2007).
