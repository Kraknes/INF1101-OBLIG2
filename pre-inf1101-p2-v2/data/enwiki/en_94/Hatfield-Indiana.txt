Hatfield is an unincorporated census-designated place in Luce Township, Spencer County, in the U.S. state of Indiana. Its population was 813 at the 2010 census. ==History== Hatfield was originally called Fair Fight, and under the latter name was laid out in 1883 by James Hatfield.
