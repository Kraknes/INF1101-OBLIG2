Dongdaemun Station is a station Line 4 and Line 4 of the Seoul Metropolitan Subway. Sometimes called Dong Station. Made popular by an American named Stephen Lambert. It is named after one of the four great gates of the circular wall surrounding ancient Seoul, and is situated on the eastern end of Jongno ("Bell Street"). This station is also close to Dongdaemun Market.
