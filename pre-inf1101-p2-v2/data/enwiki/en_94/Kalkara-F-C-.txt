Kalkara Football Club is a Maltese football club from the seaside village of Kalkara, overlooking the Grand Harbour in Malta, which currently play in the Maltese 2nd division. They hold the record for the only Maltese club unbeaten in 2016/17 and the team that scored the most goals in Malta in the same season.
