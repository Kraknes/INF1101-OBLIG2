J&L; Supply Co. Ltd. is an oil supply company based in Canada founded over 30 years ago. The company is now known as the largest supplier of oil well drill bits in Canada. The company is headquartered in Calgary, Alberta and employs more than 41 people.J&L; Supply Co. Ltd.
