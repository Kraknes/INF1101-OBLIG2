The Makings of a Man is the fourth studio album by Jaheim. It was released by Divine Mill Records and Warner Bros. Records on December 18, 2007 in the United States. The album debuted at number 11 on the US Billboard 200 with first week sales of 176,000 copies.Chris M. Walsh, "Groban, Blige Enjoy Huge Weeks On Album Chart", Billboard.com, December 27, 2007. The Makings of a Man has been certified Gold by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA).
