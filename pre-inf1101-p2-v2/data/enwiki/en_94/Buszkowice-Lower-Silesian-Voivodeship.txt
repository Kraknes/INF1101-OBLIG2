Buszkowice () is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Scinawa, within Lubin County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Prior to 1945 it was in Germany. It lies approximately north of Scinawa, north-east of Lubin, and north-west of the regional capital Wroclaw.
