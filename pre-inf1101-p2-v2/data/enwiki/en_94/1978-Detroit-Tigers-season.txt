The 1978 Detroit Tigers finished in fifth place in the American League East with a record of 86-76, 13 1/2 games behind the Yankees. They outscored their opponents 714 to 653. It was the franchise's first winning season since 1973 and would start a string of winning seasons that would not come to an end until 1989. == Offseason == * January 10, 1978: Chris Codiroli was
