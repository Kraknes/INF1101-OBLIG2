Alexander George "Alex" Sinclair (June 28, 1911 - October 2, 2002)Alexander Sinclair's obituary was a Canadian ice hockey player who competed in the 1936 Winter Olympics. Sinclair was a member of the 1936 Port Arthur Bearcats, which won the silver medal for Canada in ice hockey at the 1936 Winter Olympics.
