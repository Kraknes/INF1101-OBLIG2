Silvestre Vargas (1901-1985), 250px Silvestre Vargas (31 December, 1901 - October 7, 1985, Guadalajara) was a Mexican mariachi musician. In 1928, he became the leader of Mariachi Vargas de Tecalitlan, an ensemble from Jalisco begun by his father in 1898. In the 1930s the group moved to Mexico City, and Vargas, along with Ruben Fuentes, became pivotal composers in the evolution of the genre.History of the Mariachi, Mariachi.org. Silvestre and the Mariachi Vargas made dozens of recordings and starred in many films through the 1960s. He died in 1985, and his gravesite became a popular site for pilgrimages on the date of his death.
