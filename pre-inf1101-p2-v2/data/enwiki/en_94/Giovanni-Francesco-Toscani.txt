Duomo, Prato. Giovanni Francesco Toscani (1372 - 2 May 1430) was an Italian painter. Born in Florence, he was the pupil of Giottino, in whose style he painted. His masterpiece is said to have been an Annunciation for a chapel of the Bishop's palace in Arezzo. It was restored by Agnolo di Lorenzo, and later by Vasari.
