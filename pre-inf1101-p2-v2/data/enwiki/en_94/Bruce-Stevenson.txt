Bruce Stevenson (born 8 August 1949) is a former Australian rules footballer who played with Hawthorn Football Club in the Victorian Football League (VFL) and Central District and West Adelaide Football Clubs in the South Australian National Football League (SANFL). Recruited from Swinburne Tech, Stevenson debuted in 1969 for Hawthorn and was a premiership player for Hawthorn in 1971, teaming well with Don Scott in the ruck.
