Save the Tiger Fund (or STF for short) was established in 1995 as a partnership between the ExxonMobil Foundation and the National Fish and Wildlife Foundation, STF gave 336 grants totaling $17.3 million between 1995 and 2009, amounting to about one quarter of all philanthropic funds spent on tiger conservation globally.
