Pauline L. Schultz (June 7, 1915 - October 30, 2011) was an archivist and writer on Wyoming history. She also founded the Salt Creek Oil Field Museum in 1980. Schultz received one of the 2007 National Humanities Medals. The award cited her for "stewardship of a precious trove of local historical knowledge.
