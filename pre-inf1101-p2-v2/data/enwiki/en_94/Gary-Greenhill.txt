Gary Greenhill (born 16 June 1985 in Kirkcaldy, Fife) is a Scottish professional football midfielder who is who plays for Dundonald Bluebell Juniors. ==Career== He started his career with Dunfermline Athletic and was part of the squad that lost to Celtic in the 2004 League Cup Final. He then moved on to East Fife. Gary played for Berwick Rangers following his release from East Fife and helped Berwick win the Scottish 3rd Division title in season 06/07. Realising their mistake in releasing Gary East Fife then bought him back from Berwick and he also helped East Fife win the Scottish 3rd division title in season 07/08.
