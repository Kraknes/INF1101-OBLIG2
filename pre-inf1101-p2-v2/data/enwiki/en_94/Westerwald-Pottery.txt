Domestic pottery Westerwald Pottery or Stoneware is a distinctive type of salt glazed grey pottery from the Hohr-Grenzhausen and Ransbach-Baumbach area of Westerwaldkreis in Rheinland-Pfalz, Germany. It is known as Kannenbackerland Stoneware. Typically it is decorated with cobalt blue painted designs, though the collectable items are more multicoloured. There is a pottery museum at Hohr-Grenzhausen that displays the construction techniques and the history of salt glaze.
