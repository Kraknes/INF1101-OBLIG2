Sodium malate is a compound with formula Na2(C2H4O(COO)2). It is the sodium salt of malic acid. As a food additive, it has the E number E350.Current EU approved additives and their E Numbers, Food Standards Agency, 11 January 2017 == Properties == Sodium malate is an odorless white crystalline powder. It is freely soluble in water. == Use == It is used as an acidity regulator and flavoring agent. It tastes similar to sodium chloride (table salt). ==References== Category:Malates Category:Organic sodium salts Category:E-number additives
