This following is a list of members of the Italian Antimafia Commission, a bicameral body of the Italian Parliament, composed of members from the Chamber of Deputies and the Senate. The Antimafia Commission is a commission of inquiry into, initially, the "phenomenon of the Mafia". Subsequent commissions investigated "organized crime of the Mafia type", which included other Italian criminal organizations such as the Camorra, the 'Ndrangheta and the Sacra Corona Unita.
