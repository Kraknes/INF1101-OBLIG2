The Centre for Energy and Environmental Markets (CEEM) at the University of New South Wales conducts interdisciplinary research into energy and environmental markets and associated policies. CEEM brings together researchers from the areas of Business, Engineering and Social Sciences. In addition to undertaking research, CEEM hosts short courses and seminars and participates in conferences in Australia and internationally.
