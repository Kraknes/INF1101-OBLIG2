Mario Neto, known as Sukata (born 1974 in Recife, Brazil) is a Brazilian mixed martial artist with a record of 12-5.Mario Sukata e Ouro no Mundial Master He is now in his 15th year as a mixed martial artist with a victory over UFC veterans Seth Petruzelli, Travis Fulton and Gary Goodridge.
