Ingmar Ljones (born 3 December 1943 in Strandebarm) is a Norwegian politician for the Christian Democratic Party. He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament from Hordaland in 2001, but was not re-elected in 2005. He then served in the position of deputy representative during the term 2005-2009\.
