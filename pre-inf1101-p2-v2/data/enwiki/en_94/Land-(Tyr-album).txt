Land is the fourth full-length album by the Faroese Viking / folk metal band Tyr. It is a multilingual album with vocals in Faroese, English, Norwegian, Danish in Sinklars Visa and Icelandic in Brennivin. It was released on May 30, 2008 through Napalm Records. The album is based on Nordic folklore.
