The 22nd General Assembly of Prince Edward Island represented the colony of Prince Edward Island between March 3, 1863, and 1867. The Assembly sat at the pleasure of the Governor of Prince Edward Island, George Dundas. Thomas Heath Haviland was elected speaker.
