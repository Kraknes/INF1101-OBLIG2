Martland is an unincorporated community in Fillmore County, Nebraska, United States. It lies at an elevation of 1640 feet (500 m). ==History== Martland got its start following construction of the railroad through the territory. A post office was established at Martland in 1889, and remained in operation until it was discontinued in 1949. ==References== Category:Unincorporated communities in Fillmore County, Nebraska Category:Unincorporated communities in Nebraska
