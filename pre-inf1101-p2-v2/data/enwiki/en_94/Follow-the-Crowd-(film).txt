Follow the Crowd is a 1918 American short comedy film with Harold Lloyd. Previously thought to be a lost film, the SilentEra website says now that a "print exists". A truncated version (of slightly less than seven minutes), with both English and Spanish intertitles, was posted on YouTube in 2016.
