Acanthofrontia is a genus of erebid moths in the subfamily Arctiinae with Afrotropical distribution. It was described by George Hampson in 1910. ==Species== As of November 2019, AfroMoths lists the following species: * Acanthofrontia anacantha Hampson, 1914 * Acanthofrontia atricosta Hampson, 1918 * Acanthofrontia bianulata (Wichgraf, 1922) * Acanthofrontia dicycla Hampson, 1918 * Acanthofrontia frontalis (Strand, 1909) * Acanthofrontia lithosiana Hampson, 1910 \- type species ==References== Category:Lithosiini Category:Moth genera
