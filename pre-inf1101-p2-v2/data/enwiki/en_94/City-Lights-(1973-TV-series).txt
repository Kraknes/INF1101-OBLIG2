City Lights was a Canadian television series hosted by Brian Linehan and produced by Citytv in Toronto, and syndicated throughout Canada and internationally, running from 1973 to 1988. It featured Linehan interviewing film and television celebrities about their roles and lives. Linehan developed a reputation for well-researched questions and non-adversarial style.Celebrity interviewer Brian Linehan dead at 58. CBC News, June 4, 2004. In 1988, the series was replaced with MovieTelevision, an expanded magazine series cohosted by Linehan and Jeanne Beker."Linehan, Beker to team up for new weekly movie show". Toronto Star, July 24, 1988. Linehan remained with the new show for a single season before leaving in 1989."Brian Linehan will no longer light up CITY".
