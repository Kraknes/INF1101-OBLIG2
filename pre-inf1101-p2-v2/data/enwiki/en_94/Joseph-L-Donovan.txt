Joseph L. Donovan (June 16, 1893 - October 28, 1985) was a Minnesota politician, a member of the Democratic-Farmer-Labor Party, and Minnesota's Secretary of State from 1955 to 1971. He also served as a delegate to the Democratic National Convention in 1960 and 1964. Donovan was born in Champion, Michigan, and later moved to Duluth. He was married to Mary Noldin.
