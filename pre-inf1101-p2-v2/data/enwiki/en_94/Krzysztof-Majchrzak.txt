Krzysztof Majchrzak (born 2 March 1948) is a Polish film actor. His first part in a major production was in Andrzej Wajda's The Promised Land (1975). He has appeared in 30 films since 1975. He was nominated for an award as Best Actor for his role in Pornografia at the 2004 Polish Film Awards.
