Barstowite, formula Pb4[Cl6|CO3]*H2O, is a transparent to white mineral in the monoclinic system.Barstowite at Mindat.org mineralogical website It has a Mohs hardness of 3, a white streak and an adamantine lustre. The type locality for Barstowite is Bounds Cliff, St Endellion, Cornwall in the United Kingdom. It is named after Richard W. Barstow (1947-1982), a Cornish mineral collector. ==References== Category:Lead minerals Category:Carbonate minerals Category:Halide minerals Category:Monoclinic minerals
