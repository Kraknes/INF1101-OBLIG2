The is a water bus service in Tokyo. A public company called operates the lines on Tokyo riverside. The services include public lines listed below, as well as event cruises and chartered ships. All lines close on Monday (or the next day if Monday is a holiday), and between December 29 and January 3. ==Lines== Arrows (-) indicate ships only go that direction. Dashes (--) indicate ships go both directions.
