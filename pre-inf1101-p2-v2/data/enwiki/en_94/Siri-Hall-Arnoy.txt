Siri Hall Arnoy. Siri Hall Arnoy (born 28 November 1978 in Oslo) is a former Norwegian politician for the Socialist Left Party. She was elected to the Norwegian Parliament from Akershus in 2001, but was not re-elected in 2005. She then served in the position of deputy representative during the term 2005-2009\. Arnoy is openly lesbian. In 2012 she took the PhD at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology with the thesis The Hopeful Hydrogen.
