Pine Street Art Works is a privately owned art gallery and store, in Burlington, Vermont, showing 20th and 21st century painting and photography, as well 20th century posters, chromolithographs and assorted works on paper. The gallery is located in a former factory, which was built during the mid 20th century, to dress fibers and bristles for brooms and brushes.
