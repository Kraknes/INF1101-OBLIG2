Ligunga is a town in southern Tanzania near the border with Mozambique. == Transport == In October 2007, it was proposed to railway to Ligunga to Mlimba and from Ligunga via Mchuchuma to Mbamba Bay on Lake Malawi. Railway Gazette International November 2007, p688 Railway Gazette: East African rail master plan == Access == Liganga lies in a gap in the mountains that provides better than average access to Lake Malawi.
