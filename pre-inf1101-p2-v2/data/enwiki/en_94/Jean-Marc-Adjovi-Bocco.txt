Jean-Marc Adjovi-Bocco (born 22 December 1963) is a Beninese former professional footballer who played as a defender. Bocco captained the Benin national football team, until he retired from international football in the summer of 1997.In Africa, success is your own boots; Man from the mean streets so content at Easter Rd, Daily Mail, 7 November 1997.
