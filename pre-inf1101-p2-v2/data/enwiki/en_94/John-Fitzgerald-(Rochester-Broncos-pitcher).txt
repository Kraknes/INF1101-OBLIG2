John J. Fitzgerald (1866 - December 20, 1892), also known as Warren B. Fitzgerald, was an American Major League Baseball player who pitched one season in the Majors with the 1890 Rochester Broncos. He pitched in a total of 11 games, started all of them, and completed eight.
