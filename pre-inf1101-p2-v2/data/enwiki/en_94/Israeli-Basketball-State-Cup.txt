The Israeli Basketball State Cup () is the second most important professional basketball competition in Israel, after the Israeli Super League. It is the national federation cup of Israel. The tournament began in the 1955-56 season, and is run by the Israel Basketball Association. The tournament format consists of teams facing each other for a one-game elimination match. The winner advances to the second round, which also consists of one game elimination match.
