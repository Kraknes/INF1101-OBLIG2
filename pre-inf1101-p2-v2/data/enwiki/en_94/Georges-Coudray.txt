Georges Coudray (2 June 1902, in Evran, Cotes d'Armor - 18 January 1998 in Saint Malo) was a French politician, and a deputy to the French National Assembly for the Popular Republican Movement. He was the last mayor of Parame before its merger with the city of Saint Malo.
