Donja Vogosca is a village in Vogosca municipality, near Sarajevo, Federation of Bosnia and Herzegovina, Bosnia and Herzegovina. ==Population== ===Ethnic composition, 1991 census=== * Serbs - 265 (64.32%) * Muslims by nationality - 124 (30.09%) * "Yugoslavs" - 15 (3.64%) * Croats - 5 (1.21%) * Others and unknown - 3 (0.72%) Total: 412 ==History== ==References== * Official results from the book: Ethnic composition of Bosnia-Herzegovina population, by municipalities and settlements, 1991. census, Zavod za statistiku Bosne i Hercegovine - Bilten no.234, Sarajevo 1991.
