Vera Steadman (June 23, 1900 - December 14, 1966) was an American film actress of the silent era. She appeared in 99 films between 1915 and 1941, in her first years appearing as one of the Sennett Bathing Beauties.Location Filming in Los Angeles By Karie Bible, Marc Wanamaker, Harry Medved, p. 61.
