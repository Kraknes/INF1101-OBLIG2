Dabrowa Srodkowa () is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Scinawa, within Lubin County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. It lies approximately west of Scinawa, east of Lubin, and north-west of the regional capital Wroclaw. The village has a population of 70. ==References== Category:Villages in Lubin County
