(also known as Party Room 21) released Family Computer and Super Famicom video games during the 1990s. They also published a game for the Game Boy. Most of the games produced were of the board game/trivia genres. Their most "notable" titles during their years of production were the Famicom game Battle Storm and the Super Famicom game Super Hockey '94. During the 1994 fiscal year, the company published a range of software for the Family Computer before Yonezawa was bought by Sega.
