The Order of Stjepan Radic () is a Croatian national decoration which ranks thirteenth in importance. The order was formed on 1 April 1995. The Order of Stjepan Radic is granted to Croatians and foreigners for services and sacrifices for the national and social rights of the Croatian people. It is named after Stjepan Radic. ==References== ==Sources== * Law of decorations and recognitions of the Republic of Croatia ("Narodne novine", no. 20/95., 57/06. i 141/06.) - articles 6 and 17 * Statute of Order of Stjepan Radic ("Narodne novine", no. 108/00. from November 3, 2000).
