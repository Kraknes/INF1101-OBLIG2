"Let Me In" is a song by Canadian indie rock band Hot Hot Heat, released as the second single from their third studio album, Happiness Ltd.. It was released in the UK and the US as a downloadable single on September 3, 2007. The song was featured on the soundtrack for Saints Row 2.
