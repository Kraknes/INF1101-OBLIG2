The Fidesco Group is one of the first and largest retail supermarket chains in Moldova. Fidesco Group was founded in 1992. Its main activity is import and retail/wholesale of food products. The legal form of ownership is a limited company, 100% owned by private capital. Currently, the Fidesco group includes 35 stores in Chisinau, Balti, Ialoveni, Orhei, Calarasi, Causeni, Comrat, Ceadir-Lunga, Tvardita and Anenii Noi.
