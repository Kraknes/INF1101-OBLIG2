Age Konradsen (born 28 September 1954 in Lenvik) is a Norwegian politician for the Conservative Party. He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament from Troms in 2001, but was not re-elected in 2005. He had previously served as a deputy representative during the term 1997-2001\. Konradsen was a member of Lenvik municipality council from 1991 to 2001, serving as mayor since 1995. He became leader of the county party chapter in 1993.
