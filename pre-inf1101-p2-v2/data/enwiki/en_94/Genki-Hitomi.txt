(born Noriaki Hitomi, October 1, 1957 in Tokyo, Japan) is a Japanese former singer and teacher is best known as the lead vocalist of Japanese heavy metal band Vow Wow in the 1980s. ==Biography== Hitomi started his career as a singer when he was a student of TUFS. Then he formed Noiz with the members of Carmen Maki & OZ. Noiz released their first album in 1983, but they soon broke up. In 1984, Hitomi joined Bow Wow, and the band was renamed to Vow Wow. Vow Wow released six studio albums and broke up in 1990.
