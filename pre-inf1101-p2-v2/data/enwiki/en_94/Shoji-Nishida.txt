Shoji Nishida in front of the Japanese National Diet Building, Tokyo in 2016 is a conservative Japanese politician of the Liberal Democratic Party, a member of the House of Councillors in the Diet (national legislature). A graduate of Shiga University, he was elected to the House of Councillors for the first time in 2007 after serving in the assembly of Kyoto Prefecture since 2003. In early 2011, he was involved in a political scandal, revealing information that forced Seiji Maehara to resign.A foreigner in her own home: Shoddy treatment of its Korean residents once again deals Japan a black eye.
