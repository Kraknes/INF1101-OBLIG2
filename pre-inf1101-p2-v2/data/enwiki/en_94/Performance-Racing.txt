Performance Racing is a racing team based in Great Britain, involved in many areas of motorsport. The team has been managed since 1999 by Bobby Issazadhe. == History == The Performance Racing business was founded in Sweden, in 1999, to concentrate various motorsport activities into one source. The business was bought by Issazadhe in 2000 to manage his own racing team. In the beginning, in 1999 and 2000, the team ran in Europa Cup Formula Opel. It also became involved in Formula Three in Germany, Sweden and Great Britain. Since 2006-07, Performance Racing manage the A1 Team Pakistan in the A1 Grand Prix World Cup of Motorsport.A1 Team Pakistan informations a1podium.com In January 2008, the team became the managers of A1 Team Indonesia.
