Wadroze () is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Rudna, within Lubin County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Prior to 1945 it was in Germany. It lies approximately north-east of Lubin, and north- west of the regional capital Wroclaw. The village has a population of 140. ==References== Category:Villages in Lubin County
