The 1999 Vuelta a Espana was the 54th Edition of Vuelta a Espana, taking place from 4 September starting in Murcia and finishing in Madrid on 26 September 1999. It consisted of 21 stages over , ridden at an average speed of . The favourites were Laurent Jalabert, Alex Zulle, Jan Ullrich and defending champion Abraham Olano.
