Timothy W. Peper (born October 4, 1980, Nyack, New York) is an American film, television and stage actor. ==Career== Peper is a graduate of New York University's Tisch School of the Arts. He has performed in numerous theatrical productions such as Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat, Othello, Lobby Hero, Waiting for Godot, and Fool for Love. His television credits include a recurring role on Conviction. He has guest-starred on Law & Order, The Good Wife and As the World Turns. Prior to his casting on Carpoolers, he had a recurring role in Guiding Light playing Jimmy Donovan.
