Ian Bremner (born 11 April 1947) is a former Australian rules footballer who represented and in the Victorian Football League (VFL) during the 1960s and 1970s. A half back flanker recruited from Pearcedale, Bremner started his career at Collingwood in 1966 but after playing just one game in the season he crossed to Hawthorn. He was a premiership player in 1971, losing grand finalist in 1975 and a premiership player again in 1976.
