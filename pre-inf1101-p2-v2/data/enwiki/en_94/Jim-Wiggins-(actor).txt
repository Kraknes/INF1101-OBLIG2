Jim Wiggins (13 March 1922 in Birkenhead - 1999, London) was an English actor, best known for his role as Paul Collins in the long-running television soap Brookside. An amateur actor for many years, he finally turned professional in 1978, having had careers in the Army, civil service and as a teacher. As well as his role in Brookside (from 1982 to 1990), he appeared in a number of other television series.
