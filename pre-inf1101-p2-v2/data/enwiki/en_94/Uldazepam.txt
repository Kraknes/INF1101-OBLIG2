Uldazepam is a drug which is a benzodiazepine derivative. It has sedative and anxiolytic effects similar to those of other benzodiazepines. ==Synthesis== Thio thionamide is even more prone to amidine formation than the lactam itself. Uldazepam synthesis: J. B. Hester, Jr., (1970); Chem. Abstr., 73: 99,001t (1970). Reaction of thionamide (2) with O-allyl-hydroxylamine gave the oximino (3) uldazepam. ==See also== *Benzodiazepine ==References== Category:Allyl compounds Category:Benzodiazepines Category:Chloroarenes Category:GABAA receptor positive allosteric modulators
