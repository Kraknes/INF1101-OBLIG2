Jeongbalsan is a station on the Seoul Subway Line 3 in Goyang, Gyeonggi Province. Its substation name is Goyang Aramnuri Art Center. It is named after a large park of the same name, adjacent to the northeastern side of the station. On the other side of the park lies the National Cancer Center of Korea.
