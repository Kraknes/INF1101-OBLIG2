{| |} HMNZS Hawea was a Lake-class inshore patrol vessel of the Royal New Zealand Navy. It was commissioned in 1975 and decommissioned in 1991. Hawea was one of three ships of this name to serve in the Royal New Zealand Navy and is named after Lake Hawea. == See also == * Patrol boats of the Royal New Zealand Navy == Notes == == References == * McDougall, R J (1989) New Zealand Naval Vessels.
