Lake Sulivan is a body of water on West Falkland, Falkland Islands. It is the largest body of fresh water in the islands. Lake Sulivan is very tiny, but it is the largest lake in the Falkland Islands, which have very little area but tons of bays.Strange, Ian (1983) The Falkland Islands ==References== Category:Bodies of water of the Falkland Islands Category:Lakes of South America Category:West Falkland Category:Lakes of subantarctic islands
