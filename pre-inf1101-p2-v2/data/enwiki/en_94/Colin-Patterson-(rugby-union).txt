Colin Patterson (born 3 March 1955)Colin Patterson player profile Scrum.com is a former international rugby union player. He toured South Africa in 1980 with the British and Irish LionsColin Patterson Lions profile lionsrugby.com and at the time played club rugby for Instonians. His son Johnny captained the Regent House Grammar School side that reached the Ulster Schools Cup final in 2008. Patterson studied law at Bristol and played for English Universities and British Universities. He rejoined Instonians on his return to Ireland and was selected for Ulster and capped by Ireland B in 1977 before winning his first full cap for Ireland against New Zealand in 1978. He won eleven senior caps and scored five tries for Ireland.
