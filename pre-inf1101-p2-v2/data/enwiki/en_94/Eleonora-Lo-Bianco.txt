Eleonora Lo Bianco (born 22 December 1979) is an Italian volleyball player. ==Career== Born in Borgomanero, in her youth she played in the Omegna volleyball team. In the season 1998/1999 she was recruited to the Club Italia, a special team organized by the Italian volleyball Federation to train young players. After that experience she played for several teams in the Italian woman Volleyball League. She is 172 cm tall and weighs 70 kg. Lo Bianco played with her national team at the 2014 World Championship.
