Thanh Nhan Hospital (Benh vien Thanh Nhan in Vietnamese) is a large hospital in Thanh Nhan ward, Hai Ba Trung district, Hanoi, Vietnam. Located in a labour quarter, it is regarded as a hospital for workers and the poor in Hanoi.
