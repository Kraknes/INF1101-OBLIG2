Ernest Joseph Field "Ernie" Loveless (6 December 1907 - 21 Dec 1950) was an Australian rules footballer who played for St Kilda and Hawthorn in the VFL. Loveless played in the back pocket and started his career with St Kilda. He crossed to Hawthorn in 1933 and was a best and fairest winner the following season.
