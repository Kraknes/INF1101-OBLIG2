Edward Jozef Szymkowiak (February 13, 1932 - January 28, 1990) was a notable Polish footballer who played as a goalkeeper. ==Club career== He played for such a clubs like Ruch Chorzow and Legia Warszawa, but most of his career he spent in Polonia Bytom. He is one of the legend of that club. The stadium of Polonia Bytom is called by his name. ==International career== Szymkowiak participated in the Olympic Games in Helsinki and Rome.
