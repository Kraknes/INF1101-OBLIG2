Anne Berit Andersen (born 2 December 1951 in Sogne) is a Norwegian politician for the Conservative Party. She was elected to the Norwegian Parliament from Vest-Agder in 2001, but was not re-elected in 2005. She served in the position of deputy representative during the terms 1997-2001 and 2005-2009\.
