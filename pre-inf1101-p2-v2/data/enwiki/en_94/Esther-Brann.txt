Esther Brann (July 21, 1899 - January 9, 1998) was an American children's author and illustrator. == Education == Brann was born in New York City. She attended the Art Students League of New York, Cooper Union, the National Academy of Design, and the Fontainebleau School of Fine Arts in France. == Career == After finishing her education in France, Brann returned to New York looking for work as an illustrator. When she could not find work, she decided to work for herself and began writing and illustrating her own books. Brann was named one of ten national award winners for her children's books. ==Personal Life== Brann traveled around the world with her husband and son when her husband was enlisted in the Navy. They lived in Guam, Florida, Virginia and California.
