Raymond Robertsen Raymond Robertsen (born 12 September 1974 in Hammerfest) is a Norwegian politician for the Conservative Party. He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament from Finnmark in 2001, but was not re-elected in 2005. Robertsen was a member of the executive committee of Hammerfest municipality council from 1995 to 2001.
