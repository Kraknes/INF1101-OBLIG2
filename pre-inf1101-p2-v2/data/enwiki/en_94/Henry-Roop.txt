{| |}The Henry Roop was a two-masted wooden schooner built in 1835 in Black Rock, New York, USA.outside.in * Black Rock, Buffalo NY It was also seen as "H. Roop." The ship measured 65 by 19 by 7 feet and weighed 80 tons. Her last cargo was salt. She was lost in a storm on October 12, 1843, bound for Sandusky, Ohio, USA.
