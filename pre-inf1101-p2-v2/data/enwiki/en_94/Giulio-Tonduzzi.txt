Giulio Tonduzzi (c.1513 - c.1583) was an Italian painter of the Renaissance period.Art in Northern Italy, by Corrado Ricci; editor: New York: Charles Scribner's Sons (1911); page 322. He was born in Faenza, which flourished in the first half of the 16th century. He was a pupil of Giulio Romano. He painted a Stoning of S. Stephen for the church in Faenza.
