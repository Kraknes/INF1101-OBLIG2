Osiek () is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Lubin, within Lubin County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. It was the birthplace of the Protestant Reformer Kaspar Schwenkfeld von Ossig in 1490. It lies approximately south-east of Lubin and north-west of the regional capital Wroclaw. The village has an approximate population of 1,000. ==Notable people== *Caspar Schwenckfeld (1489/90-1561), German theologian, writer, and preacher ==References== Osiek
