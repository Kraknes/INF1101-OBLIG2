Johan Mikael Eklund (born 14 September 1981), known as Mikael Eklund, is a Swedish footballer who plays for IK Brage as a defender. ==Career== Eklund started his career in Forssa BK of Borlange. Eklund got his breakthrough when playing for IK Brage in Superettan, and he signed for Kalmar FF in 2003.
