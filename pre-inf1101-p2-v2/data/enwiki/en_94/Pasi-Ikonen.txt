Pasi Ikonen (born June 30, 1980 in Vihanti) is a Finnish orienteering competitor, winner of the 2001 World Orienteering Championships, Short distance,World Orienteering Championship, senior statistics 1966-2006 (Retrieved on December 14, 2007) and also silver on the Sprint distance same year. In France 2011, he won silver on the long distance.
