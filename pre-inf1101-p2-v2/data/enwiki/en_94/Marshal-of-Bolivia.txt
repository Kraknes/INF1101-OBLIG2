In Bolivia, the title Marshal is a military distinction, not a military rank and it is granted to generals for exceptional achievements and it is used to denote a senior military leader. == List of incumbents == There were only four in the history of Bolivia: *Andres de Santa Cruz, Marshal of Zepita. *Bernardino Bilbao Rioja, Marshal of Kilometer 7. *Jose Ballivian, Marshal of Ingavi. *Otto Philipp Braun, Marshal of Montenegro.
