Imavere Parish () was a rural municipality of Estonia, in Jarva County. It had a population of 1,025 (2006) and an area of 139 km2. ==Villages== , Imavere Parish had 13 villages: Eistvere, Hermani, Imavere, Jalametsa, Jaravere, Kiigevere, Kasukonna, Laimetsa, Puiatu, Pallastvere, Taadikvere, Tammekula and Vorevere. ==External links== *
