Hans Gjeisar Kjaestad (born 2 January 1947 in Porsgrunn) is a Norwegian politician for the Conservative Party. He served as a deputy representative in the Norwegian Parliament from Oslo during the terms 1997-2001, 2001-2005 and 2005-2009\. During the entire middle term he sat as a regular representative, replacing Kristin Krohn Devold who was appointed to the second cabinet Bondevik.
