Zlatko Nastevski (Macedonian: Zlatko Hactevcki ; born 4 August 1957 in Skopje) is a Macedonia retired football player. He played as an attacking midfielder. ==Later years== In October 2009, he played in the Australian 'Olderoos' squad in the World Masters Games.
