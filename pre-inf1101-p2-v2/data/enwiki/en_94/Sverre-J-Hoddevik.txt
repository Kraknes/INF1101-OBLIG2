Sverre J. Hoddevik (born 10 June 1946, in Selje) is a Norwegian politician for the Conservative Party. He was elected to the Norwegian Parliament from Sogn og Fjordane in 1997, and was re-elected once, in 2001. (The Norwegian Parliament serves in four-year, fixed, terms.)
