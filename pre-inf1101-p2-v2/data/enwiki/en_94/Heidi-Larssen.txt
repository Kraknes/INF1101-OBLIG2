Heidi Larssen (born 8 November 1951 in Oslo) is a Norwegian politician for the Conservative Party. She was elected to the Norwegian Parliament from Oslo in 2001, but was not re-elected in 2005. She served in the position of deputy representative during the term 2005-2009\. Larssen held various positions in Oslo city council from 1997 to 2001.
