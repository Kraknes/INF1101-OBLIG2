Carruth Plaza is a Western sculpture garden in Houston, Texas. It is part of NRG Park. Completed in 2003, the plaza was named after Allen H. "Buddy" Carruth, former chairman of the Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo. ==External links== * Hermes Architects page on Carruth Plaza Category:Sculpture gardens, trails and parks in the United States Category:Parks in Houston Category:Culture of Houston
