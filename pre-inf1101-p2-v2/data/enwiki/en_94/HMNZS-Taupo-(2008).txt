{| |} HMNZS Taupo is a Lake-class inshore patrol vessel of the Royal New Zealand Navy. Taupo was delivered to the Ministry of Defence on the 28 May 2009 and commissioned into the Royal New Zealand Navy on 29 May 2009. Taupo is the third ship of this name to serve in the Royal New Zealand Navy and is named after Lake Taupo. ==See also== * Patrol boats of the Royal New Zealand Navy == External links == *Taupo (official website, Royal New Zealand Navy) Category:Protector-class inshore patrol vessels Category:2008 ships Category:Active patrol vessels of New Zealand
