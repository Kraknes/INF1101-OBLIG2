Association of Employees in Government Educational Administration (, ) is the smallest of the member trade unions of Confederation of Unions for Academic Professionals in Finland with 141 members.Akavan jasenjarjestot ja aanimaarat. Retrieved 11-15-2007. The union consists of civil servants working in the educational sector of the provincial boards.
