Actinoleuca campbelli is a species of sea snail or true limpet, a marine gastropod mollusc in the family Lottiidae, one family of true limpets. ==Subspecies== Subspecies within this species include: * Actinoleuca campbelli bountyensis * Actinoleuca campbelli macquariensis ==References== * Powell A.
