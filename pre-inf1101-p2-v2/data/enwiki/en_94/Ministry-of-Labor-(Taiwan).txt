The Ministry of Labor (MOL; ) is a ministry of the Republic of China (Taiwan) representing opinions of employees, political and academic circles to review labor policies, laws and regulations, as well as related projects and programs in Taiwan. MOL is working with various international organizations and bilateral exchanges for elevating the welfare of labors in ROC,https://english.mol.gov.tw/6386/6438/6530/ such as the Labor Insurance program.http://www.bli.gov.tw/en/ ==History== The Ministry was originally established as the Council of Labor Affairs (CLA; ) on 1 August 1987.
