The JZ series 611 is a historic vehicle of Yugoslav Railways. It was a diesel- electric multiple unit made from aluminium alloy. It was nicknamed the "Aluminium train". A total of 3 units were made. The first examples had a weak engine, but later models had a stronger one. They performed very well on level lines, but on mountain railways they suffered some damage. ==History== * Service start: 1965/09/28 * Last year of service: 1974 ==External links== *611 at railfaneurope.net Category:Multiple units of Yugoslavia Category:Train- related introductions in 1965
