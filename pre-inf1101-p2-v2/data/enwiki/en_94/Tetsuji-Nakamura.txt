Tetsuji Nakamura is a Japanese politician of the Democratic Party of Japan, a member of the House of Councillors in the Diet (national legislature). == Overview == A native of Ikoma, Nara and graduate of Kyoto University, he was elected to the House of Councillors for the first time in 2007 after running unsuccessfully for the House of Representatives in 2005. == External links == * in Japanese.
