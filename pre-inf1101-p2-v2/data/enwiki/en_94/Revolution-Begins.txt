Revolution Begins is the third EP released by Swedish melodic death metal band Arch Enemy. It was released on 31 August 2007, through Century Media Records. A music video was made for the self-titled song, "Revolution Begins", containing cyber-revolution scenes. The songs, "Revolution Begins" and "Blood on Your Hands" are featured on the album Rise of the Tyrant.
