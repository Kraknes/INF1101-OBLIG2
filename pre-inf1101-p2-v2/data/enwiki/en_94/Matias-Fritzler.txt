Matias Lionel Fritzler (born 23 August 1986) is an Argentine football midfielder. He currently plays for Club Atletico Colon. ==Career== Fritzler is of German descent. He started his professional in 2004 with Lanus. He went on to establish himself as an important member of the first team and, in 2007, was part of the squad that won the 2007 Apertura tournament, Lanus' first ever top flight league title. During the 2010-11 La Liga season, he played on loan for Hercules CF.
