Alessandro Casolani (1552-1606) was an Italian painter of the late-Renaissance period, active mainly in Siena. ==Biography== He was also called Alessandro della Torre. He was born at Siena, and was the pupil of Ventura Salimbeni and of Cristoforo Roncalli. His works are principally in the churches of Siena, but are also found in Naples and Genoa. He also etched one plate, a Madonna. His son, Ilario Casolani was also a painter.
