Omwony Ojwok (1 June 1947 - 11 November 2007) was a Ugandan politician. He served as director of the Ugandan AIDS Commission (1994-1999), Minister for Northern Rehabilitation (1999-2001) and State Minister for Economic Monitoring (2001-2007).
