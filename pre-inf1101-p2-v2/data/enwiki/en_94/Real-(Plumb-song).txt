"Real" is the lead single from Plumb's studio album Beautiful Lumps of Coal. It reached the No. 41 position in the UK. The single was released digitally and physically. ==Track listing== #"Real" - 3:42 #"Nice, Naive & Beautiful" - 4:15 ==Music video== The music video for "Real" shows Tiffany inside of a closet with a sleek dress and gloves on. It is in black & white. ==Chart performance== The song peaked at No. 30 on Top 40 Tracks.
