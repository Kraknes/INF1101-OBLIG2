La Ciudad de los Arboles (The City of the Trees) is the eighth studio album by Spanish folk metal group Mago de Oz, it was released on 6 November 2007. It comes in Digibook format and includes a DVD. The first single of the album is "Y Ahora Voy a Salir (Ranxeira)", a tribute to Mexico in ranchera style.
