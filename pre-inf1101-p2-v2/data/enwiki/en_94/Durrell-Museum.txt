Durrell Museum is a museum located in the town of Durrell, Newfoundland and Labrador, Canada. It features several historical exhibits. The museum is located on Museum Road, off Durrell Street (or Howlett's Lane from shore) in Durrell. ==Affiliations== The Museum is affiliated with: CMA, CHIN, and Virtual Museum of Canada. == See also == *Durrell, Newfoundland and Labrador *Twillingate, Newfoundland and Labrador ==References== == External links == *Information on the museum Category:History museums in Canada Category:Museums in Newfoundland and Labrador
