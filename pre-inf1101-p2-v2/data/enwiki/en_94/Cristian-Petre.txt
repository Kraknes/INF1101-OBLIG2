Cristian Constantin Petre (born 22 March 1979 in Oradea)Cristian Petre player profile Scrum.com is a Romanian former rugby union player and current coach. He had 92 caps for the Romania national rugby union team, with 6 tries scored, 30 points in aggregate. Petre played in all the four games for his country at the 2007 Rugby World Cup finals.
