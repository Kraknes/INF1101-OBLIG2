CP Morgan was a family-owned homebuilding company, based in the United States. Founded in 1983, its headquarters were in Indianapolis, Indiana, with additional offices in Charlotte, North Carolina and Kernersville, North Carolina. CP Morgan offered homes that ranged from 1,000 to , with starting prices of $95,000 - $260,000. The company focused primarily on single-family homes for first time and first time move-up homebuyers.
