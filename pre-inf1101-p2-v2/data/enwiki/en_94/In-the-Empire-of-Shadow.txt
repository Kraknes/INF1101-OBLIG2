Del Rey Books) Cover artist: Peter Peebles In the Empire of Shadow (1995) is the second fantasy novel in The Worlds of Shadow trilogy by Lawrence Watt-Evans. ==Plot introduction== A group from this world is trapped in a science fiction universe.
