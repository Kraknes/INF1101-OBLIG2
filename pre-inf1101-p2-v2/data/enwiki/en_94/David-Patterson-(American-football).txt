David Bernard Patterson Jr (born February 11, 1985) is a former American and Canadian football defensive tackle. He was signed by the Falcons in 2007. He played college football at The Ohio State University. He played his high school football at Warrensville Heights High School in Warrensville Heights, Ohio, under head coach Dan Thorpe. Patterson married the model Anansa Sims and the two appeared together on the reality television series Beverly's Full House on the Oprah Winfrey Network.
