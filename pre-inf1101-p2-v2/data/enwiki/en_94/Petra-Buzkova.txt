Petra Buzkova (born 7 December 1965) was a former Minister of Education, Youth and Sports in the Czech cabinet. She belongs to the Czech Social Democratic Party. In 1992-2006 she was a member of the Chamber of Deputies. Buzkova obtained her Doctor of Laws degree from the Charles University in 1989.
