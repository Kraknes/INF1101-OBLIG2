The was one of the ministries in the Japanese government. Ministry was resulted as merger of Ministry of Postal Services (You Zheng Sheng ) and Ministry of Telecommunications (Dian Qi Tong Xin Sheng ) on 1 August 1952, which were superseding from 1 April 1946. Head Office, Chiyoda, Tokyo The ministry introduced the POSIVA system for giving aid to foreign countries in January 1991. In January 2001, the ministry was merged with other ministries to form the Ministry of Internal Affairs and Communications.
