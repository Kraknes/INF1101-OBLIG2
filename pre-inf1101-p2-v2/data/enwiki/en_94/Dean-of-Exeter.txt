Exeter Cathedral, the maintenance of the structure and decoration of which is traditionally one of the principal duties of the deanSee e.g. internal fittings added and restorations performed by Dean Jeremiah Milles (1762-1784) and his predecessor The Dean of Exeter is the head of the Chapter of Cathedral Church of Saint Peter in Exeter, England.Details up to 1950 are from: The chapter was established by William Briwere, Bishop of Exeter (1224-44) who set up the offices of dean and chancellor of Exeter Cathedral, allowing the chapter to elect those officers. The deanery is at 10 The Close, Exeter.
