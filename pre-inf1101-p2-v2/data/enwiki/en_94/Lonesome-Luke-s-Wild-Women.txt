Lonesome Luke's Wild Women is a 1917 American short comedy film starring Harold Lloyd. A print of the film exists in a collection. ==Cast== * Harold Lloyd - Lonesome Luke * Bebe Daniels * Snub Pollard * Bud Jamison * Sammy Brooks * W.L. Adams * David Voorhees * Charles Stevenson - (as Charles E. Stevenson) * Billy Fay * Sandy Roth * Margaret Joslin - (as Margaret Joslin Todd) * Fred C. Newmeyer * Gilbert Pratt * Max Hamburger * Gus Leonard * Marie Mosquini * Dorothea Wolbert ==Production== Lonesome Luke's Wild Women was filmed on Deadman's Island off San Pedro, Los Angeles, California, which was later removed in 1928 as part of a harbor development effort. ==Reception== Like many American films of the time, Lonesome Luke's Wild Women was subject to cuts by city and state film censorship boards.
