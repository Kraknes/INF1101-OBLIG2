Viktor Mucha (17 April 1877 in Koniggratz - 6 June 1933 in Vienna) was a dermatologist from Austria. He was involved in early syphilis research. He studied medicine at the universities of Vienna and Strasbourg, receiving his doctorate in 1904. From 1905 he worked as an assistant under Ernst Finger in the department of skin and venereal diseases at Vienna. He was also a physician at the Kaiserin-Elisabethspital (1909-13) and the St. Anna- Kinderspital (1913/14).
