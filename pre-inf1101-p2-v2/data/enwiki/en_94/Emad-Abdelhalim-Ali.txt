Emad Abdelhalim Ali is an amateur boxer from Egypt. ==Career== Ali is best known for having won the gold medal in the men's super heavyweight division at the 2007 All-Africa Games. In 2006, he still competed at 201 lbs and lost in the first round of the Ahmed Oner Cup to Ali Mazaheri by RSCO.
