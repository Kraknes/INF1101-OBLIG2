Michael David Stonebreaker (born January 14, 1967 in Baltimore, Maryland) is a former professional American football player who played linebacker for two seasons for the Chicago Bears and New Orleans Saints. Played high school football at John Curtis Christian High School in River Ridge, Louisiana and graduated in 1986. Finished third in balloting for the Butkus Award in 1988 and 1990 during his career at Notre Dame (1986-1990).
