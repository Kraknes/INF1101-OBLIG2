Seokgye Station is a station on Seoul Subway Line 1 and Line 6. The name of this station does not refer to any one neighborhood in this area; its name comes from a portmanteau of Seokgwan-dong and Wolgye-dong, located in Seongbuk-gu and Nowon-gu, respectively. == References == Category:Seoul Metropolitan Subway stations Category:Metro stations in Seongbuk District Category:Metro stations in Nowon District Category:Railway stations in South Korea opened in 1985
