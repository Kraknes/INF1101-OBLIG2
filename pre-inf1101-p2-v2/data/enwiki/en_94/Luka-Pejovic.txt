Luka Pejovic (Cyrillic: Luka Pejovitsh; born 31 July 1985Soccerway profile in Titograd) is a Montenegrin international footballer. ==Career== ===Club=== After starting his senior career at FK Crvena Stijena, Pejovic played for FK Grbalj Radanovici for one year then was bought by FK Mogren Budva in 2007. He won Best Montenegrin Footballer in the league award for the year 2007, alongside ex team mate Aleksandar Nedovic. He was also part of Mogren team that won the National Championship in the 2008-09 season. In January 2011, he joined Polish club Jagiellonia Bialystok on four and a half year contract.Pejovic w Jagielloni juz od srody! 17.01.2011, ekstraklasa.net In January 2017 he made a deal with Montenegrin club Iskra Danilovgrad and he was a third player to come in this club (after Miroje Jovanovic and Semir Agovic). 03.01.2017, m.cdm.me ===International=== Luka Pejovic made his debut for Montenegro against Japan on 1 June 2007 as Montenegro lost 2-0. He has earned a total of 23 caps, scoring 1 goal.
