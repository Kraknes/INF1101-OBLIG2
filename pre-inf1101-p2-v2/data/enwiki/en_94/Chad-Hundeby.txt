Chad Hundeby is an American long distance swimmer. As a child, he was a member of the Blue Buoy Swim School' based in Southern California.National Swim Schools articleSwim for Life Foundation's 4th annual ocean swim In 1991, he won a gold medal in the men's 25 kilometre open water swimming event at the 1991 World Aquatics Championships. In 1994, he set the (then) world record for the fastest swim of the English Channel in a time of 7 h 17 mins.Dover Life Channel Swimming - Successful Crossings The previous record had stood since 1978 when Penny Lee Dean completed the swim in 7 h 40 mins.
