Queenwood is a suburb in northern Hamilton in New Zealand. This place is separated by Chartwell Park from Chartwell. It is mostly a residential area, with the typical range of local shops. It is located between River Road and Hukanui Road. ==Demographics== The statistical area of Queenwood (Hamilton City), which also includes Harrowfield, had a population of 2,382 at the 2018 New Zealand census, an increase of 117 people (5.2%) since the 2013 census, and an increase of 144 people (6.4%) since the 2006 census. There were 864 households. There were 1,149 males and 1,233 females, giving a sex ratio of 0.93 males per female. The median age was 41.1 years, with 456 people (19.1%) aged under 15 years, 450 (18.9%) aged 15 to 29, 1,062 (44.6%) aged 30 to 64, and 414 (17.4%) aged 65 or older. Ethnicities were 82.2% European/Pakeha, 12.8% Maori, 3.0% Pacific peoples, 12.3% Asian, and 2.0% other ethnicities (totals add to more than 100% since people could identify with multiple ethnicities). The proportion of people born overseas was 22.7%, compared with 27.1% nationally. Although some people objected to giving their religion, 50.5% had no religion, 39.0% were Christian, 1.4% were Hindu, 0.6% were Muslim, 1.0% were Buddhist and 2.0% had other religions. Of those at least 15 years old, 612 (31.8%) people had a bachelor or higher degree, and 207 (10.7%) people had no formal qualifications. The median income was $38,100.
