WCSS (1490 AM) is a radio station broadcasting an oldies/classic hits-centered full service format. Licensed to Amsterdam, New York, United States, the station serves the Capital Region, with studios in the Riverfront Center mall in that city's downtown. The station is currently owned by Cranesville Block Co., Inc. and features programming from Westwood One's Good Time Oldies network.
