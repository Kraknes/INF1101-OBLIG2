Over the Fence is a 1917 American short comedy film directed by and starring Harold Lloyd. The film is notable as the debut of the "Glasses" or "Boy" character. Prints of the film survive at the film archive of the Museum of Modern Art. ==Cast== * Harold Lloyd as Ginger, a tailor * Snub Pollard as Snitch, another * Bud Jamison as The Boss * Bebe Daniels as Ginger's girl * J.
