Masayuki Naoshima with the United States Ambassador to Japan John Roos October 21, 2009 is a Japanese politician of the Democratic Party of Japan, a member of the House of Councillors in the Diet (national legislature). A native of Ikeda, Osaka and graduate of Kobe University, he was elected for the first time in 1992.
