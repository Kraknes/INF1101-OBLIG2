Albert Smith (January 30, 1929 - June 24, 1985) was an American racing driver from Dayton, Ohio who competed in the USAC Championship Car series. While he drove in numerous races on pavement, he only attempted the Indianapolis 500 once in 1967 and failed to make the field. He made 14 Champ Car starts from 1966 to 1969.
