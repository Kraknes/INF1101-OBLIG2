Torture Central: E-mails From Abu Ghraib is the title of the memoir of Michael Keller, a soldier stationed in Abu Ghraib, Iraq during 2005/2006. It was published on October 29, 2007, and chronicles many events previously unreported in the news media, including torture that continued at Abu Ghraib over a year after the abuse photos were published. == External links == * Official Book Website * Tales of pain at Abu Ghraib, book review by Thomas Lake, St. Petersburg Times, 11 January 2008.
