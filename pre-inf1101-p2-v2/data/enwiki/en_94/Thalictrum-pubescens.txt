Thalictrum pubescens, the king of the meadow or tall meadow-rue, is a plant in the buttercup family, Ranunculaceae. ==Description== Thalictrum pubescens is a herbaceous plant with alternate, pinnately compound leaves, on hollow, green stems. The flowers are white, borne in spring and summer. == Distribution == The range of this plant includes most of eastern Canada and United States excluding Florida. ==References== pubescens Category:Plants described in 1813 Category:Flora of Eastern Canada Category:Flora of the Northeastern United States Category:Flora of the Southeastern United States Category:Flora of the North-Central United States
