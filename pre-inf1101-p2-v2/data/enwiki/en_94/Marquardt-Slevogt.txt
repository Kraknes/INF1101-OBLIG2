Friedrich Marquardt Slevogt (22 March 1909 in Karlsruhe - 25 May 1980) was a German ice hockey player who competed in the 1928 Winter Olympics and 1932 Winter Olympics. In 1928 he was a member of the German ice hockey team, which placed last in his preliminary group of the Olympic tournament and did not advance. Four years later he was a member of the German ice hockey team, which won the bronze medal.
