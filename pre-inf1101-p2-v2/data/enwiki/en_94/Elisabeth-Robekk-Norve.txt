Elisabeth Robekk Norve in 2017 Elisabeth Robekk Norve (born 29 March 1951 in Borgund) is a Norwegian politician for the Conservative Party. She was elected to the Norwegian Parliament from More og Romsdal in 2001, but she was not re- elected in 2005. She served in the position of deputy representative during the terms 1997-2001 and 2005-2009, when she was elected to the Stortinget once more. Norve held various positions in Alesund city council from 1995 to 2003.
