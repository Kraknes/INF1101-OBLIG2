KRUX (91.5 FM) is one of New Mexico State University's two radio stations, located in Las Cruces, New Mexico, United States. Student-run and operated, it serves southern New Mexico an eclectic mix of musical genres and more live performances than any other station in the region. Programming features over 20 different styles of music and news including live performances and interviews. KRUX was the first non-commercial educational FM station to go on the air in Las Cruces.
