The Hasdate (also: Hajdate, ) is a small river in the Apuseni Mountains, Cluj County, western Romania. It is a left tributary of the river Aries. It flows through the municipalities Savadisla, Ciurila and Petrestii de Jos, and joins the Aries at Cornesti, near Turda. Its length is and its basin size is .
