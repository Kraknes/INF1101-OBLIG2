Raffaelle Castellini (1791-1874)https://www.consiglio.marche.it/informazione_e_comunicazione/pubblicazioni/quaderni/pdf/128.pdf, p. 110 was an Italian artist, who was the director of the Mosaic School at the Vatican, and executed the splendid mosaics of The Sibyl of Cumae after Domenichino and St. John the Baptist after Guercino for the Basilica of St. Peter's.
