Doksan Station is a station on Line 1 of the Seoul Subway. It is an above- ground station located in southwestern Seoul, with service between Uijeongbu and Suwon/Cheonan. It is a relatively new addition to this section of Line 1, having been opened 1998 in response to growing demand from the adjacent Doksan and Gasan neighborhoods, as well as the Ha'an area in the nearby suburban city of Gwangmyeong.
