Carl Friedrich August Alexander Cruger (26 February 1813, Furstenberg - 21 May 1885, Hamburg ) was a German entomologist who specialised in Lepidoptera. His collection of world Lepidoptera is in the Biozentrum Grindel und Zoologisches Museum . ==Works== *1876 Ueber Schmetterlinge von Guayaquil.
