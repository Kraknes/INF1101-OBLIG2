Lubos Pecka (born 19 February 1978 in Prachatice) is a retired Czech footballer. He was a striker or attacking midfielder. == Biography == Pecka first began his youth career with FK Laziste and Tatran Prachatice. His first team came with a brief spell at SK Ceske Budejovice (2000-2001) before returning to Tatran Prachatice (2001-2004). He then move to Czech top flight club FK Mlada Boleslav. In his first season, he scored three times in 16 games (2004-05). His next season was big improvement because he scored nine in 25 games (2005-06). The next season, he was top scorer of the Gambrinus liga with 16 in 29 (2006-07). This earned him a loan move to Alemannia Aachen.
