SpellCaster is a Sega Master System video game that stars a character named Kane. This game was known in Japan as and in South America as Warrior Quest. It had a sequel, Mystic Defender. ==Plot== The game starts when Kane is summoned by Daikak, the great leader of the Summit Temple, to stop a war between factions of warlords. The player explores temples and defeats enemies like ghosts, ninja and feral beasts. There are also villages to explore and people to talk to as the player assembles clues on who desecrated his home town's temple and killed its guardians.
