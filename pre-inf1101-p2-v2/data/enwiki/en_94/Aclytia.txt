Aclytia is a genus of tiger moths in the family Erebidae. The genus was erected by Jacob Hubner in 1819. ==Species== * Aclytia albistriga Schaus, 1910 * Aclytia apicalis Walker, 1854 * Aclytia bractea Moschler, 1877 * Aclytia coerulonitans Rothschild, 1912 * Aclytia conjecturalis Draudt, 1930 * Aclytia flavicaput Rothschild, 1912 * Aclytia flavigutta Walker, 1854 * Aclytia gynamorpha Hampson, 1898 * Aclytia heber Cramer, 1872 * Aclytia hoffmannsi Rothschild, 1912 * Aclytia jonesi Rothschild, 1912 * Aclytia klagesi Rothschild, 1912 * Aclytia leucaspila Fleming, 1959 * Aclytia mariamne Druce, 1855 * Aclytia mictochroa Hampson, 1914 * Aclytia modesta Kohler, 1924 * Aclytia petraea Schaus, 1892 * Aclytia punctata Butler, 1876 * Aclytia pydna Druce, 1899 * Aclytia reducta Rothschild, 1912 * Aclytia signatura Walker, 1854 * Aclytia superbior Strand * Aclytia terra Schaus, 1896 * Aclytia ventralis Guerin-Meneville, 1843 ==References== * Category:Euchromiina Category:Moth genera
