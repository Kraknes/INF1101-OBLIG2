Beep Beep is a rock band from Omaha, Nebraska, on Saddle Creek Records.Interview with Beep Beep. SFStation, February 4, 2005. Accessed November 16, 2007. The band was formed in July 2001 by Eric Bemberger and Chris Hughes, formerly of Saddle Creek group Gabardine.Interview with Beep Beep, Thrasher Magazine, March 2005. Accessed November 16, 2007. Former member Joel Petersen plays bass in The Faint and also has his own electronica project, Broken Spindles.Beep Beep Has Unusual Influences.
