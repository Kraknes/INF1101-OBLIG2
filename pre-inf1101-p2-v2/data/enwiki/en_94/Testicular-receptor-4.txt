Testicular receptor 4 also known as NR2C2 (nuclear receptor subfamily 2, group C, member 2) is a protein that in humans is encoded by the NR2C2 gene. The testicular receptor 4 is a member of the nuclear receptor family of transcription factors. == Interactions == Testicular receptor 4 has been shown to interact with * Androgen receptor, * Estrogen receptor alpha, and * Hepatocyte nuclear factor 4 alpha. == See also == * Testicular receptor == References == == Further reading == * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * == External links == * Category:Intracellular receptors Category:Transcription factors
