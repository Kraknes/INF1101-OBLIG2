Rodney Christopher O'Donnell (born 16 August 1956)Rodney O'Donnell player profile ESPN Scrum.com is a former Ireland international rugby union player. He made his Ireland debut in Australia in 1979 when Ireland won both tests (the first Northern Hemisphere side to win 2 tests in the Southern Hemisphere). He toured South Africa in 1980 with the British Lions and at the time played club rugby for St. Mary's College RFC.
