Jalowiec () is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Luban, within Luban County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Prior to 1945 it was in Germany (Lower Silesia) and had the name Wingendorf. It lies approximately south of Luban, and west of the regional capital Wroclaw.
