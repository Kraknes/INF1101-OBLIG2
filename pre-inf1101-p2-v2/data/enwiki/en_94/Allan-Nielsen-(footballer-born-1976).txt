Allan Nielsen (born June 26, 1976) is a Danish former professional football midfielder. Nielsen moved in 2007 from Koge BK to the Danish Superliga side Odense Boldklub. As he only played four matches for them in six months, he moved to the promoted team, Lyngby BK.
