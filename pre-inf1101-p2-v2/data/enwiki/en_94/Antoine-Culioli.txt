Antoine Culioli (4 September 1924 - 9 February 2018)https://diakritik.com/2018/02/12/antoine-culioli-1924-2018-figure- majeure-de-la-linguistique-contemporaine/ was a French linguist of Corsican origins. He developed a linguistic theory known as "Theorie des Operations Enonciatives" (sometimes abbreviated as TOE).
