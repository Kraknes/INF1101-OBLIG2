The Chennai Egmore - Nagercoil Superfast Express is a Superfast train belonging to Southern Railway that runs between Chennai Egmore and Nagercoil Junction in India. It is currently being operated with 12667/12668 train numbers on a weekly basis. == Service== The 12667/Chennai Egmore - Nagercoil Weekly SF Express has an average speed of 55 km/hr and covers 724 km in 13h 15m. The 12668/Nagercoil - Chennai Egmore Weekly Superfast Express has an average speed of 57 km/hr and covers 724 km in 12h 40m. == Route and halts == The important halts of the train are: * * * * * * * ==Coach composite== The train has standard ICF rakes with max speed of 110 kmph.
