Nestle in Perm Nestle Rossiya is a Russian company that manufactures confectioneries, chocolate, and other products such as prepared foods, dairy products, ice cream, beverages, infant foods, and pet care products. It is based in Moscow, Russia and was established in 1871. Nestle Rossiya is a subsidiary company of Nestle S.A.
