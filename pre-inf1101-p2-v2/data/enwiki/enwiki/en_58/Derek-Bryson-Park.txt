Derek Bryson Park (born April 11, 1956) is an American Portfolio Manager, currently serving as Principal of Wilmington Capital Securities. Park has held a number of public positions in his career, including Director of the Federal Home Loan Banks, Vice-Chairman of New York Industrial Development Agency and Director of the New York City Economic Development Corporation. Park attended University of Western Ontario and earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in 1977. In 1979, he earned an MPA from New York University's Robert F. Wagner Graduate School of Public Service, and a Ph.D. from the New York University Graduate School of Arts and Science in 1982.
