Weenen is a settlement in KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa. Weenen may also refer to: * Weenen massacre, a massacre of Voortrekkers by the Zulu * Weenen- Kliprivier Commando, a regiment of the South African Army * Edward van Weenen (1847-1925), an Australian philatelist * John van Weenen (born 1941), an 8th Dan karateka and humanitarian
