Monterey Center is an unincorporated community in Allegan County, Michigan, United States. ==History== Monterey Center was settled in 1833. By 1873, the population was 175, and the settlement had a general store, hotel, and wagon maker. Produce and lumber were the chief industries. ==References== Category:Unincorporated communities in Allegan County, Michigan Category:Unincorporated communities in Michigan
