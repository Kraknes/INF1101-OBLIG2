Marcell Strebek (born 31 July 1975) is a Hungarian football manager and former player, who is currently the manager of THSE Sashalom. ==Managerial career== ===Sashalom=== In an interview with Pesti Foci, Strebek said that he hopes that his team will prosper in the second half of the 2016-17 BLASZ season.
