Lee Ah-reum (; born 22 April 1992) is a South Korean taekwondo athlete. She attended Seoul Physical Education High School () and Korea National Sport University. She won the gold medal at the 2014 Asian Games in the women's featherweight category, defeating Mayu Hamada of Japan.
