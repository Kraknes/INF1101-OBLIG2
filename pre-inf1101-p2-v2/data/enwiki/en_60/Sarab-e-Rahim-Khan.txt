Sarab-e Rahim Khan (, also Romanized as Sarab-e Rahim Khan; also known as Sarab) is a village in Akhtachi-ye Mahali Rural District, Simmineh District, Bukan County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 728, in 139 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Bukan County
