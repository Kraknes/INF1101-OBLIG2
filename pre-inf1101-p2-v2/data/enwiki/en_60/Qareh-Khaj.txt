Qareh Khaj (, also Romanized as Qareh Khaj; also known as Kara Agach, Qarah Khaj, Qareh Aghaj, and Qareh Khach) is a village in Qaleh Darrehsi Rural District, in the Central District of Maku County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 274, in 73 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Maku County
