Yulia Sergeevna Deulina (born 14 April 1984 in Krasnogorsk, Russian SSR, Soviet Union) is a Russian ice hockey forward. ==International career== Deulina was selected for the Russia national women's ice hockey team in the 2010 Winter Olympics.
