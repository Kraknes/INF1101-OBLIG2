Sari Chaman (, also Romanized as Sari Chaman; also known as Sar Chimeh, Sar-e- Chama, Sere-Chima, and Ser-i-Chima) is a village in Bakrabad Rural District, in the Central District of Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 93, in 18 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
