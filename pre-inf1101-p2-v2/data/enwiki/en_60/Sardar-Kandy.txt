Sardar Kandy (, also Romanized as Sardar Kandy; also known as Besheshai, Besh Shay, Bishak, and Byshechay) is a village in Ozomdel-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 29, in 7 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
