Qeshlaq-e Supurgali (, also Romanized as Qeshlaq-e Supurgali; also known as Qeshlaq-e Aran, Qeshlaq-e Sopurgalu, and Qeshlaq-e Supergalu) is a village in Tirchai Rural District, Kandovan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 97, in 23 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
