Sareban Qoli (, also Romanized as Sareban Qoli and Sarebanqoli; also known as Sarvan Qoli, Sorsan Kuli, and Sorvankuli) is a village in Sis Rural District, in the Central District of Shabestar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,340, in 338 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Shabestar County
