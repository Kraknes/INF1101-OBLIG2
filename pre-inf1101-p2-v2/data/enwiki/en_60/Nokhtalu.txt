Nokhtalu (, also Romanized as Nokhtalu, Nokhtalu, and Nokhtalu) is a village in Ajorluy-ye Sharqi Rural District, Baruq District, Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 189, in 38 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
