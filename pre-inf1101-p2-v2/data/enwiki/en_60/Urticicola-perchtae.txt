Urticicola perchtae is a fossil species of air-breathing land snail, a terrestrial pulmonate gastropod mollusk in the family Helicidae, from the Early/Middle Miocene of Sandelzhausen (type locality) and Oggenhausen, southern Germany. ==References== Category:Hygromiidae Category:Miocene gastropods Category:Neogene Germany Category:Fossils of Germany Category:Fossil taxa described in 2013
