Pallavi Gungaram (born 15 December 1993) is a Mauritian beauty pageant titleholder who was crowned Miss Mauritius 2013, and represented her country at the Miss Universe 2014 pageant. ==Early life== Pallavi Gungaram is currently doing her bachelor of science (honors) in psychology at the University of Mauritius as well as her diploma in yoga at the IGCIC. ==Pageantry== ===Miss Mauritius 2013=== Pallavi Gungaram, she is the new Miss Mauritius 2013.
