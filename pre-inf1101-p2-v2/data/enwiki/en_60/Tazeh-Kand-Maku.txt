Tazeh Kand (, also Romanized as Tazeh Kand and Tazehkand; also known as Tazeh Kand-e Bazargan) is a village in Qaleh Darrehsi Rural District, in the Central District of Maku County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 186, in 35 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Maku County
