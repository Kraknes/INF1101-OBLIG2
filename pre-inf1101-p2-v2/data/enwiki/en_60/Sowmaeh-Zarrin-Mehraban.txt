Sowmaeh Zarrin (, also Romanized as Sowma'eh Zarrin; also known as Sowma'eh, Sowme'eh, Suma, and Summa) is a village in Alan Baraghush Rural District, Mehraban District, Sarab County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 444, in 112 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Sarab County
