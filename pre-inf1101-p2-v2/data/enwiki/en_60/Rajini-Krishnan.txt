Rajini Krishnan is an Indian motorcycle racer. He became national road racing champion in 2011 and in 2013 became the first Indian winner of an international road racing event with his victory at the Losail Asian Road Racing Championship in Qatar. He won the Malaysian Superbike championship (open class) in 2015. Rajini was racing for Motorev India as junior driver in the 2011 FIM Asian Road Racing Championship (ARRC). ==Achievements== Nine time consecutive national road racing Champion in multiple categories.
