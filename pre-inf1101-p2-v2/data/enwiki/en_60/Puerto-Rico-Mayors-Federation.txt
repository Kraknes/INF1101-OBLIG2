The Puerto Rico Mayors Federation -- (FAPR)-- is one of the associations that represents the mayors of Puerto Rico. The association represents the mayors from the New Progressive Party while the Puerto Rico Mayors Association represents the ones from the Popular Democratic Party. The organization was founded in 1968 by Carlos Romero Barcelo as the Federation of Municipalities of Puerto Rico.
