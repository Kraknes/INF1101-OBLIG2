Seyyedlar (; also known as Sar Lar, Sarlaro, and Seyyedlar-e Dalikanlu) is a village in Garmeh-ye Shomali Rural District, Kandovan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 139, in 34 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
