Pir Yadegar (, also Romanized as Pir Yadegar and Pir Yadgar; also known as Pirdavar and Pir Dawar) is a village in Hajjilar-e Shomali Rural District, Hajjilar District, Chaypareh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 232, in 46 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaypareh County
