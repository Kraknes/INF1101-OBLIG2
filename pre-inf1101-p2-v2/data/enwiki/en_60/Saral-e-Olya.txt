Saral-e Olya (, also Romanized as Saral-e 'Olya; also known as Saral-e Bala) is a village in Almahdi Rural District, Mohammadyar District, Naqadeh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 186, in 39 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Naqadeh County
