The Taitung Story Museum () or Eslite Taitung Story Museum is a museum in Taitung City, Taitung County, Taiwan. ==History== The museum building was built on the site of the former Taitung Land Administration Office pursuant to a petition to the local government. ==Transportation== The museum is within walking distance of Yangmei Station of Taiwan Railways. ==See also== * List of museums in Taiwan ==References== Category:2004 establishments in Taiwan Category:Literary museums in Taiwan Category:Museums established in 2004 Category:Museums in Taitung County
