Nisvai is an Oceanic language spoken in southeast Malekula, Vanuatu, on the eastern tip of the island, by about 200 speakers.Lynch, John & Terry Crowley. 2001. Languages of Vanuatu: A new survey and bibliography. Canberra: Australian National University. The languages surrounding Nisvai include, or used to include, Port Sandwich, Nasvang, Sorsorian, Axamb and Avok. ==References== Category:Malekula languages Category:Languages of Vanuatu
