Pardul (, also Romanized as Pardul, Pardool, and Pardowl; also known as Pardel, Pardol, Pardoli, Parduli, Perdil, and Pordel) is a village in Rudqat Rural District, Sufian District, Shabestar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 322, in 77 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Shabestar County
