Paeniglutamicibacter cryotolerans, also known as Arthrobacter cryotolerans, is a species of bacteria. It is psychrotolerant, halotolerant, Gram-positive, motile and facultatively anaerobic. It possesses a rod-coccus cycle. Its type strain is LI3T (= DSM 22826T = NCCB 100315T). ==References== ==Further reading== *Whitman, William B., et al., eds.
