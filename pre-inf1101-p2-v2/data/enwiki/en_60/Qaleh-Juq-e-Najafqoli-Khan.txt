Qaleh Juq-e Najafqoli Khan (, also Romanized as Qal'eh Juq-e Najafqoli Khan; also known as Qal'eh Juq) is a village in Owch Tappeh-ye Gharbi Rural District, Torkamanchay District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 626, in 105 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
