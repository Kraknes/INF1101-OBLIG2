Ulu (; ) is a rural locality (a selo) under the administrative jurisdiction of the Town of Tommot in Aldansky District of the Sakha Republic, Russia, located on the left bank of the Ulu River, a tributary of the Amga, and from Tommot. Its population as of the 2010 Census was 139;Sakha Republic Territorial Branch of the Federal State Statistics Service. Results of the 2010 All-Russian Census.
