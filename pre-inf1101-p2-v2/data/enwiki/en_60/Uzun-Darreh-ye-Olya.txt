Uzun Darreh-ye Olya (, also Romanized as Uzun Darreh-ye 'Olya; also known as Uzondarreh 'Olya, Uzun Darreh, Uzun Darreh Bala, Uzun Darreh-ye Bala, and Uzur Darreh) is a village in Akhtachi-ye Gharbi Rural District, in the Central District of Mahabad County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 173, in 22 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Mahabad County
