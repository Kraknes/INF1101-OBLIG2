Trypauchenichthys is a genus of gobies native to fresh, brackish and marine waters along the Indian Ocean and Pacific coasts of Asia. ==Species== There are currently three recognized species in this genus: * Trypauchenichthys larsonae Murdy, 2008 * Trypauchenichthys sumatrensis Hardenberg, 1931 * Trypauchenichthys typus Bleeker, 1860 ==References== Category:Amblyopinae
