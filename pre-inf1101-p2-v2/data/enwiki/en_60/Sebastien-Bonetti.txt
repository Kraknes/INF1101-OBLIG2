Sebastien Bonetti (born 22 September 1977 in Bergerac), is a French rugby union player. He has played for Biarritz Olympique. ==Career== Sebastien Bonetti began playing Rugby Union with Biarritz Olympique. He moved to AS Beziers Herault in 2006.
