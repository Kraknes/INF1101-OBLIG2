The rough cownose ray (Rhinoptera adspersa) is a species of eagle ray in the genus Rhinoptera. ==Distribution== The species occurs in the Indo-West Pacific off India, Malaysia, and the East Indies. The validity of this species is questioned though in Compagno's 1999 checklist.
