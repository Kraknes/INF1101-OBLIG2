Qol Hasan (, also Romanized as Qol Hasan; also known as Kolosan, Kulusan, and Qal'eh Hasan) is a village in Mokriyan-e Shomali Rural District, in the Central District of Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 657, in 150 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
