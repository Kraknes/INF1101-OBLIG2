Sergei Teryayev (born October 13, 1994) is a Russian professional ice hockey defenceman. He is currently playing with Sokol Krasnoyarsk of the Supreme Hockey League (VHL), the second-tier league in Russia. Teryayev made his Kontinental Hockey League debut playing with Amur Khabarovsk during the 2013-14 season. During the 2015-16 season, Teryayev was traded by Amur to fellow KHL club, Salavat Yulaev Ufa on December 1, 2015.
