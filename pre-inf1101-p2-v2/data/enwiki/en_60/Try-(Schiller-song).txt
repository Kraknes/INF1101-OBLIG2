"Try" is the first single from the 2010 Schiller gold album Atemlos with vocals by singer Nadia Ali from New York City. The single was officially released on 9 March 2010 and was peaking at number 58 on German Singles Chart in 2010. The single includes the song ''Salton Sea''. The cover art work shows a photograph of a scene from the music video with Nadia Ali and Christopher von Deylen.
