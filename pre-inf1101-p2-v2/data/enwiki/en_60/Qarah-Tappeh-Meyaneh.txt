Qarah Tappeh (, also Romanized as Qareh Tappeh; also known as Karatepeh, Qara Tappeh, and Qara Tepe) is a village in Barvanan-e Sharqi Rural District, Torkamanchay District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 122, in 35 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
