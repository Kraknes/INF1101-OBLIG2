Tokhm Del (, also Romanized as Tokhomdel; also known as Tokhumdil and Tokhundil') is a village in Ozomdel-e Shomali Rural District, in the Central District of Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 660, in 169 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
