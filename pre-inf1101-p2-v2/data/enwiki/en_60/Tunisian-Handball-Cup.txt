The Tunisian Handball Cup is an elimination handball tournament held annually in Tunisia. It is the second local most important competition in handball after the Tunisian Handball League. It started in 1956, just after Tunisia had gained its Independence, The tournament is dominated mostly by two teams from Tunis with Esperance de Tunis won 26 tournament Cups as a record 16 of them are consecutive followed by Club Africain with 19 Cups and Third team we find Etoile du Sahel with 5 Cups, however The tournament winners will represent Tunisia in the African Handball Cup Winners' Cup. == Winners List== {| |valign="top" | {| class="wikitable" |- !
