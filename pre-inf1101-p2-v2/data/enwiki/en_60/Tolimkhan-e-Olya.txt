Tolimkhan-e Olya (, also Romanized as Tolimkhan-e 'Olya; also known as Towlim Khan-e Bala) is a village in Chaybasar-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Maku County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 58, in 12 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Maku County
