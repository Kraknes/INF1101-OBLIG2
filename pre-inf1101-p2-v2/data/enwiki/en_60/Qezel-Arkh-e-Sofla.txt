Qezel Arkh-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Qezel Arkh-e Sofla; also known as Mahmud Kandi; also known as Qezel Arkh) is a village in Chaybasar-e Shomali Rural District, Bazargan District, Maku County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 506, in 80 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Maku County
