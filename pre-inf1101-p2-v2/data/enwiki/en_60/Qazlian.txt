Qazlian (, also Romanized as Qazlian and Qazilyan; also known as Kasaliah and Qazelyah) is a village in Akhtachi Rural District, in the Central District of Bukan County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 439, in 70 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Bukan County
