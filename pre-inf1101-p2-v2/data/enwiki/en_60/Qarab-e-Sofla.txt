Qarab-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Qarab-e Sofla; also known as Farab-e Sofla, Kerov Ashagi, Nizhnyaya Kerov, and Qarab Pa'in) is a village in Kaghazkonan-e Shomali Rural District, Kaghazkonan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 39, in 16 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
