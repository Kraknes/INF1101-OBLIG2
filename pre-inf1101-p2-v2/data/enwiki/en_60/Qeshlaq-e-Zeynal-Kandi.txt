Qeshlaq-e Zeynal Kandi (, also Romanized as Qeshlaq-e Zeynal Kandi) is a village in Mokriyan-e Shomali Rural District, in the Central District of Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 114, in 27 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
