Steven Russell Young (born July 18, 1953) is a former National Football League offensive tackle who played for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers and Miami Dolphins from 1976 to 1977. He attended Chaffey High School and then the University of Colorado before being drafted by the Buccaneers in the third round, 61st overall, in the 1976 NFL Draft.DatabaseFootball Steve currently serves as area director for the Fellowship of Christian Athletes in Tampa.
