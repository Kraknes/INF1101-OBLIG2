Qarah Bolagh (, also Romanized as Qarah Bolagh and Qareh Bolagh; also known as Qareh Bolaq) is a village in Akhtachi-ye Gharbi Rural District, in the Central District of Mahabad County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 245, in 39 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Mahabad County
