{| |} The Troyer Sportplane VX is an American aircraft that was designed for homebuilt construction. ==Design and development== The Sportsman VX is a mid- wing, open cockpit aircraft with conventional landing gear. The fuselage is made from welded steel tubing with aircraft fabric covering, the wing construction is all-wood with spruce spars. ==Specifications (Troyer VX) == ==See also== ==References== Category:Homebuilt aircraft
