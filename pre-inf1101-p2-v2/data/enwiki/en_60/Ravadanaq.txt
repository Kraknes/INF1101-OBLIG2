Ravadanaq (, also Romanized as Ravadanaq; also known as Iravadana, Ravaranaq, and Rovadanaq) is a village in Ozomdel-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 186, in 40 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
