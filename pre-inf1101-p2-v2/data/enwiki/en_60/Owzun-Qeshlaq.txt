Owzun Qeshlaq (, also Romanized as Owzun Qeshlaq and Uzun Qeshlaq) is a village in Akhtachi-ye Mahali Rural District, Simmineh District, Bukan County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 323, in 61 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Bukan County
