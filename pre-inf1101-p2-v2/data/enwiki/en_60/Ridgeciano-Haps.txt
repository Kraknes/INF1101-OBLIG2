Ridgeciano Haps (born 12 June 1993) is a Dutch professional footballer who currently plays as a left-back for Feyenoord, in the Eredivisie. ==Career== Born in Utrecht, Haps is of Surinamese descent. He came through the youth system of AZ where he established himself as a consistent performer at left- back in the Eredivisie. In summer 2017, he joined Feyenoord.
