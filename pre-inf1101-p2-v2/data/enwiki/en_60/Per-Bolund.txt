Per Bolund (born 3 July 1971) is a Swedish politician. He has served as Minister for Financial Markets since October 2014 and as Minister for Housing since January 2019. Bolund was acting as Minister for Housing and Urban Development from 18 April to 25 May 2016. Bolund was elected to the Riksdag in 2006, representing the Green Party.
