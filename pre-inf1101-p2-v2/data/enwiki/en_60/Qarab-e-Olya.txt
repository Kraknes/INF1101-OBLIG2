Qarab-e Olya (, also Romanized as Qarab-e 'Olya; also known as Farab-e 'Olya, Qarab, Qarab Bala, Qarab-e Bala, and Verkhnyaya Kerov) is a village in Kaghazkonan-e Shomali Rural District, Kaghazkonan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 125, in 33 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
