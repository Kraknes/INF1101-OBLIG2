Vaughn Fowler (born July 7, 1995) is an American professional soccer player. ==Career== On July 12, 2013 it was announced that Fowler, along with Evan Fowler, were promoted to the Richmond Kickers first-team, becoming the very first Academy graduates to make it to the first-team.
