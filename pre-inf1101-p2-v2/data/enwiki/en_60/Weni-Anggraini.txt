Weni Anggraini (born 16 October 1990 in Sridadi) is an Indonesian badminton player. She is a doubles specialist. == Achievements == === BWF Grand Prix (1 title, 2 runners-up) === The BWF Grand Prix has two level such as Grand Prix and Grand Prix Gold. It is a series of badminton tournaments, sanctioned by Badminton World Federation (BWF) since 2007.
