Vira Silenti (16 April 1931 - 1 November 2014) was an Italian actress. ==Career== Born in Naples as Elvira Giovene, Silenti debuted as child actress at ten years old in Una notte dopo l'opera (1942). She studied law at the university and enrolled at the dance school of Jia Ruskaja, then she left her studies to devote herself to the acting career. One of the most active actresses in the Italian television between fifties and sixties, she slowed her activity after her marriage with the film producer Ermanno Donati, until her retirement in the early 1980s. ==Death== Silenti was struck by a car on 31 October 2014 in Rome, Italy.
