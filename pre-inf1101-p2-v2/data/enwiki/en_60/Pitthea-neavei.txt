Pitthea neavei, or Neave's highflier, is a moth of the family Geometridae first described by Louis Beethoven Prout in 1915."Pitthea neavei Prout, 1915". African Moths. Retrieved April 12, 2019. It is found in Malawi and Congo. This species has a wingspan of 37-43 mm.Prout (1915). "New genera and species of African Geometridae".
