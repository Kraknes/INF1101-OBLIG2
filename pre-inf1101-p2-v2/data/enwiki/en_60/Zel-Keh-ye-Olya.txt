Zel Keh-ye Olya (, also Romanized as Zel Keh-ye 'Olya; also known as Zilakeh-ye 'Olya) is a village in Chaybasar-e Shomali Rural District, Bazargan District, Maku County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 750, in 134 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Maku County
