The Edie Adams Show (also Here's Edie) is an American variety/sketch comedy television show starring Edie Adams, Don Chastain, Peter Hanley, Dick Shawn. It originally ran on ABC from September 26, 1963, to March 19, 1964, for 21 episodes.
