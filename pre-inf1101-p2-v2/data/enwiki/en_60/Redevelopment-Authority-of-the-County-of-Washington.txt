The Redevelopment Authority of the County of Washington, also known as the Washington County Redevelopment Authority is the redevelopment authority for Washington County, Pennsylvania. It is charged with redeveloping blighted areas and administering the county's Community Development Block Grant. It was created in 1956. Its operation is governed by the Pennsylvania Housing and Assistance Law of 1949 as well as the Urban Redevelopment Law of 1956. The redevelopment authority managed the creation of Southpointe.
