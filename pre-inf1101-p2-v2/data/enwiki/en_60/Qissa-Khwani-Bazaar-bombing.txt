Qissa Khwani Bazaar bombing took place in the Qissa Khwani Bazaar market in Peshawar, Pakistan on 29 September 2013 killing at least 41 people and another 100 were injured. ==Attack== The attack took place when the car bomb detonated about 11 a.m. (local time) on 29 September 2013 in the crowded market. In this incident, 41 people were killed, of which 16 people were from the same family.
