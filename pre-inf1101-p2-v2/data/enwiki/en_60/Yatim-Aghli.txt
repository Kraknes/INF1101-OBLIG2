Yatim Aghli (, also Romanized as Yatim Aghli and Yatim Agholi; also known as Atamkoli, Etmagli, Etmakli, and Yatim Aqoli) is a village in Hajjilar-e Shomali Rural District, Hajjilar District, Chaypareh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 114, in 30 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaypareh County
