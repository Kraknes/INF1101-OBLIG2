Qamishlu (, also Romanized as Qamishlu; also known as Qameshlu) is a village in Ajorluy-ye Sharqi Rural District, Baruq District, Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 82, in 18 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
