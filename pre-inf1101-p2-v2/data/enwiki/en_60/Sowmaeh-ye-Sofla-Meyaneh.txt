Sowmaeh-ye Sofla (, also Romanized as Sowma'eh-ye Sofla; also known as Sowma'eh, Sowma'eh-ye Pa'in, and Sowme'eh-ye Pa'in) is a village in Barvanan-e Sharqi Rural District, Torkamanchay District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 186, in 45 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
