Bust of general Vo Duy Duong in Dong Thap Province Museum Vo Duy Duong (Wu Wei Yang ; 1827-1866), also Thien Ho Duong (Qian Hu Yang ) was a Vietnamese warlord who, after the submission of the Vietnamese government to the French in 1862, continued to fight against the French (1862-1866) in the wetlands of Dong Thap Muoi, where he died of disease.Vo Duy Duong ==References== Category:1827 births Category:1866 deaths
