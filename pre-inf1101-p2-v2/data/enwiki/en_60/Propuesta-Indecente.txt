"Propuesta Indecente" ("Indecent Proposal") is a bachata song by American singer Romeo Santos from his second studio album Formula, Vol. 2. The song mixes the sound of bachata and Argentine tango. The music video was filmed in Argentina and features Mexican entertainer Eiza Gonzalez and earned the Lo Nuestro Award for Video of the Year at the 26th Lo Nuestro Awards. The following year it received the Lo Nuestro Award for Tropical Song of the Year.
