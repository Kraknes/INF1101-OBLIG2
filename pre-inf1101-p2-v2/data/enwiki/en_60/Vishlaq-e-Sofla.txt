Vishlaq-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Vishlaq-e Sofla and Vishlaq Sofla; also known as Ashaga Veshlya, Vaslar Ashaghi, Vishlagh Sofla, Vishlaq-e Pa'in, and Vishlaq Pa'in) is a village in Valdian Rural District, Ivughli District, Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 377, in 91 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Khoy County
