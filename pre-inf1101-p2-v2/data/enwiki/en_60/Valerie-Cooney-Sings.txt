Valerie Cooney Sings is an Australian television series of which very little information is available. Broadcast on Sydney station ABN-2 from 8 February 1958 to 15 March 1958, TV listings suggest the series featured Valerie Cooney and the Jack Allan Trio. It was a 15-minute series aired on Saturdays. Cooney had previously hosted Picture Page and later in 1958 appeared in TCN-9's series The House and Garden Show.
