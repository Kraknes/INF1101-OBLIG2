Siah Kalan (, also Romanized as Siah Kalan, Siah Kalan, Siyah Kalan, and Siah Kalan; also known as Siahkulian, Siah Kulyan, and Siakh-Kulan) is a village in Ozomdel-e Shomali Rural District, in the Central District of Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 471, in 119 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
