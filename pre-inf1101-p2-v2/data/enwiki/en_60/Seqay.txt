Seqay (, also Romanized as Seqay and Saqay; also known as Saghay, Sirgai, and Sygay) is a village in Jushin Rural District, Kharvana District, Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 869, in 187 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
