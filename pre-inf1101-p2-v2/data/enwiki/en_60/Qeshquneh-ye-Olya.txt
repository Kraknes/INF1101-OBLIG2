Qeshquneh-ye Olya (, also Romanized as Qeshquneh-ye 'Olya; also known as Qeshqeneh-ye 'Olya) is a village in Mangur-e Sharqi Rural District, Khalifan District, Mahabad County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 62, in 6 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Mahabad County
