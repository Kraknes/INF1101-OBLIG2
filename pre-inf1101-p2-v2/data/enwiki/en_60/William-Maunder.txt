William "Podge" Maunder (30 November 1902 - 25 June 1964) was an Australian association football player. Maunder is recognised as the player who scored Australia's first international goal. ==Playing career== ===Club career=== In his club career, Maunder scored more than 500 goals in Northern NSW competition and was offered a professional contract by Scottish club St Mirren F.C.. ===International career=== Maunder made his international debut for Australia on 17 June 1922 in Australia's first recognised international match, scoring on 45 minutes.
