Qaranqu (, also Romanized as Qaranqu; also known as Gharanghoo, Karangi, Qarangu, Qaranku, and Qarengi) is a village in Baba Jik Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 538, in 103 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
