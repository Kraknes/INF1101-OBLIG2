Sovinj-e Olya (, also Romanized as Sovinj-e 'Olya; also known as Sovich-e Bala) is a village in Barvanan-e Gharbi Rural District, Torkamanchay District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 40, in 9 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
