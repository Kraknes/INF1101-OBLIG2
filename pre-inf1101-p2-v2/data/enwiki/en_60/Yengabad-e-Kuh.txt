Yengabad-e Kuh (, also Romanized as Yengabad-e Kuh; also known as Nigabad-e Kuh) is a village in Kolah Boz-e Sharqi Rural District, in the Central District of Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 223, in 54 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
