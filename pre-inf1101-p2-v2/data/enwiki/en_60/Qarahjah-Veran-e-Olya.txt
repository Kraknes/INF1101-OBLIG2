Qarahjah Veran-e Olya (, also Romanized as Qarahjah Veran-e 'Olya; also known as Qarahchah Veran-e Bala, Qarahchah Veran-e 'Olya, and Qarehchah Veran-e Bala) is a village in Chaldoran-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 166, in 22 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
