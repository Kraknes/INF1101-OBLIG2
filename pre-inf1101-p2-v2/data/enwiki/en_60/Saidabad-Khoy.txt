Saidabad (, also Romanized as Sa'idabad; also known as Saidawar, Saidvan, and Seydvan) is a village in Gowharan Rural District, in the Central District of Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 2,876, in 699 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Khoy County
