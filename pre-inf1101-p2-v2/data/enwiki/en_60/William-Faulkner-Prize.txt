The William Faulkner Prize or William Faulkner Award could refer to: *William Faulkner Prize (Rennes, a prize given by the William Faulkner Foundation at University of Rennes 2 in France. * Pirate's Alley William Faulkner Prize, a prize given by the Pirate's Alley Faulkner Society in New Orleans. *William Faulkner Foundation Award, an award given by the William Faulkner Foundation in the United States. *PEN/Faulkner Award for Fiction, a prize given by the PEN/Faulkner Foundation.
