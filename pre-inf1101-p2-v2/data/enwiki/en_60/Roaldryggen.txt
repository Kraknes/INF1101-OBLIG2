Roaldryggen is a mountain ridge in Sorkapp Land at Spitsbergen, Svalbard. It has a length of about 3.5 kilometers, and is located southwest of Vasil'evbreen. The highest peak on the ridge is 730 m.a.s.l. The ridge is named after polar explorer Roald Amundsen. ==References== Category:Mountains of Spitsbergen
