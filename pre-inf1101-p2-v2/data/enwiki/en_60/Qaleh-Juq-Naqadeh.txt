Qaleh Juq (, also Romanized as Qal'eh Juq and Qal'eh-ye Juq; also known as Qal'a Jukh and Qal'eh Jug) is a village in Solduz Rural District, in the Central District of Naqadeh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 158, in 37 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Naqadeh County
