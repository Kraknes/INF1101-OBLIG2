Snowville Christian Church, also known as Cypress Grove Christian Church, is a historic Christian Church (Disciples of Christ) church complex located in Snowville, Pulaski County, Virginia. It was built in 1864, and is a one-story, gable-roofed frame church building. The building measures 40 feet by 60 feet.
