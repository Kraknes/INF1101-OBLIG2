Qermezi Dash-e Anbar (, also Romanized as Qermezi Dash-e Anbar; also known as Qermezdash and Qermezdash-e Chaldoran) is a village in Chaldoran-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
