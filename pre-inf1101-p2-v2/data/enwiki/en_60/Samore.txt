Samore is a surname. Notable people with the surname include: * Gary Samore, Barack Obama's White House Coordinator for Arms Control and Weapons of Mass Destruction * Antonio Samore (1905-1983), Italian Cardinal of the Catholic Church * Samori Ture (c. 1830-1900), founder of the Wassoulou Empire *Abhishek Singh Samore (1995 - ), Founder & President of NGO: Jai Jawan Society
