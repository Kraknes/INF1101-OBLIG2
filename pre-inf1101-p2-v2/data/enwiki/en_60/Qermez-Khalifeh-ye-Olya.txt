Qermez Khalifeh-ye Olya (, also Romanized as Qermez Khalifeh-ye 'Olya) is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, in the Central District of Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 539, in 152 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
