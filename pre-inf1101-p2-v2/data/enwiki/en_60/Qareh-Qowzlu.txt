Qareh Qowzlu (, also Romanized as Qareh Qowzlu; also known as Qareh Gowzlu) is a village in Marhemetabad-e Miyani Rural District, Marhemetabad District, Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 702, in 177 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
