Zeyn ol Hajjilu (, also Romanized as Zeyn ol Hajjilu and Zin al Hajjilu'; also known as Zeynal Aghaj) is a village in Shurakat-e Jonubi Rural District, Ilkhchi District, Osku County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,443, in 357 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Osku County
