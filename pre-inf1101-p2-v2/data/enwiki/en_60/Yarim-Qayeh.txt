Yarim Qayeh (, also Romanized as Yarim Qayeh; also known as Yarem Qayeh, Yarim Ghayeh, Yarimqaya, and Yarymkaya) is a village in Dizaj Rural District, in the Central District of Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 357, in 75 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Khoy County
