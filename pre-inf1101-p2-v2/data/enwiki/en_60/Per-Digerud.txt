Per Digerud (25 July 1933 - 13 August 1988) was a Norwegian cyclist. He was born in Oslo, and was the father of Geir Digerud. He competed at the 1960 Summer Olympics in the individual road race . He finished at 71. place. He won the Norwegian National Road Race Championship in 1960, 1961 and 1964.
