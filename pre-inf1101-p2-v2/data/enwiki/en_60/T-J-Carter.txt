Marc Antonio Carter, commonly known as T. J. Carter (born May 22, 1985) is an American professional basketball player for Quimsa of the LNB. He is a 1.92 m (6 ft 3 in) tall shooting guard. ==College career== Carter played college basketball at North Carolina-Wilmington, with the UNC Wilmington Seahawks, from 2003 to 2008. ==Professional career== Carter began his pro career in the German League with the Dusseldorf Giants in 2008. He then played with the Cypriot League club AEL Limassol. He moved to the German club Phoenix Hagen in 2011.Sportando.net Phoenix Hagen adds TJ Carter. He next played with the Greek League club Rethymno, before joining the Greek club Panionios in 2013.Eurocupbasketball.com PANIONIOS gets Carter. He moved to the Greek club PAOK in 2014.Eurocupbasketball.com PAOK Thessaloniki picks up Carter at guard. In June 2015, he signed with AEK Athens.AEK Athens brings in Carter.Marc Antonio Carter moves to AEK Athens. Carter joined the Polish Stal Ostrow Wielkopolski on November 12, 2016.Stal Ostrow lands Marc Antonio Carter. On July 22, 2017, Carter signed with Lietuvos rytas Vilnius of the Lithuanian Basketball League, but could not adapt to become a part of head coach Kurtinaitis' tactics, so eventually the contract was terminated by mutual agreement on November 11, 2017.
