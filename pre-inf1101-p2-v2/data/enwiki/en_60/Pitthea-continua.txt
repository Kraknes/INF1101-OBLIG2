Pitthea continua is a moth of the family Geometridae first described by Francis Walker in 1854. ==Distribution== It is found in Cameroon, the Democratic Republic of the Congo, Gabon, Ghana, Nigeria, Sierra Leone and Uganda. ==References== ==External links== * "Pitthea continua Walker, 1854".
