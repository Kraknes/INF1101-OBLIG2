Pursekhlu (, also Romanized as Pursekhlu; also known as Porsekhlu) is a village in Owch Tappeh-ye Sharqi Rural District, in the Central District of Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 307, comprised in 64 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
