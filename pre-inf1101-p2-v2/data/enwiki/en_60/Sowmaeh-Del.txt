Sowmaeh Del (, also Romanized as Sowma'eh Del, Sowme'eh Del, and Sowmeh Del; also known as Sumadi, Sumadil, and Suma-Dyl') is a village in Ozomdel-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,369, in 321 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
