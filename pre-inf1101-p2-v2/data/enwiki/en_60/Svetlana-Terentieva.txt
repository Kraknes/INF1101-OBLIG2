Svetlana Sergeevna Terentieva (born 25 September 1983 in Yekaterinburg, Russian SSR, Soviet Union) is a Russian ice hockey forward. ==International career== Terentieva was selected for the Russia national women's ice hockey team in the 2002 and 2010 Winter Olympics. In both Olympics she recorded one goal in five games. She also played in the qualifying tournament for the 2006 Olympics.IIHF - Team Russia Stats - 2002 OlympicsIIHF - Team Russia Stats - 2010 OlympicsIIHF - Team Russia Stats - 2006 Olympics Qualifying Tournament Terentieva has also appeared for Russia at nine IIHF Women's World Championships. Her first appearance came in 1999.
