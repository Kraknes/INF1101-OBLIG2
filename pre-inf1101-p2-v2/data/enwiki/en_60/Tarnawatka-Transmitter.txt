Tarnawatka Transmitter is a radio-television transmitting centre located in the village of Tarnawatka near Zamosc. The 154 metre guyed steel mast was built in 1984 in order to provide radio and television coverage in Zamosc Voivodeship and the surrounding areas. It is a broadcasting station of about medium powers and reception is also possible in the westmost areas of Ukraine.
