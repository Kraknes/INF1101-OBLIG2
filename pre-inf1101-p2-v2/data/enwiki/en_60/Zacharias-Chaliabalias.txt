Zacharias Chaliabalias (; 8 March 1946 - 22 July 2020) was a Greek international footballer who spent his entire career with Iraklis playing as a centre-back. ==Club career== Chaliabalias was born in Thessaloniki, and started his career for Iraklis in 1964. He was driven out of the club in 1975, after he admitted to a bribe attempt by Panathinaikos officials. Chaliabalias is the fifth all-time leader in league appearances for Iraklis, appearing in no less than 280 occasions for the club. ==International career== Chaliabalias made his debut for Greece in a friendly 4-1 home win against Egypt on 21 November 1968.
