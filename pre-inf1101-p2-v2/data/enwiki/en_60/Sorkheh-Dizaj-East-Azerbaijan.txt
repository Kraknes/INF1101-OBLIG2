Sorkheh Dizaj (, also Romanized as Sorkheh Dizaj; also known as Sirkhazach) is a village in Ozomdel-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 496, in 101 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
