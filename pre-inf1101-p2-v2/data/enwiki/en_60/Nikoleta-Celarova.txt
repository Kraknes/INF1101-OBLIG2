Nikoleta Celarova (born 27 February 1983 in Kezmarok, Czechoslovakia) is a Slovakian ice hockey forward. ==International career== Celarova was selected for the Slovakia national women's ice hockey team in the 2010 Winter Olympics. She played in all five games, but did not record a point.IIHF - Team Slovakia Stats - 2010 Olympics She played in all seven games of the 2010 Olympic qualifying campaign scoring twenty-three points.IIHF - Team Slovakia Stats - 2010 Olympic Qualifier round 2IIHF - Team Slovakia Stats - 2010 Olympic Qualifier round 1 Celarova has also appeared for Slovakia at five IIHF Women's World Championships, across two levels.
