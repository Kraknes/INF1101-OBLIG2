Steane Kremerskothen is a former Australian rules football player from Tasmania. Kremerskothen holds the record for number of games played at the top level of regional and statewide football in Tasmania. He played 49 games for Launceston Football Club, 61 with Clarence Football Club, and 242 with North Launceston Football Club for a total of 352 senior appearances.
