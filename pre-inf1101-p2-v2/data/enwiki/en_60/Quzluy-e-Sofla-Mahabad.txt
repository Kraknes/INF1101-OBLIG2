Quzluy-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Quzluy-e Sofla; also known as Qowzlu-ye Pa'in and Qowzlu-ye Sofla) is a village in Kani Bazar Rural District, Khalifan District, Mahabad County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 346, in 49 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Mahabad County
