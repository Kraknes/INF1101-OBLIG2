Qoli Dizaji (, also Romanized as Qoli Dizaji; also known as Qoli Dizaj) is a village in Avajiq-e Jonubi Rural District, Dashtaki District, Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 267, in 49 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
