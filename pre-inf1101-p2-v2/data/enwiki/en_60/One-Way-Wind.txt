One Way Wind is a Samoan football club. It currently plays in the Samoa National League. ==History== The first recorded appearance of One Way Wind in the Samoan football league system was in 2010-11 when they finished third in the Samoan first division.Samoa 2010-11 at RSSSF It is unclear whether there were play-offs for promotion or indeed, whether the club were promoted at all, because details are missing for the next season.Samoa - List of Champions at RSSSF However, they finished fourth in 2012-13, above Adidas Soccer Club on goal difference, but behind Kiwi, with 37 points, winning 11 and drawing four of their 22 games.Samoa 2012-13 at RSSSF ==References== Category:Football clubs in Samoa
