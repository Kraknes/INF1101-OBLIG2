Qarah Tulki-ye Sofla (, also Romanized as Qarah Tulki-ye Sofla and Qareh Tulki-ye Sofla; also known as Ghareh Tavakol Sofla, Kara Tulki, Qareh Towlki- ye Pa'in, Qareh Tuli-ye Pa'in, Qareh Tulki, Qareh Tulki Pa'in, and Qareh Tulki-ye Bala) is a village in Baba Jik Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran.
