Zhu Jiawei (Chinese: Zhu Jia Wei ; Pinyin: Zhu Jiawei, born 19 August 1990) is a Chinese football player. ==Club career== Zhu joined Inter Shanghai youth team system with his countyman Rao Weihui in 2003 and was promoted to Shaanxi Chanba first team squad in 2008. On 17 May 2008, he made his senior debut aged 17 years and 271 days in a 2008 Chinese Super League match which Shaanxi Chanba tied with Dalian Shide 1-1.
