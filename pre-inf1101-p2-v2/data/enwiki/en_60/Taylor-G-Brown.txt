Taylor Groves Brown (1890-1957) was a member of the Wisconsin State Senate. ==Biography== Brown was born on February 28, 1890 in Richland County, Wisconsin. He attended the University of Wisconsin-Platteville and the University of Wisconsin-Madison. ==Career== Brown was a member of the Senate from the 19th district from 1939 to 1950. Previously, he was Mayor of Oshkosh, Wisconsin from 1930 to 1933. He was a Republican.
