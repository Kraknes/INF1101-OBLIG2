Qurdarik-e Olya (, also Romanized as Qurdarik-e 'Olya; also known as Qurdik-e Bala and Qurdik-e 'Olya) is a village in Sokmanabad Rural District, Safayyeh District, Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 622, in 132 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Khoy County
