Shakarrao Deo aka Nanasaheb Deo was the founder of Satkaryottejak Sabha. He was a member of Constituent Assembly of India in 1949. He was First Secretary of the Maharashtra Pradesh Congress Committee, 1920, and General secretary of the Congress, 1946-50, where he played a key role during the drafting of the Constitution of India and the transfer-of-power negotiations. ==References== Category:People from Dhule Category:Members of the Constituent Assembly of India Category:Indian National Congress politicians Category:Indian independence activists from Maharashtra
