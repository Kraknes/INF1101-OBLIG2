Tryssogobius is a genus of small gobies native to the western Pacific Ocean. The species in this genus are known colloquially as "fairygobies". ==Species== There are currently seven recognized species in this genus: * Tryssogobius colini Larson & Hoese, 2001 (Colin's fairygoby) * Tryssogobius flavolineatus J. E. Randall, 2006 (Yellow-lined fairygoby) * Tryssogobius longipes Larson & Hoese, 2001 (Longfin fairygoby) * Tryssogobius nigrolineatus J. E. Randall, 2006 (Deepreef fairygoby) * Tryssogobius porosus Larson & I. S. Chen, 2007 * Tryssogobius quinquespinus J. E. Randall, 2006 (Fivespine fairygoby) * Tryssogobius sarah G. R. Allen & Erdmann, 2012 (Sarah's fairygoby) ==References== Category:Gobiidae Category:Taxa named by Helen K. Larson
