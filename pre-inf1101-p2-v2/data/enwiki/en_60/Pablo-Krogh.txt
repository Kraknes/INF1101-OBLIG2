Pablo Willy Krogh Baraona (21 February 1963 - 2 September 2013) was a Chilean film, theater, television and voice actor. His credits included the Chilean films Machuca in 2004 and the 2009 dramatic film, Dawson Isla 10, in which he portrayed the late politician, Jose Toha. His television roles included the Chilevision telenovela series, La Dona. He was currently starring in the TVN television series, Bim bam bum, at the time of his death in 2013. Krogh died from tongue cancer in Santiago, Chile, at approximately 5:00 a.m. on 2 September 2013, at the age of 50. He had been diagnosed with tongue cancer in 2012.
