Shanejan (, also Romanized as Shanejan and Shanjan; also known as Chakawan, Chana'van, and Keyvan) is a village in Guney-ye Sharqi Rural District, in the Central District of Shabestar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2011 census, its population was 356, in 102 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Shabestar County
