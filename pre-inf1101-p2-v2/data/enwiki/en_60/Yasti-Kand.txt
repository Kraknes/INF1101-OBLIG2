Yasti Kand (, also Romanized as Yasti Kand; also known as Yasi Kand) is a village in Ajorluy-ye Sharqi Rural District, Baruq District, Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 331, in 73 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
