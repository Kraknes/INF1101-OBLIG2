Teghnit-e Vasat (, also Romanized as Teghnit-e Vasat; also known as Urtakand) is a village in Chaldoran-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. According to the 2006 census, its population is 66, in 12 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
