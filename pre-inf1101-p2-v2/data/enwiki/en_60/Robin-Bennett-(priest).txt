Robin Bennett (b 6 November 1934) was Archdeacon of Dudley from 1985 until 1986.Church news The Times (London, England), Tuesday, October 14, 1986; pg. 19; Issue 62588 Bennett was educated at Northgate Grammar School, Ipswich and St John's College, Durham;'BENNETT, Robin', Who's Who 2013, A & C Black, an imprint of Bloomsbury Publishing plc, 2013; online edn, Oxford University Press, Dec 2012 ; online edn, Nov 2012 accessed 24 Sept 2013 and ordained in 1961.Crockford's Clerical Directory 1975-76 London: Oxford University Press, 1976 p77 After curacies and incumbencies in Essex and East London he was Principal of the Aston Training Scheme from 1977. He left the Church of England in 1988 to join the Society of Friends.
