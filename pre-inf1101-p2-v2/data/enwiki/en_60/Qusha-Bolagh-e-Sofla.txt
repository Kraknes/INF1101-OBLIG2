Qusha Bolagh-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Qusha Bolagh-e Sofla and Qowsha Bolagh-e Sofla; also known as Qusha Bolagh-e Pa'in) is a village in Baba Jik Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 185, in 32 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
