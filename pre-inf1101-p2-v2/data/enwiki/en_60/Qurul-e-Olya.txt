Qurul-e Olya (, also Romanized as Qurul-e 'Olya; also known as Qurul-e Bala) is a village in Bastam Rural District, in the Central District of Chaypareh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 230, in 41 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaypareh County
