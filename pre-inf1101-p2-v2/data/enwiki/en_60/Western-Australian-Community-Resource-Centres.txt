Western Australian Community Resource Centres is a government agency in Western Australia. In the 1990s the Western Australian Government created funding for community centres in Western Australia.http://www.drd.wa.gov.au/programsandprojects/services/Pages/default.aspx The system was boosted in the 2000s by funding from the Royalties for Regions program. In some cases they were successors to earlier Telecentres around the state.http://www.wanowandthen.com/telecentres.html ==Notes== Category:Statutory agencies of Western Australia
