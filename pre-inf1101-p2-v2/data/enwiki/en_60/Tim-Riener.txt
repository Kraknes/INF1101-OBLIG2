Tim Riener is a former political candidate in Wisconsin. ==Biography== Riener was born on December 16, 1960. He was raised in Milwaukee, Wisconsin and later attended the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee and Marquette University. Riener went on to serve in the United States Marine Corps and the Wisconsin Air National Guard. On July 10, 1982, Riener married Doris Spangler. They have three children and reside in Oak Creek, Wisconsin. ==Political career== Riener was candidate for the United States House of Representatives from Wisconsin's 4th congressional district in 2000. He lost to incumbent Jerry Kleczka. Previously, Riener had defeated Roman R.
