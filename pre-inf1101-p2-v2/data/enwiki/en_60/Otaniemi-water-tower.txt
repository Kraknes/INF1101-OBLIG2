Otaniemi water tower in 2010 The Otaniemi water tower is a structure designed by the well-known Finnish architect Alvar Aalto in 1971. The water tower is located in Otaniemi area, Espoo, Finland. The total capacity of the tower is 6000 m3. ==References== Category:Alvar Aalto buildings Category:Buildings and structures in Espoo Category:Water towers in Finland
