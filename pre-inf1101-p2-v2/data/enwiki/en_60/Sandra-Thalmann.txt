Sandra Thalmann (born 18 December 1992) is a Swiss ice hockey player. ==International career== Thalmann was selected for the Switzerland national women's ice hockey team in the 2010 Winter Olympics. She played in all five games, but did not register a point.IIHF - Team Switzerland Stats - 2010 Olympics Thalmann has also appeared for Switzerland at four IIHF Women's World Championships. Her first appearance came in 2009. She was a member of the bronze medal-winning team at the 2012 championships.IIHF - Team Switzerland Stats - 2009 World ChampionshipIIHF - Team Switzerland Stats - 2011 World ChampionshipIIHF - Team Switzerland Stats - 2012 World ChampionshipIIHF - Team Switzerland Stats - 2013 World Championship Thalmann made two appearances for the Switzerland women's national under-18 ice hockey team at the IIHF World Women's U18 Championships. Her first came in 2008.
