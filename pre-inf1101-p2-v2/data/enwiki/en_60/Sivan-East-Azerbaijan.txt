Sivan (, also Romanized as Sivan; also known as Sanvan, Senavan, Sevan, Seyyedan, and Suan) is a village in Arzil Rural District, Kharvana District, Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 51, in 17 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
