Thermodesulforhabdus norvegica is a species of thermophilic sulfate-reducing bacteria, the type and only species of its genus. It is gram-negative, acetate-oxidizing, with type strain A8444. ==References== ==Further reading== *Whitman, William B., et al., eds. Bergey's manual(r) of systematic bacteriology. Vol. 3.
