Qarajeh Malek (, also Romanized as Qarajeh Malek and Qarajeh Molk; also known as Gharajeh Malek, Karadzha, Karajamulik, and Qarajeh Malik) is a village in Ozomdel-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 75, in 16 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
