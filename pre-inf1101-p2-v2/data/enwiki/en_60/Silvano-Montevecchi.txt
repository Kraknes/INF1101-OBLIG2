Silvano Montevecchi (31 March 1938 - 27 September 2013) was a Roman Catholic bishop. Ordained to the priesthood in 1962, Montevecchi was named bishop of the Diocese of Ascoli Piceno, Italy, in 1997 where he died in office in 2013.Silvano Montevecchi ==Notes== Category:1938 births Category:2013 deaths Category:Bishops in le Marche
