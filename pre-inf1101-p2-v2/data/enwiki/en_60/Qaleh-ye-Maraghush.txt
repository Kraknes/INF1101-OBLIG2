Qaleh-ye Maraghush (, also Romanized as Qal'eh-ye Maraghush, Qal'ehmaraghush, and Qal'eh Maraghush) is a village in Guney-ye Gharbi Rural District, Tasuj District, Shabestar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 271, in 56 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Shabestar County
