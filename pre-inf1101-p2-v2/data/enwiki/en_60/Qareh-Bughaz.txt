Qareh Bughaz (, also Romanized as Qareh Bughaz; also known as Qarah Bughar) is a village in Ajorluy-ye Gharbi Rural District, Baruq District, Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 29, in 5 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
