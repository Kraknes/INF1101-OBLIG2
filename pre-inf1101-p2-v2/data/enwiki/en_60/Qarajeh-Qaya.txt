Qarajeh Qaya (, also Romanized as Qarajeh Qaya and Qarejeh Qaya; also known as Gharajeh Ghiya, Qarajeh Qayah, Qarajeh Qayeh, Qarehjeh Qia, and Qarajenkiah) is a village in Owch Tappeh-ye Sharqi Rural District, in the Central District of Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 112, in 25 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
