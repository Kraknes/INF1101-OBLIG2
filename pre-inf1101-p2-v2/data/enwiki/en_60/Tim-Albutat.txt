Tim Albutat (born 23 September 1992) is a German footballer who plays as a defensive midfielder for KFC Uerdingen. ==Career== He made his professional debut in the Bundesliga on 11 May 2013 against SpVgg Greuther Furth. He joined MSV Duisburg for the 2014-15 season. He extended his contract on 12 June 2019.
