Qanat-e Mirza Jalil (, also Romanized as Qanat-e Mirza Jalil; also known as Kahriz) is a village in Churs Rural District, in the Central District of Chaypareh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 602, in 144 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaypareh County
