Sara Benz (born 25 August 1992 in Zurich, Switzerland) is a Swiss ice hockey forward. ==International career== Benz was selected for the Switzerland national women's ice hockey team in the 2010 Winter Olympics. She played in all five games, scoring a goal.IIHF - Team Switzerland Stats - 2010 Olympics Benz has also appeared for Switzerland at four IIHF Women's World Championships. Her first appearance came in 2009. She was a member of the bronze medal winning team at the 2012 championships. IIHF - Team Switzerland Stats - 2009 World ChampionshipIIHF - Team Switzerland Stats - 2011 World ChampionshipIIHF - Team Switzerland Stats - 2012 World ChampionshipIIHF - Team Switzerland Stats - 2013 World Championship Benz made three appearances for the Switzerland women's national under-18 ice hockey team, at two levels of the IIHF World Women's U18 Championships. Her first came in 2008.
