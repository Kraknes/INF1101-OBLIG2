Zangabad (, also Romanized as Zangabad; also known as Zangiabad and Zengiabad) is a village in Ozomdel-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 421, in 73 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
