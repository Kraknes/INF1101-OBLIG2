Siaqul-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Siaqul-e Sofla; also known as Siah Gol-e Pa'in) is a village in Mangur-e Sharqi Rural District, Khalifan District, Mahabad County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 142, in 22 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Mahabad County
