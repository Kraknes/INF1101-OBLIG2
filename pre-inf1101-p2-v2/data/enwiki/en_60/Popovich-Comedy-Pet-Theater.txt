The Popovich Comedy Pet Theater (also known as the Pet Travel Show Inc.) is a show combining animal and human performers. They perform comedy routines through a variety of skits that include clowns, acrobatics, juggling, and stunts under the direction of Gregory Popovich, the show's creator. == Acts and stunts == Acts include pet fire marshals, dog football, Dr. Doolittle, and funny geese. Stunts include cats and dogs walking tight ropes, balancing on metallic balls, jumping through hoops, jumping rope, using scooters and playing football.
