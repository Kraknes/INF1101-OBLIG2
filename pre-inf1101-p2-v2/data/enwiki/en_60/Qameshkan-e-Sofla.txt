Qameshkan-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Qameshkan-e Sofla; also known as Qameshgan-e Pa'in and Qameshgan-e Sofla) is a village in Bastam Rural District, in the Central District of Chaypareh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 92, in 24 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaypareh County
