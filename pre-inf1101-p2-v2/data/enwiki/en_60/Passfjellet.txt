Passfjellet is a mountain in Nordenskiold Land at Spitsbergen, Svalbard. It has several peaks, of which the highest is 785 m.a.s.l. The mountain is located between Bjornsonfjellet, the upper part of Grondalen, Passdalen and Istjorndalen. The mountain pass Grondalspasset separates the mountain from the glacier Tavlebreen. ==References== Category:Mountains of Spitsbergen
