Richard J. Burdge (December 28, 1833 -8 May 1916) Richard J Burdge at findagrave.com was a member of the Wisconsin State Assembly and the Wisconsin State Senate. == Background == Burdge was born on December 28, 1833 in Axbridge, England. Later, he resided in Beloit, Wisconsin. == Political career == Burdge was a member of the Assembly during the 1879 and 1880 sessions. Later, he was a member of the Senate during the 1891 and 1893 sessions, representing the 17th District. Other positions Burdge held include member of the School Board and Mayor of Beloit.
