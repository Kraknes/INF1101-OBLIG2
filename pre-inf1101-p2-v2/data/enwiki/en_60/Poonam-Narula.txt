Poonam Narula is an Indian actress who started her career on the small screen. She is known for her roles in popular television shows like Shararat and Kasautii Zindagii Kay among others. == Career == Poonam started her career on television after taking acting lessons from Ashok Kumar's acting academy in Mumbai. She was seen in Star Plus TV show Shararat where she played one of the female lead roles in the sitcom. Within a short span of her entry, she won the hearts of TV audiences and then she was offered TV shows by Balaji Telefilms' Itihaas, Kasautii Zindagii Kay', Mano Ya Na Mano, Kaahin Kissii Roz, Kkusum and Kahaani Ghar Ghar Kii. == Personal life == Poonam married actor Manish Goel in 2002.
