Paul Coghlan is a former Trials Division and Court of Appeals justice at the Supreme Court of Victorias. He has been practicing law since 1969 and was the principal judge of the criminal division of the Court between 2010 and 2012. Between 2000 and 2007 he served as Director of Public Prosecutions and Chief Crown Prosecutor. He retired from the Bench in 2014.
