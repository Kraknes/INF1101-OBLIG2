Yekmaleh-ye Sofla (, also romanized as Yekmaleh-ye Sofla; also known as Yekmaleh and Yekmaleh-ye Pa'in) is a village in Chaldoran-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 37, in 9 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
