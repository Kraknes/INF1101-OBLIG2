Shireh Jin (, also Romanized as Shireh Jin; also known as Shirvanchiq, Shirvanjik, and Shirvan Jikh) is a village in Razliq Rural District, in the Central District of Sarab County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,304, in 340 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Sarab County
