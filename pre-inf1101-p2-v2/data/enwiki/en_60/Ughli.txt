Ughli (, also Romanized as Ughli; also known as Eroqli, Evli, Oghlu, Ovli, and Ovly) is a village in Aji Chay Rural District, in the Central District of Tabriz County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 738, in 199 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Tabriz County
