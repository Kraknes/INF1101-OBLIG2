Sarkesh (, also Romanized as Sarkash and Serkesh; also known as Sirkash and Sirkesh) is a village in Jushin Rural District, Kharvana District, Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 224, in 53 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
