Oxfordhalvoya is a peninsula in Wahlenbergfjorden at the southwestern side of Nordaustlandet, Svalbard. It is located between Bodleybukta and Etonbreen at the head of the fjord. The bay Kloverbladbukta cuts into the peninsula, and its highest point is Carfaxhaugen at 103 m.a.s.l.
