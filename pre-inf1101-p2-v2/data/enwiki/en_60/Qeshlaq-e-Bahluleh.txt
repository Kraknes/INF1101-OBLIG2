Qeshlaq-e Bahluleh (, also Romanized as Qeshlaq-e Bahluleh; also known as Bahluleh) is a village in Chaybasar-e Shomali Rural District, Bazargan District, Maku County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 238, in 50 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Maku County
