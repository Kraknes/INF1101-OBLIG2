Qurdarik-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Qurdarik-e Sofla; also known as Qurdik-e Pa'in and Qurdik-e Sofla) is a village in Sokmanabad Rural District, Safayyeh District, Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 232, in 57 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Khoy County
