Qareh Qayeh (; also known as Karaga, Qaraga, Qarah Qaya, Qarehgeh, and Qareh Qaya) is a village in Garmeh-ye Shomali Rural District, Kandovan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 100, in 24 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
