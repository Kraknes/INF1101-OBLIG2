Ricardo Jacob Israel Zipper (born 7 October 1950) is a Chilean lawyer and academician. Israel was a candidate for President of Chile in 2013, representing the Regionalist Party of the Independents (PRI), but lost the election obtaining 0.57 per cent of the vote. ==Biography== Israel is married to Sara Cofre, has two children (Daniela and Igal), and three grandsons (Vicente, Julian, and Dalia). He completed his university studies in 1981 at the Law Faculty of the University of Chile, becoming a lawyer.
