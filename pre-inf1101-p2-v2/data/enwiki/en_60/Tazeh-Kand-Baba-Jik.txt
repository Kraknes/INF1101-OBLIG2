Tazeh Kand (, also Romanized as Tazeh Kand; also known as Mokhtar Kandi, Qarah Tulki-ye Tazehkand, and Qareh Tulki) is a village in Baba Jik Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 20, in 6 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
