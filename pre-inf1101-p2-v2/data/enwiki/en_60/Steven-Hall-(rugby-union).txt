Steven Hall (born 17 September 1972, in Johannesburg) is a former French rugby union player. He played as a lock and occasionally number eight. He played for AS Beziers where he won Rugby Pro D2 in 2000. He earned his first cap with the French national team on 2 February 2002 against Italy at Stade de France.
