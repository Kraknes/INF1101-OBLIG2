Qinar-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Qinar-e Sofla; also known as Qiz-e Pa'in) is a village in Sokmanabad Rural District, Safayyeh District, Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 360, in 57 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Khoy County
