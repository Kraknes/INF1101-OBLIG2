Shurjeh-ye Hoseynabad (, also romanized as Shurjeh-ye Hoseynabad; also known as Hoseynabad) is a village in Bastam Rural District, in the Central District of Chaypareh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaypareh County
