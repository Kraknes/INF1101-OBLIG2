Tazeh Kand-e Jabal (, also Romanized as Tazeh Kand-e Jabal; also known as Tazeh Kand-e Bolbol) is a village in Almahdi Rural District, Mohammadyar District, Naqadeh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 377, in 90 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Naqadeh County
