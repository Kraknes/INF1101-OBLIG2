Soleyman Qeshlaq (, also Romanized as Soleyman Qeshlaq; also known as Sulaiman Qishlaq and Suleyman-Kishlak) is a village in Kaghazkonan-e Markazi Rural District, Kaghazkonan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 30, in 6 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
