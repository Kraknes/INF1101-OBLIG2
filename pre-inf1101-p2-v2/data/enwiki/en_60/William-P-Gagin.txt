William P. Gagin (November 6, 1916 in Illinois - July 18, 1998), was a member of the Wisconsin State Assembly. He was in the wholesale drugs and tool and dye business. He was married with three children.Social Security Death Index ==Career== Gagin was first elected to the Assembly in 1978.
