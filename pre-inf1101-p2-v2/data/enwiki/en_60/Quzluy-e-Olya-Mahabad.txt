Quzluy-e Olya (, also Romanized as Quzluy-e 'Olya; also known as Kozlu-giaria, Qowzlu-ye Bala, Qowzlu-ye 'Olya, Qozlu, and Quzlu) is a village in Kani Bazar Rural District, Khalifan District, Mahabad County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 402, in 68 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Mahabad County
