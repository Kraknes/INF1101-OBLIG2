Tombi Bates (nee Bell; born April 19, 1979), is an U.S. Virgin Islands former professional basketball player, who last played for the Minnesota Lynx of the WNBA. ==Basketball career== ===High school=== Bates played for Miami Norland Senior High School, and during her tenure there, was one of the top players in her county. ===College=== Bates played for the Florida Gators from 1997 to 2001.
