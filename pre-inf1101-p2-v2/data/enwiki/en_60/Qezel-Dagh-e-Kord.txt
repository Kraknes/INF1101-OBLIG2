Qezel Dagh-e Kord (, also Romanized as Qezel Dagh-e Kord and Qezeldagh-e Kord) is a village in Chaybasar-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Maku County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 838, in 141 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Maku County
