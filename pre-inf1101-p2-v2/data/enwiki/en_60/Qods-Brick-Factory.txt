Qods Brick Factory ( - Karkhaneh Ajormashini-ye Qods va Shahrak-e San') is a village and company town in Rahal Rural District, in the Central District of Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 9, in 7 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Khoy County Category:Company towns in Iran
