Sichan Bolaghi (, also Romanized as Sichan Bolaghi; also known as Sechan Bolaghi and Sichan Bolagh) is a village in Akhtachi-ye Gharbi Rural District, in the Central District of Mahabad County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 53, in 10 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Mahabad County
