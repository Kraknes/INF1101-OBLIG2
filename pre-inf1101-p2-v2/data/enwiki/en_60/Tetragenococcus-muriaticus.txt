Tetragenococcus muriaticus is a species of moderately halophilic lactic acid, histamine-producing bacteria. X-1 (= JCM 10006) is the type strain of this species. ==See also== *Tetragenococcus halophilus ==References== ==Further reading== *Whitman, William B., et al., eds.
