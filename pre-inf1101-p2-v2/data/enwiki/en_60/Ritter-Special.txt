{| |} The Riter Special is an American Homebuilt aircraft design. ==Design and development== The Riter special is a single engine, low wing, single seat, retractable conventional landing gear aircraft. The wings fold upward for ground transport on a trailer. The landing gear is retracted by hand with a worm-gear mechanism. ==Specifications (Riter Special) == ==See also== ==References== Category:Homebuilt aircraft
