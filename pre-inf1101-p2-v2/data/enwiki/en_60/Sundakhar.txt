Sundakhar (, also Romanized as Sundakhar; also known as Sindakhir, Sindakhyr, Sondakhvor, Sondeh Khor, Sowndeh Khvor, Sund Akhowr, Sundakhur, and Sundeh Khur) is a village in Dizmar-e Markazi Rural District, Kharvana District, Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 107, in 24 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
