Turkmenbashi railway station () is the main railway station in Turkmenbashi, Turkmenistan. It was built in 1895. The station is operated by the state owned company Turkmendemiryollary. == History == Depot Station Turkmenbashi in the Moorish style was built in the years 1895-96. Architect - Alexei Leontievitch Benois, who has worked in Krasnovodsk. The station building - one of the most beautiful in the whole of Central Asia rail. The architecture of the station, there are many elements of the palace of the Emir of Bukhara.
