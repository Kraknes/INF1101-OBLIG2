Rob Dumas (born March 19, 1969) is a Canadian retired professional roller hockey defenceman. He played 71 games in Roller Hockey International, and scored 30 goals, and 64 assists with the Vancouver Voodoo, and Orlando Jackals. Dumas also had a 13 season minor league career, and was drafted in the 9th round (180th overall) of the 1987 NHL Entry Draft by the St. Louis Blues.
