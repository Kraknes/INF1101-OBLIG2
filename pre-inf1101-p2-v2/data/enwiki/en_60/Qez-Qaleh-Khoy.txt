Qez Qaleh (, also Romanized as Qez Qal'eh; also known as Ghez Ghal'eh and Qiz Qal'eh) is a village in Dizaj Rural District, in the Central District of Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 839, in 194 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Khoy County
