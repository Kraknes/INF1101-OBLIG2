Yekaterina Ananina (Ekaterina Anan'ina ; born 13 June 1991 in Yekaterinburg, Russian SSR, Soviet Union) is a Russian ice hockey forward. ==International career== Ananina was selected for the Russia national women's ice hockey team in the 2010 Winter Olympics. She played in all five games, but did not record a point.IIHF - Team Russia Stats - 2010 Olympics Ananina has also appeared for Russia at three IIHF Women's World Championships. Her first appearance came in 2009.
