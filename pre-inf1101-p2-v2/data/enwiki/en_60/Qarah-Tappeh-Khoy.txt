Qarah Tappeh (, also Romanized as Qareh Tappeh; also known as Qara Tappeh, Qara Tepe, and Rusta-ye Qarah Tappeh) is a village in Rahal Rural District, in the Central District of Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 422, in 93 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Khoy County
