Shadluy-e Olya (, also Romanized as Shadluy-e 'Olya; also known as Qorban Kandi and Shadlu-ye 'Olya) is a village in Avajiq-e Jonubi Rural District, Dashtaki District, Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 204, in 40 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
