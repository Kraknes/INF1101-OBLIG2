Piramides station is a Madrid Metro station in Madrid city centre. It was opened on 5 June 1968 and is one of the oldest stations on Line 5of the Madrid Metro. It gives access to the Vicente Calderon Stadium, home to the Atletico Madrid football club, and is located in fare Zone A.
