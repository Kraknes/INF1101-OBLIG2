Rabotbreen is a glacier in Sabine Land at Spitsbergen, Svalbard. The glacier is a tributary glacier to Fimbulisen, and is located in the upper part of Sassendalen. Nearby mountains are Bairdfjellet and Moskusryggen. ==Name== The glacier is named after French geographer and Arctic explorer Charles Rabot. ==References== Category:Glaciers of Spitsbergen
