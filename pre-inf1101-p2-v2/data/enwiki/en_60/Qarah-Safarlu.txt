Qarah Safarlu (, also Romanized as Qarah Safarlu; also known as Dul Baghi) is a village in Kaghazkonan-e Shomali Rural District, Kaghazkonan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 6, in 6 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
