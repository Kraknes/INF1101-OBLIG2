Zaviyeh-ye Olya (, also Romanized as Zaviyeh-ye 'Olya, Zavieh-ye 'Olya, and Zaviyeh Olya; also known as Zavieh, Zavieh Yukari, and Zaviyeh-ye Bala) is a village in Chaldoran-e Shomali Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 143, in 33 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
