Petra Babiakova (born 27 July 1977 in Zvolen, Czechoslovakia) is a Slovakian ice hockey defender. ==International career== Babiakova was selected for the Slovakia national women's ice hockey team in the 2010 Winter Olympics. She played the second most minutes on her team, but did not record a point.IIHF - Team Slovakia Stats - 2010 Olympics She played in the 2010 Olympic qualifying campaign.IIHF - Team Slovakia Stats - 2010 Olympic Qualifier Babiakova also appeared for Slovakia at nine IIHF Women's World Championships, across three levels.
