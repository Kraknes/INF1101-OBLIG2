Satelmish-e Mohammadlu (, also Romanized as Satelmish-e Mohammadlu) is a village in Zarrineh Rud Rural District, in the Central District of Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,564, in 345 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
