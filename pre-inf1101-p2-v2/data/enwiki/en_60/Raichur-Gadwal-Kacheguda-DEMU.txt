Raichur-Gadwal-Kacheguda DEMU is a train between Raichur in Karnataka and Kacheguda in Andhra Pradesh established in October 2013. The train numbers are 77690/94. It runs between Raichur and Kacheguda via Gadwal, Mahbubnagar and Shamshabad. The train started after a line between Gadwal and Raichur was laid in October 2013.
