Yegor Sergeyevich Omelyanenko (; born 10 July 1993) is a Russian former professional ice hockey player. He played for Amur Khabarovsk of the Kontinental Hockey League (KHL) and Podhale Nowy Targ of the Polska Hokej Liga.
