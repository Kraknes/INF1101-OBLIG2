Qeshlaq-e Khurasha-ye Sofla (, also Romanized as Qeshlaq-e Khurasha-ye Sofla; also known as Qeshlaq-e Khurasha) is a village in Dizmar-e Markazi Rural District, Kharvana District, Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 28, in 6 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
