Nikos Kalles (Greek: Nikos Kalles; born April 20, 1987) is a Greek professional basketball player. He is a 6 ft 11 in (2.11 m) tall center. ==Professional career== Kalles started his pro career in 2006, with Iraklis. He moved to PAOK in 2009. He stayed at the club until 2014. In 2014, he signed with Panelefsiniakos.Panelefsiniakos signed Nikos Kalles. On October 23, 2015, Kalles signed with Aries Trikala, of the Greek Basket League.Trikala signed Nikos Kalles.
