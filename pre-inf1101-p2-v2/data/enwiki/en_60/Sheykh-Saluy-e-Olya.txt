Sheykh Saluy-e Olya (, also Romanized as Sheykh Saluy-e 'Olya and Sheikh Salooy Olya; also known as Shaikh Silu Yukari, Sheykh Salu-ye Bala, Sheykh Salu-ye 'Olya, Sheykh Selu-ye Bala, and Sheykh Solu-ye Bala) is a village in Chaldoran-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 117, in 30 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
