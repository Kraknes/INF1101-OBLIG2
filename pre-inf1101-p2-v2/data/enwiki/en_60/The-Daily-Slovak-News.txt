The Daily Slovak News (also known as TheDaily SK) is an English online newspaper headquartered in Bratislava, Slovakia. ==Profile== The Daily covers current affairs, politics, business, legislation, foreign affairs, sport and culture. The editor-in-chief of the daily is John Boyd and Tony Papaleo is his deputy.
