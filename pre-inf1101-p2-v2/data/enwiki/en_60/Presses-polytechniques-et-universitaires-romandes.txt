The Presses polytechniques et universitaires romandes (PPUR, literally "Polytechnic and university press of French-speaking Switzerland") is a Swiss academic publishing house. It is based in Lausanne, on the Lausanne campus, in the Rolex Learning Center.http://www.ppur.org (page visited on 11 October 2013). The Presses polytechniques et universitaires romandes has an English- language imprint called EPFL Press. == Publications == The Presses polytechniques et universitaires romandes publish << Le savoir suisse >> (literally "The Swiss knowledge"). This series was created in 2002 in collaboration with Bertil Galland. Between 2002 and 2012, it edited 88 books and sold 150'000 copies (in French).Nicolas Dufour, "La collection << Le Savoir suisse >> vise d'autres terres", Le Temps, Thursday 1 November 2012. 28 of these books were translated, mainly in German. == Notes and references == == External links == * Official website * Category:University presses of Switzerland Category:Publishing companies established in 1980 Category:Swiss companies established in 1980
