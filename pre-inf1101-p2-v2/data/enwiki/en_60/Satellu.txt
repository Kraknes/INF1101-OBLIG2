Satellu (, also Romanized as Satellu and Satellu; also known as Saltu, Satil, Satloo, Satlu, and Satyn) is a village in Tazeh Kand Rural District, Khosrowshahr District, Tabriz County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,365, in 247 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Tabriz County
