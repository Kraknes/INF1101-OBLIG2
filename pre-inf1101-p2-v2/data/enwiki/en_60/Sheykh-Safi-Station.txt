Sheykh Safi Station ( - Istgah-e Sheykh Safi and Istgah-e Sheykh Safi) is a village and railway station in Owch Tappeh-ye Gharbi Rural District, Torkamanchay District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 23, in 4 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County Category:Railway stations in Iran
