Ole Martin Rindaroy (born 16 May 1995) is a Norwegian football defender who currently plays for Sogndal. ==Career== ===Club=== Rindaroy hails from Aukra where he started his youth career in Gossen. When his older brother Knut Olav Rindaroy was signed by Molde FK, the family moved to Molde. Ole Martin Rindaroy joined the same club and made his first-team debut in the 2011 Norwegian Football Cup against Tiller IL. On 8 February 2016, Rindaroy was loaned to Lillestrom for the 2016 season.
