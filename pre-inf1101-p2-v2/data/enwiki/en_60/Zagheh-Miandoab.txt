Zagheh (, also Romanized as Zagheh; also known as Zagheh-ye Hoseynabad) is a village in Ajorluy-ye Sharqi Rural District, Baruq District, Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 315, in 64 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
