Yal Qeshlaqi (, also Romanized as Yal Qeshlaqi; also known as Yar Qeshlaqi) is a village in Garmeh-ye Shomali Rural District, Kandovan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 57, in 13 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
