Pseudoxanthomonas japonensis is a species of mesophilic, motile, strictly aerobic, Gram-negative, non-spore-forming, rod-shaped bacteria with one polar flagellum, first isolated from human urine, riverside urban soil and anaerobic digester. 12-3T (=CCUG 48231T =CIP 107388T =JCM 11525T) is the type strain. ==References== ==Further reading== *Whitman, William B., et al., eds.
