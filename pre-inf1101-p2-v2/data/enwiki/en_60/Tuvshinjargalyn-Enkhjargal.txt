Tuvshinjargalyn Enkhjargal (; born 14 June 1992) is a Mongolian racing cyclist and triathlon athlete. In road cycling she won the individual time trial at the 2013 Asian Cycling Championships and finished 6th at the 2014 Asian Games. She competed in the 2013 UCI World Championships women's road race in Florence.
