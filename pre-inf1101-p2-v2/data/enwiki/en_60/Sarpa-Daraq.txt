Sarpa Daraq (, also Romanized as Sarpa Daraq; also known as Sarpa-Dara, Sarpa Dareh, Sarpa Darreh, Sarpeh Daraq, and Subeh Daraq) is a village in Bakrabad Rural District, in the Central District of Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 41, in 11 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
