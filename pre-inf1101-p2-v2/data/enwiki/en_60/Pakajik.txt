Pakajik (, also Romanized as Pakajik; also known as Bia Jik, Pagajek, Paga Jik, Parch, Parchi, Payadzhik, Paya Jik, Pega Jik, Pikachik, and Yakachik) is a village in Gowharan Rural District, in the Central District of Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,216, in 298 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Khoy County
