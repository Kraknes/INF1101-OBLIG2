Shabiluy-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Shabiluy-e Sofla; also known as Shabilu- ye Pa'in and Shabilu-ye Sofla) is a village in Zarrineh Rud-e Shomali Rural District, in the Central District of Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,635, in 398 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
