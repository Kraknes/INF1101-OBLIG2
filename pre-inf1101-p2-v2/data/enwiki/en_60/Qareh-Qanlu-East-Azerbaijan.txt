Qareh Qanlu (, also Romanized as Qareh Qanlu; also known as Karaganlu, Qaraghanlu, Qarah Qatlu, and Qaraqanlu) is a village in Kaghazkonan-e Markazi Rural District, Kaghazkonan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 94, in 23 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
