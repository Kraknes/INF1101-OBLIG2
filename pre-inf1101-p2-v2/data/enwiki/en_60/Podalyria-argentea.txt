Podalyria argentea Salisb. is a species of flowering plant in the legume family (Fabaceae or Leguminosae), native to the Cape Province of South Africa. It should not be confused with Podalyria argentea Willd., which is a synonym for a quite different species, Ammodendron bifolium, from Russia and Xinjiang, China. ==References== Category:Podalyrieae Category:Endemic flora of South Africa Category:Flora of the Cape Provinces Category:Fynbos Category:Plants described in 1806
