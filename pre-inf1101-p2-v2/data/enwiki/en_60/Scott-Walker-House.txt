The Scott-Walker House is a historic home located near Saltville, Smyth County, Virginia. It was built about 1800, and is a two-story, three bay, limestone dwelling with a hall-parlor-plan on each floor. It has a side gable roof and exterior end chimneys. A one-story, three room wing was added in 1992 and garage in 1993.
