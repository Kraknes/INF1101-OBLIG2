Qurul-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Qurul-e Sofla; also known as Qurul-e Pa'in) is a village in Bastam Rural District, in the Central District of Chaypareh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 217, in 51 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaypareh County
