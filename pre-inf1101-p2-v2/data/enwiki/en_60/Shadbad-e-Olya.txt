Shadbad-e Olya (, also Romanized as Shadbad-e 'Olya; also known as Shadabad-e Bala and Shadabad-e 'Olya) is a village in Meydan Chay Rural District, in the Central District of Tabriz County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,884, in 508 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Tabriz County
