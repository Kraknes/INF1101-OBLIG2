Sadd-e Shahid Kazemi (, also Romanized as Sadd-e Shahid Kazemi) is a village in Behi-e Feyzolah Beygi Rural District, in the Central District of Bukan County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported. == References == Category:Populated places in Bukan County
