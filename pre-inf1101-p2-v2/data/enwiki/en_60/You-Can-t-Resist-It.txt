"You Can't Resist It" is a song recorded written and originally recorded by Lyle Lovett on his 1986 self-titled debut album. It was included on the soundtrack to the 1991 film Switch, from which it was released as a single. At the 1992 Grammy Awards, it was nominated for the Grammy Award for Best Song Written for Visual Media.
