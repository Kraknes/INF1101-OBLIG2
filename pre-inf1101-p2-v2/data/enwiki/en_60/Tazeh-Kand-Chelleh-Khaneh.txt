Tazeh Kand (, also Romanized as Tazeh Kand; also known as Mahmudabad and Taza- kend) is a village in Chelleh Khaneh Rural District, Sufian District, Shabestar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 973, in 229 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Shabestar County
