Yarem Qayah-e Sofla (, also Romanized as 'Yarem Qayah-e Sofla, Yarem Qayyah-e Sofla, and Yarem Qiyeh-ye Sofla; also known as Yarim Ghiyeh Sofla and Yarim Qiyeh-ye Sofla) is a village in Qaleh Darrehsi Rural District, in the Central District of Maku County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 573, in 129 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Maku County
