Pseudoxanthomonas broegbernensis is a yellow-pigmented bacteria the type species of its genus. Its type strain is B1616/1T (= DSM 12573T). ==References== ==Further reading== *Whitman, William B., et al., eds. Bergey's manual(r) of systematic bacteriology. Vol. 2.
