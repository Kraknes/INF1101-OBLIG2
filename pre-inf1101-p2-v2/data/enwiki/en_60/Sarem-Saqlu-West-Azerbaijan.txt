Sarem Saqlu (, also Romanized as Sarem Saqlu; also known as Qahreman Kandi and Sarem Sakhlu) is a village in Avajiq-e Shomali Rural District, Dashtaki District, Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 85, in 18 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
