Stephen Gough is a Canadian politician, who was elected to the Nova Scotia House of Assembly in the 2013 provincial election. A member of the Nova Scotia Liberal Party, he represents the electoral district of Sackville-Beaver Bank."Nova Scotia votes: Riding-by-riding results for Halifax region".
