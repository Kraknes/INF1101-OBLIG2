Reinis Kaudzite (Old orthography: Reinis Kaudsit; May 12, 1839 - August 21, 1920), was a Latvian schoolteacher and writer. His novel Mernieku laiki ("Time of the Surveyors") was the first novel written in Latvian language, co- authored with his brother Matiss Kaudzite in 1879. Reinis Kaudzite was born in "Madari", Vecpiebalga Parish, in the heart of Vidzeme.
