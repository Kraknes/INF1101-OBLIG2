Qarah Hajji-ye Sofla (, also Romanized as Qarah Hajji-ye Sofla; also known as Qarah Hajjilu-ye Pa'in and Qarah Hajjilu-ye Sofla) is a village in Kandovan Rural District, Kandovan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 45, in 8 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
