Panj Tan-e Shahid (, also Romanized as Panj Tan-e Shahid; also known as Mohammad Shah-e Sofla) is a village in Beygom Qaleh Rural District, in the Central District of Naqadeh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 176, in 42 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Naqadeh County
