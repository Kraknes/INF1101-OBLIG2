Zaviyeh-ye Sofla (, also Romanized as Zaviyeh-ye Sofla and Zaviyeh Sofla; also known as Zaveyeh, Zavieh, Zaviyeh, and Zaviyeh-ye Pa'in) is a village in Chaldoran-e Shomali Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 401, in 99 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
