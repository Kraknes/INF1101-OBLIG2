Stefanie Eva Wyss (born 19 October 1985 in Bern, Switzerland) is a Swiss ice hockey defender. ==International career== Wyss was selected for the Switzerland national women's ice hockey team in the 2010 Winter Olympics. She played in all five games, but did not record a point.IIHF - Team Switzerland Stats - 2010 Olympics Wyss has also appeared for Switzerland at three IIHF Women's World Championships.
