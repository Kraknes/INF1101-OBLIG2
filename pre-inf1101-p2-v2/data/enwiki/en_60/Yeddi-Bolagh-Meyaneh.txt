Yeddi Bolagh (, also Romanized as Yeddi Bolagh; also known as Yeddeh Bolagh) is a village in Kolah Boz-e Sharqi Rural District, in the Central District of Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 154, in 26 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
