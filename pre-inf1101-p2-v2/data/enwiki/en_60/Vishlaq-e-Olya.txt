Vishlaq-e Olya (, also Romanized as Vishlaq-e 'Olya and Vishlaq 'Olya; also known as Vaslar Yukari, Vishlagh Olya, Vishlaq Bala, Vishlaq-e Bala, and Yukhari-Veshlya) is a village in Valdian Rural District, Ivughli District, Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 784, in 225 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Khoy County
