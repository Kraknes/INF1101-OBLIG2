Quch Ghar (, also Romanized as Quch Ghar and Quchghar; also known as Koshkar, Qareh Bolagh, Qoshkar, and Quchqar) is a village in Kaghazkonan-e Shomali Rural District, Kaghazkonan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 79, in 32 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
