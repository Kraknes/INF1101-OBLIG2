Sheykhlar (; also known as Ishihlar, Ishikhlar, Mashayekh) is a village in Kaghazkonan-e Markazi Rural District, Kaghazkonan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 102, in 36 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
