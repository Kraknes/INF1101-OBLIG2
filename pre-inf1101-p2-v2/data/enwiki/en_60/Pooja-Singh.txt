Pooja Singh is an Indian actress who acts in television soap operas. She is best known for her role as Emily in the StarPlus serial Diya Aur Baati Hum and Forum Bhanushali in Dil Se Dil Tak. From 2018 to present, she is portraying Raavi in Colors TV's Shakti - Astitva Ke Ehsaas Ki. ==Personal life== Pooja Singh was born in Khatri family. Palamau district of Jharkhand on 1st Feb 1991 and did her schooling at Rabindranath Tagore School. During her school days she was actively involved in extra curricular activities like dancing, performing plays and dramas. She is an ardent animal lover.
