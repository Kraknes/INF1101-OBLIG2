Sowgoli Tappeh (, also Romanized as Sowgoli Tappeh) is a village in Zarrineh Rud-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 2,945, in 745 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
