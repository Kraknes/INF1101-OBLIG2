Sara Mustonen (born 8 February 1981) is a Swedish racing cyclist, who currently rides for UCI Women's Continental Team . Prior to becoming a cyclist she was a boxer. She took up cycle racing after working as a bicycle courier. She competed in the women's road race at the 2008 Summer Olympics and the 2013 UCI women's road race in Florence.
