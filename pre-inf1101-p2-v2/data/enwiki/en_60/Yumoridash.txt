Yumoridash (, also Romanized as Yumoridash, Yumori Dash, Yumri Dash, Yoomeridash, and Yumeri Dash; also known as Yumeru Dasht, Yumuridash, and Yumury Dasht) is a village in Baba Jik Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. According to the 2006 census, its population was 264 individuals among 48 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
