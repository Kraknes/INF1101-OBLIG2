Sanyan-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Sanyan-e Sofla; also known as Sanyan-e Pa'in) is a village in Barvanan-e Gharbi Rural District, Torkamanchay District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 494, in 100 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
