Rubropsichia is a genus of moths belonging to the family Tortricidae. ==Species== *Rubropsichia brasiliana Razowski, 2009 *Rubropsichia fuesliniana (Stoll, in Cramer, 1781) *Rubropsichia kartaboana Razowski, 2011 *Rubropsichia santaremana Razowski, 2009 ==Etymology== The name refers to the second part of the name Mictopsichia, and the main colour of the moth. It is derived from Latin ruber (meaning red). ==See also== *List of Tortricidae genera ==References== * 2009: Revision of Mictopsichia Hubner with descriptions of new species and two new genera (Lepidoptera: Tortricidae).
