Qeshlaq (, also Romanized as Qeshlaq; also known as Qeshlaq-e Feruraq and Qeshlaq-e Taqi Beyg) is a village in Firuraq Rural District, in the Central District of Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,287, in 302 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Khoy County
