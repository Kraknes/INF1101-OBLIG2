The Prime Minister's Office is the principal workplace of the Prime Minister of Pakistan and is headed by the Principal Secretary to the Prime Minister of Pakistan, currently Azam Khan. It is responsible for formulating policies for the Prime Minister's cabinet, conducting its cabinet meetings and supervising and overseeing the implementation of the Cabinet's policy. In addition, it is in charge of other governmental bodies, which report directly to the Prime Minister.
