Shuraq Gol (, also Romanized as Shuraq Gol; also known as Shuraghol) is a village in Chaybasar-e Shomali Rural District, Bazargan District, Maku County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 167, in 31 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Maku County
