The building on Kobmagergade Varehuset Messen was a department store on Kobmagergade in Copenhagen, Denmark, operated by Hilligsoe, Koedt & Co.. ==History== Messen in Kobmagergade The building was completed in 1895 to designs by Emil Blichfeldt. ==Architecture== The five-storey building consists of nine bays on Kobmagergade and 10 bays on Klareboderne. The building is topped by a Hermes statue by Julius Schultz. ==Today== It is now used by Niels Brock Copenhagen Business College whose main building is on Kultorvet. ==References== Category:Retail buildings in Copenhagen Category:Buildings and structures completed in 1895
