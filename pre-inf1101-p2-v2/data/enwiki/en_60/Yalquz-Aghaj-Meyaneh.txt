Yalquz Aghaj (, also Romanized as Yalquz Aghaj; also known as Baltuz Aghaj, Lal Kuzacach, Lalquzacach, and Yalquz Aqaj) is a village in Barvanan-e Gharbi Rural District, Torkamanchay District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 255, in 79 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
