Tazeh Kand-e Bekrabad (, also Romanized as Tazeh Kand-e Bekrabad; also known as Taza-Kyand and Tazeh Kand) is a village in Ozomdel-e Shomali Rural District, in the Central District of Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 124, in 26 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
