Mulberry Grove is a historic home located near Cornwall, Rockbridge County, Virginia. It was built in 1796, and is a two-story, three bay, stone I-house dwelling. It has a side gable roof, exterior end chimneys, and a bold cornice decorated with modillions and dentils. A two-story frame addition and one- story porch were added about 1900.
