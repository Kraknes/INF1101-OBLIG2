Shah Ali Beyglu (, also Romanized as Shah 'Ali Beyglu; also known as Shalveli) is a village in Kaghazkonan-e Shomali Rural District, Kaghazkonan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 65, in 29 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
