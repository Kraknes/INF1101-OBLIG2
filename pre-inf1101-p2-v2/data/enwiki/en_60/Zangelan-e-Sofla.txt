Zangelan-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Zangelan-e Sofla; also known as Zangalan Pa'in and Zangelan-e Pa'in) is a village in Hajjilar-e Jonubi Rural District, Hajjilar District, Chaypareh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 734, in 167 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaypareh County
