Orencik is a village in the District of Kazan, Ankara Province, Turkey. To differentiate it from the other nearby Orencik village in the District of Kizilcahamam, people in the area call Orencik "Leblebi Orencik" for its production of leblebis, or roasted chickpeas, while calling the other village "Cakmak Orencik." ==References== Category:Villages in Kahramankazan District
