Zabarlu (, also Romanized as Zabarlu, Zabarloo, Zaberlu, and Zebarlu; also known as Zabazlu, Zaberlyu, and Zabarli) is a village in Rudqat Rural District, Sufian District, Shabestar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 74, in 18 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Shabestar County
