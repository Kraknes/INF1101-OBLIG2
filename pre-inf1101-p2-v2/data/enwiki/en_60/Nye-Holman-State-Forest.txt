Nye-Holman State Forest is a Connecticut state forest located in the town of Tolland. The Willimantic River flows through the forest. ==Recreation== The portion of the Willimantic River that flows through the forest is a trout management area restricted to catch and release fly-fishing, the Cole W. Wilde Trout Management Area.
