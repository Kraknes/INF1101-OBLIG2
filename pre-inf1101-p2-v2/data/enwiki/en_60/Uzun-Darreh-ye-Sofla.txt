Uzun Darreh-ye Sofla (, also Romanized as Uzun Darreh-ye Sofla and Oozoon Darreh Sofla; also known as Uzun Darreh-ye Pa'in) is a village in Akhtachi-ye Gharbi Rural District, in the Central District of Mahabad County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 260, in 41 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Mahabad County
