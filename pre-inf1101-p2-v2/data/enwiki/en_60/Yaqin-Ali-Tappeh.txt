Yaqin Ali Tappeh (, also Romanized as Yaqin 'Ali Tappeh; also known as Yaghlan Tappeh and Yaghn'ali Tappeh) is a village in Marhemetabad Rural District, in the Central District of Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,816, in 467 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
