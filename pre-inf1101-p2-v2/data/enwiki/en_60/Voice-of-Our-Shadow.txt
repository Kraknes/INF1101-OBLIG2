Voice of Our Shadow is a novel written by Jonathan Carroll. It won the Washington Post Book of the Year in 1983. The book is about a young writer, Joe Lennox, taking refuge in Vienna after his brother's death. There he became the friend of a married couple and fell in love with the wife.
