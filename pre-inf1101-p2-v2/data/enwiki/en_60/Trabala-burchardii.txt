Trabala burchardii is a moth of the family Lasiocampidae. The species was first described by Hermann Dewitz in 1881. It is found in Angola, Cameroon, Equatorial Guinea, Kenya, Nigeria and Ghana. ==References== *Dewitz, 1881. Afrikanische Nachtschmetterlinge.
