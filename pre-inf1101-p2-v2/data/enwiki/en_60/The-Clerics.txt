The Clerics () is a 2013 Indonesian drama film directed by Rako Prijanto. The film was selected as the Indonesian entry for the Best Foreign Language Film at the 86th Academy Awards, but it was not nominated. ==Plot== Sang Kiai follows the Muslim Cleric Hasyim through the Japanese Occupation of Indonesia and the Indonesian National Revolution. ==Cast== * Ikranagara as KH Hasyim Asy'ari * Christine Hakim as Nyai Kapu * Agus Kuncoro as KH Wahid Hasyim * Adipati Koesmadji as Harun ==See also== * List of submissions to the 86th Academy Awards for Best Foreign Language Film * List of Indonesian submissions for the Academy Award for Best Foreign Language Film ==References== ==External links== * Category:2013 films Category:2013 drama films Category:Indonesian films Category:Indonesian-language films Category:Maya Award winners Category:Indonesian drama films
