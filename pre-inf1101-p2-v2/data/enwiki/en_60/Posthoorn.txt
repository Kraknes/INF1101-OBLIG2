__NOTOC__ Posthoorn is the restaurant of Suitehotel De Posthoorn in Monnickendam, Netherlands. It is a fine dining restaurant that was awarded one Michelin star for the period 2009-present. GaultMillau awarded the restaurant 15 out of 20 points. Knoopjelos - Posthoorn Head chef of Posthoorn is Jeroen Bavelaar. Knoopjelos - Posthoorn He took over from Rogier van Dam, who left the restaurant in 2010. Hotel De Posthoorn, with its new restaurant, reopened in 2005. The original hotel started as a stable and inn in 1888.
