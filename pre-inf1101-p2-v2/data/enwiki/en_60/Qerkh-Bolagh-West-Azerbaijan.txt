Qerkh Bolagh (, also Romanized as Qerkh Bolagh and Qarakh Bolagh; also known as Gharakhbolagh, Kirkh Bulak, Kirkh Bulaq, and Qikh Bolagh) is a village in Baba Jik Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 231, in 52 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
