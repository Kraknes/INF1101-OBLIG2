Wisconsin Dells High School is a high school in Wisconsin Dells, Wisconsin. The only high school in the Wisconsin Dells School District, it serves the municipalities of Wisconsin Dells, Dell Prairie, Dellona, Delton, Jackson, Lake Delton, Lyndon, New Haven, Newport, and Springville."About Us."
