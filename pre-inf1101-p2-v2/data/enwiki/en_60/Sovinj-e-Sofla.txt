Sovinj-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Sovinj-e Sofla; also known as Sovich-e Pa'in) is a village in Barvanan-e Gharbi Rural District, Torkamanchay District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 331, in 72 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
