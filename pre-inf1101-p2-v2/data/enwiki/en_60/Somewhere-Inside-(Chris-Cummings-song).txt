"Somewhere Inside" is a song recorded by Canadian country music artist Chris Cummings. It was released in 1996 as the third single from his debut album, Somewhere Inside. It peaked at number 4 on the RPM Country Tracks chart in January 1997. ==Content== The narrator comes across a box of items that were left behind from a former lover.
