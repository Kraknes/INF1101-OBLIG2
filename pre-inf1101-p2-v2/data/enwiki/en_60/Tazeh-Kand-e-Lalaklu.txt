Tazeh Kand-e Lalaklu (, also Romanized as Tazeh Kand-e Lalaklu) is a village in Marhemetabad-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 518, in 114 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
