Qermezi Bolagh (, also Romanized as Qermezi Bolagh; also known as Qermez Bolagh) is a village in Baruq Rural District, Baruq District, Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 179, in 35 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
