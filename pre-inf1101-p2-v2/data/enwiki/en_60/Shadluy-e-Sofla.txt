Shadluy-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Shadluy-e Sofla; also known as Shadlu-ye Sofla) is a village in Avajiq-e Jonubi Rural District, Dashtaki District, Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 374, in 77 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
