Qepchaq (, also Romanized as Qepchaq; also known as Qebjan) is a village in Marhemetabad-e Shomali Rural District, Marhemetabad District, Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 3,183, in 803 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
