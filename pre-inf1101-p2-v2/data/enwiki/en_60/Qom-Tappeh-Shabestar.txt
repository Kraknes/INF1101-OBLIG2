Qom Tappeh (; also known as Ghom Tppaeh, Kum Tapa, Kum Tepe, Oom Tappeh, Qum Tapeh, and Qum Tappeh) is a village in Chelleh Khaneh Rural District, Sufian District, Shabestar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 622, in 153 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Shabestar County
