Qezel Dagh-e Ajam (, also Romanized as Qezel Dagh-e 'Ajam and Qezeldagh-e 'Ajam) is a village in Chaybasar-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Maku County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 359, in 79 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Maku County
