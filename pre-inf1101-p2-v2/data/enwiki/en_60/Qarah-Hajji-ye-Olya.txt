Qarah Hajji-ye Olya (, also Romanized as Qarah Hajji-ye 'Olya; also known as Qarah Hajjilu-ye Bala and Qarah Hajjilu-ye 'Olya) is a village in Kandovan Rural District, Kandovan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 60, in 10 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
