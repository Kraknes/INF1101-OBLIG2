Otto A. Vogel was a member of the Wisconsin State Assembly. ==Biography== Vogel was born on October 10, 1886 in Newton, Manitowoc County, Wisconsin. He died on September 19, 1951 and is buried in Manitowoc, Wisconsin. ==Career== Vogel was a member of the Assembly three times. First, from 1935 to 1936, second, from 1939 to 1940 and third, from 1943 to 1948. Additionally, he was a justice of the peace from 1912 to 1918 and a Manitowoc alderman from 1918 to 1930 and again from 1932 to 1934.
