Resham Tipnis is an Indian television actress. She did a small role of Anjali Sinha in Shah Rukh Khan starrer film Baazigar in 1992. She acted in many serials notably Campus Serial in 1994. Previously she played the role of Saroj in Do Dil Ek Jaan on Life OK. She played a role as Babita in Satrangi Sasural. She has also done some Marathi films. She participated in the reality show Bigg Boss Marathi 1 in 2018 and was evicted on day 91. ==Personal life== Tipnis married actor Sanjeev Seth in 1993 at the age of 20, but they divorced in 2004. They have two children, Rishika and Manav.
