Shahid Mohammad Rauf (, also Romanized as Shahid Mohammad Ra'uf; also known as Mohammad Shah-e 'Olya) is a village in Beygom Qaleh Rural District, in the Central District of Naqadeh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 410, in 71 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Naqadeh County
