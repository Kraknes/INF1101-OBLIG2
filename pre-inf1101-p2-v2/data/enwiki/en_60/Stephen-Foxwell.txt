Stephen W. Foxwell is a headteacher who has been teaching students for thirty years in schools all over the world including Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Nigeria, The Cayman Islands and in the UK, twelve of which have been spent in Senior Leadership Positions. He was headmaster of the British School of Houston until 18 December 2014.
