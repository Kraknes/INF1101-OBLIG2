Younes Kaabouni (born 23 May 1995) is a French professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for FC Sochaux-Montbeliard. ==Career== Kaabouni made his Ligue 1 debut with Girondins de Bordeaux on 6 October 2013 in 4-1 home win against Sochaux entering the field after 88 minutes for Abdou Traore. On 31 August 2017, Kaabouni and Bordeaux agreed the termination of his contract.
