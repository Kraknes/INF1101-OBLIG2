Nowjeh Deh (; also known as Navabi, Navadeh, Novabi, Novadeh Pir, Nuvabi, and Nuvady) is a village in Tazeh Kand Rural District, Khosrowshahr District, Tabriz County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 2,057, in 372 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Tabriz County
