Salim Saghul (, also Romanized as Salim Saghul; also known as Salim Saghlu and Salimsaghlu) is a village in Mokriyan-e Gharbi Rural District, in the Central District of Mahabad County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 225, in 37 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Mahabad County
