Yengejeh-ye Daliganlu (, also Romanized as Yengejeh-ye Daliganlu; also known as Qeshlaq-e Yengejeh, Yengejeh-ye Dalikanlu, and Yengejeh-ye Qeshlaq) is a village in Garmeh-ye Shomali Rural District, Kandovan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 374, in 73 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
