Reggie Keely (born June 9, 1991) is an American professional basketball player for Kobrat of the Finnish Korisliiga. He played collegiately for the Ohio Bobcats. ==College career== Keely played four seasons for Ohio University, the team he committed to in 2008. In his time with the Bobcats, he scored a total 1,131 points for the team. This made him the fifth all-time scorer for Ohio.
