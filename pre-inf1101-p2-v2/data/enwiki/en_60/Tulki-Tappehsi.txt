Tulki Tappehsi (, also Romanized as Tulki Tappehsi; also known as Tulki) is a village in Chaldoran-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 60, in 13 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
