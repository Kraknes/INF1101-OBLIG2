Pir Mikail (, also Romanized as Pir Mika'il; also known as Pir Meka'il) is a village in the Kani Bazar Rural District, Khalifan District, Mahabad County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. As of the 2006 census, its population was 111, in 17 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Mahabad County
