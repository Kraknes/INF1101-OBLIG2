Stuart M. Sprague is an American nephrologist, is a chief of the Division of Nephrology and Hypertension and a founder of Chronic Kidney Disease Clinic. In 1995 he joined NorthShore University HealthSystem and before that was a director of both the University of Chicago's Renal Bone Program and Hospitals Chronic Hemodialysis Unit.
