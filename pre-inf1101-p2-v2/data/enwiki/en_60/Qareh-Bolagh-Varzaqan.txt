Qareh Bolagh (, also Romanized as Qareh Bolagh; also known as Karabulag, Qara Bulagh, Qarah Bowlagh, Qara Tepe, and Qareh Tappeh) is a village in Ozomdel-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 237, in 53 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
