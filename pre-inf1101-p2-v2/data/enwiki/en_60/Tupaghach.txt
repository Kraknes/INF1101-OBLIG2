Tupaghach (, also Romanized as Tupaghach; also known as Tupaghaj and Tup Aqaj) is a village in Ajorluy-ye Sharqi Rural District, Baruq District, Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
