Varrow, Inc. was a management consulting and technology services company located in Greensboro, North Carolina. ==History== The company was founded in 2008 by Jeremiah Cook and Dan Weiss. ==Awards and recognition== Some awards and recognition of Varrow include: *EMC Velocity Services Quality Award 5 years in a row *Ranked on the INC. 500/5000 4 years in a row *Triad Fast 50 top 5 for 5 years in a row *VMware Business Continuity Competency Partner of the Year - Americas. ==Acquisition== Varrow was purchased by Sirius Computer Solutions May 2015 and ceased to exist as an independent brand. ==References== ==External links== * Varrow website * Acquisition Announcement Category:Information technology companies of the United States Category:Companies based in Greensboro, North Carolina
