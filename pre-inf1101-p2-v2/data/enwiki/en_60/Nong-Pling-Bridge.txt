Nong Pling Bridge is a one-span railway bridge in Thailand. It is in Nakhon Sawan Province on the Northern Line Railway, near Nakhon Sawan Railway Station. There is only one span, with a length of about 40 metres. ==Features== It is a one-span truss bridge. ==References== ==External links== * Photos of the bridge (2008) Category:Railway bridges in Thailand Category:Bridges completed in 1976 Category:Truss bridges
