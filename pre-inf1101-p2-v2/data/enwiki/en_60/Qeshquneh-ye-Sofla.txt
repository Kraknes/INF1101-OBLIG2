Qeshquneh-ye Sofla (, also Romanized as Qeshquneh-ye Sofla; also known as Qeshqeneh-ye Sofla) is a village in Mangur-e Sharqi Rural District, Khalifan District, Mahabad County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 65, in 7 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Mahabad County
