Qurmish (, also Romanized as Qurmish and Qowrmish; also known as Gharmish, Kurmish, and Qormish) is a village in Behi Dehbokri Rural District, Simmineh District, Bukan County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 178, in 35 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Bukan County
