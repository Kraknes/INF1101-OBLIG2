Zel Keh-ye Sofla (, also Romanized as Zel Keh-ye Sofla; also known as Zilakeh-ye Sofla) is a village in Chaybasar-e Shomali Rural District, Bazargan District, Maku County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 235, in 49 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Maku County
