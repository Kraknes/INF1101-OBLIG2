{| |} The Southern Aeronautical Scamp is an American aircraft designed for homebuilt construction and Formula V Air Racing. ==Design and development== The Scamp is a single place, mid-wing aircraft with conventional landing gear. The fuselage is constructed with steel tubing and covered with fabric.
