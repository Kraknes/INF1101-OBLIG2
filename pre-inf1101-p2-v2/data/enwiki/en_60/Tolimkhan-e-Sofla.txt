Tolimkhan-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Tolimkhan-e Sofla; also known as Tazeh Kand-e Towlim Khan and Towlim Khan-e Pa'in) is a village in Chaybasar-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Maku County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 97 in 20 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Maku County
