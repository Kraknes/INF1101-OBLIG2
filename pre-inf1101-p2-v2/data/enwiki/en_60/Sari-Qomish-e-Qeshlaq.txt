Sari Qomish-e Qeshlaq (, also Romanized as Sari Qomish-e Qeshlaq; also known as Sari Qomish) is a village in Behi-e Feyzolah Beygi Rural District, in the Central District of Bukan County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 463, in 77 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Bukan County
