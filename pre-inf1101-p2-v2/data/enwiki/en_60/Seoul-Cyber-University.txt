Seoul Cyber University (SCU) (Korean: seoulsaibeodaehaggyo) is a Korean online university.Korean Online University SCU is located in Seoul, South Korea, where it has approximately 11,000 enrolled students and 100 faculty members among 9 schools. It has 19 departments.http://www.academyinfo.kr/UIPISA/uipnh/unt/ipsrch/UntUnvAcdtSrchPupInr.do?schlId=0000323&svyYr;=2012&schlDivCd;=02 It was selected as Korea's 'Best Cyber University' by the Ministry of Education, Science, and Technology in 2007 http://terms.naver.com/entry.nhn?docId=1227266&cid;=200000000&categoryId;=200003377 and acknowledged as a comprehensive university in 2008, according to the 'Higher Education Act'.http://terms.naver.com/entry.nhn?docId=1227266&cid;=200000000&categoryId;=200003377 Seoul Cyber University was established by the Law of Lifelong Education, accredited by Ministry of Education, Science and Technology (MEST) in November 2000.http://terms.naver.com/entry.nhn?docId=1227266&cid;=200000000&categoryId;=200003377 The online university's headquarters is located in Mia-dong, Gangbuk-gu, Seoul, which is within the vicinity of Mia station on Seoul Subway Line 4.
