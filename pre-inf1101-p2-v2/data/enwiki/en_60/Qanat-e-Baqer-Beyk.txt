Qanat-e Baqer Beyk (, also Romanized as Qanat-e Baqer Beyk; also known as Baqerin Kandi) is a village in Bastam Rural District, in the Central District of Chaypareh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 442, in 104 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaypareh County
