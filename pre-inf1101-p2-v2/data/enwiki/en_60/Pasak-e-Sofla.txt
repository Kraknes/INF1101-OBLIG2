Pasak-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Pasak-e Sofla; also known as Pasak and Pasak-e Pa'in) is a village in Firuraq Rural District, in the Central District of Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,944, in 428 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Khoy County
