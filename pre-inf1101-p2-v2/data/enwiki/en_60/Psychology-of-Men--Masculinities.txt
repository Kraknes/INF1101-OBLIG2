Psychology of Men & Masculinities (originally Psychology of Men & Masculinity) is a peer-reviewed academic journal published by the American Psychological Association on behalf of Division 51. The journal was established in 2000 and covers research on "how boys' and men's psychology is influenced and shaped by both gender and sex, and encompasses the study of the social construction of gender, sex differences and similarities, and biological processes." The current editor-in-chief is William Ming Liu. == Abstracting and indexing == The journal is abstracted and indexed in the Social Science Citation Index, Scopus, PsycINFO, Current Contents/Social & Behavioral Sciences, and CINAHL Plus.
