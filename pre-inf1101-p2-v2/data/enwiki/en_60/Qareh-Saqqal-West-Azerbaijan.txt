Qareh Saqqal (, also Romanized as Qareh Saqqal; also known as Qareh Saqal) is a village in Baruq Rural District, Baruq District, Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 737, in 152 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
