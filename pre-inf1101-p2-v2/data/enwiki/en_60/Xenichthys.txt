Xenichthys is a genus of grunts native to the eastern Pacific Ocean. ==Species== The currently recognized species in this genus are: * Xenichthys agassizii Steindachner, 1876 * Xenichthys rupestris Hildebrand, 1946 * Xenichthys xanti T. N. Gill, 1863 (longfin salema) ==References== Category:Haemulidae
