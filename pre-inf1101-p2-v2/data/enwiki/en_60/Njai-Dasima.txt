Njai Dasima (Perfected Spelling: Nyai Dasima) (njai meaning "companion or mistress") denotes the title character of G. Francis' 1896 novel Tjerita Njai Dasima. It may also refer to the following films: *Njai Dasima (1929 film), an adaptation of the novel directed by Lie Tek Swie *Njai Dasima (1932 film), an adaptation of the novel directed by Bachtiar Effendi
