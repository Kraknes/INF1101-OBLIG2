Tone Skogen in 2019 Tone Skogen (born 12 September 1953) is a Norwegian civil servant and politician for the Conservative Party, and a State Secretary in Norway's Ministry of Foreign Affairs. She served as a member of Akershus county council for sixteen years, and was a State Secretary in the Ministry of Trade and Industry between 2001 and 2005, as a part of Bondevik's Second Cabinet. She works as a head of department in the Ministry of Petroleum and Energy, and has been a board member of Ruter, Vinmonopolet and Oslo University Hospital.
