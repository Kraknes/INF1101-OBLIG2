Samuel Traina is an American environmental chemist and a founder of Sierra Nevada Research whose work used to be funded by the National Science Foundation, the United States Department of Energy and the United States Environmental Protection Agency. He also a member of various committees such as the Earth Resources Committee and National Research Council.
