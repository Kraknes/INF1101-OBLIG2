Johannes "Ryder" Mofokeng (born 20 August 1950 in Soweto, Gauteng) was a South African football (soccer) defender who played for amateur club White City Lucky Brothers and Kaizer Chiefs. ==Career== ===Youth career=== At White City Lucky Brothers, he was a teammate of Webster Lichaba before he was invited to play for the Chiefs reserves in 1972. ===Kaizer Chiefs=== During the "Golden Era" he was assigned as captain by Eddie Lewis when he was 23. He was captain for 11 years and won 4 NPSL titles and a quadruple in 1981 with Jingles Pereira, Marks Maponyane, Abednigo Ngcobo, Nelson Dladla and Vusi Lamola and more. He scored one goal in his whole career. ===After retirement=== He was the Kaizer Chiefs manager with Nelson Dladla in 1993.
