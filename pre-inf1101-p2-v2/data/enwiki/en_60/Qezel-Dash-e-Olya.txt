Qezel Dash-e Olya (, also Romanized as Qezel Dash-e 'Olya; also known as Qezeldash-e Bala) is a village in Sokmanabad Rural District, Safayyeh District, Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 326, in 64 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Khoy County
