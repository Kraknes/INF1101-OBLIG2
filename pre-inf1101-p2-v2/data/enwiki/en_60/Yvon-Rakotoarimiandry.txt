Yvon Rakotoarimiandry (born 5 January 1976), is a retired Malagasy athlete who specialised in the 400 metres hurdles. He represented Madagascar at the 2000 Summer Olympics narrowly missing the semifinals.Yvon Rakotoarimiandry. Sports Reference. Retrieved on 2013-10-27.
