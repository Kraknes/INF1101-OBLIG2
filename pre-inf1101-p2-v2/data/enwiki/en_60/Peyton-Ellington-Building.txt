Peyton-Ellington Building is a historic commercial building located at Charlottesville, Virginia. It was built in 1893, and is a two-story, three- bay, brick building with an iron front facade. The facade features decorated pilasters at each end that support a cornice with a plain frieze, modillions, and cornice stops. and Accompanying photo It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1982. ==References== Category:Commercial buildings on the National Register of Historic Places in Virginia Category:Victorian architecture in Virginia Category:Commercial buildings completed in 1893 Category:Buildings and structures in Charlottesville, Virginia Category:National Register of Historic Places in Charlottesville, Virginia
