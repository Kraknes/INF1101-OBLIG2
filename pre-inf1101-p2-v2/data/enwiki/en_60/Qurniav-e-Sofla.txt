Qurniav-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Qurniav-e Sofla; also known as Qurinav-e Pa'in) is a village in Baba Jik Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 76, in 14 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
