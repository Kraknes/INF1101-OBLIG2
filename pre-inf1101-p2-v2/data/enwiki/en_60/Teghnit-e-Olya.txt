Teghnit-e Olya (, also Romanized as Teghnit-e 'Olya; also known as Teghnit-e Bala) is a village in Chaldoran-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 109, in 20 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
