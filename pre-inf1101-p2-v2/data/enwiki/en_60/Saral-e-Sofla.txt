Saral-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Saral-e Sofla; also known as Saral-e Pa'in) is a village in Almahdi Rural District, Mohammadyar District, Naqadeh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 118, in 20 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Naqadeh County
