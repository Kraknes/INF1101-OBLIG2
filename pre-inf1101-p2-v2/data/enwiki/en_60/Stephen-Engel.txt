Stephen Engel is an American television producer and writer. He is an executive producer and writer of A.N.T. Farm and the executive producer of Mighty Med. He has written for Dream On, Mad About You, Alright Already, Just Shoot Me!, The War at Home, and The Big Bang Theory.
