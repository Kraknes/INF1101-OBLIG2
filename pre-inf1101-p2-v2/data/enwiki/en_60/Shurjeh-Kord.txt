Shurjeh Kord (, also Romanized as Shurjeh Kord; also known as Shurjeh and Shurjeh Hamidiyeh) is a village in Baruq Rural District, Baruq District, Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 357, in 83 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
