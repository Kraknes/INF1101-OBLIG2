Qarajeh-ye Meydan Daghi (, also Romanized as Qarajeh-ye Meydan Daghi; also known as Qarheh) is a village in Kandovan Rural District, Kandovan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
