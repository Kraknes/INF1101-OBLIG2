Qarah Bolagh (, also Romanized as Qarah Bolagh and Qareh Bolagh; also known as Kara-Bulag, Qarabulaq, and Qareh Bulagh) is a village in Kaghazkonan-e Shomali Rural District, Kaghazkonan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 471, in 141 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
