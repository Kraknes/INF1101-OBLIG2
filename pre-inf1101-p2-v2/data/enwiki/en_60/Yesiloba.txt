Yesiloba (literally "green nomad encampment" in Turkish) may refer to the following places in Turkey: * Yesiloba, Bekilli, a village in the district of Bekilli, Denizli Province, also known as Medele * Yesiloba, Besiri, a village in the district of Besiri, Batman Province * Yesiloba, Korkuteli, a village in the district of Korkuteli, Antalya Province
