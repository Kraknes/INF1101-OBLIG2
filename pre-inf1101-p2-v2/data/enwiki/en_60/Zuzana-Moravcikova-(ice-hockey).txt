Zuzana Moravcikova (born 23 October 1980 in Ruzomberok, Czechoslovakia) is a Slovakian ice hockey forward. ==International career== Moravcikova was selected for the Slovakia national women's ice hockey team in the 2010 Winter Olympics. She played in all five games, though she did not score a point.IIHF - Team Slovakia Stats - 2010 Olympics She played in the 2010 Olympic qualifying campaign.IIHF - Team Slovakia Stats - 2010 Olympic Qualifier Moravcikova has also appeared for Slovakia at six IIHF Women's World Championships, across two levels.
