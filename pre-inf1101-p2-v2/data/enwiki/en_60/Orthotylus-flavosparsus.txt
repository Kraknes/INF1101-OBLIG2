Orthotylus flavosparsus is a species of plant-eating bug in the Miridae family, which is found everywhere in Europe except for Albania and Iceland. It was introduced to North America. ==Description== The adult Orthotylus flavosparsus is normally about in length, and green in colour. ==Ecology== The species can be found on oraches and chenopods, which is their main food. ==References== ==External links== * Category:Insects described in 1841 Category:Hemiptera of Europe flavosparsus
