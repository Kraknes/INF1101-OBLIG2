Shal (, also Romanized as Shal; also known as Shal-e 'Abd or Rahmanlu) is a village in Owch Tappeh-ye Gharbi Rural District, Torkamanchay District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
