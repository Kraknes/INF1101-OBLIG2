Sheykh Selu (, also Romanized as Sheykh Selu; also known as Sheykhsilu) is a village in Qarah Quyun-e Jonubi Rural District, Qarah Quyun District, Showt County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 431, in 91 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Showt County
