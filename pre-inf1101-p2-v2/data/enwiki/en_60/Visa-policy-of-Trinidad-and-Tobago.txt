Trinidad and Tobago entry stamp Visitors to Trinidad and Tobago must obtain a visa unless they come from one of the visa exempt countries. Trinidad and Tobago signed a mutual visa-waiver agreement with the European Union on 28 May 2015 which was ratified on 15 December 2015.
