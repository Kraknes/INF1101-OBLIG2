Vali Kandi (, also Romanized as Vali Kandi; also known as Qeshlaq-e-Vali, Vali Kans, Vali Qeshlaq, and Vali Qishlaq) is a village in Chaybasar-e Shomali Rural District, Bazargan District, Maku County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 426, in 63 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Maku County
