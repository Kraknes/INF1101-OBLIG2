Paul Hazel (born May 4, 1990) is a Canadian football defensive end who is currently a free agent. He was most recently a member of the Ottawa Redblacks of the Canadian Football League (CFL). He played college football at Western Michigan where he played 49 games with 19 starts and posted 104 tackles, 24 tackles for loss, 17.5 sacks, seven forced fumbles, and one fumble recovery. He has one son and is single. == Professional career == === NFL === Unselected in the 2013 NFL Draft, Hazel first signed with the Jacksonville Jaguars.
