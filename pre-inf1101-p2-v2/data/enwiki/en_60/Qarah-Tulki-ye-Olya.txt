Qarah Tulki-ye Olya (, also Romanized as Qarah Tulki-ye 'Olya and Qareh Tulki- ye 'Olya; also known as Ghareh Tavakol Olya, Kara Tulki, and Qareh Tulki-ye Tazeh Kand) is a village in Baba Jik Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 43, in 8 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
