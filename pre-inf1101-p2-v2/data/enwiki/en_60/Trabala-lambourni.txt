Trabala lambourni is a moth of the family Lasiocampidae first described by George Thomas Bethune-Baker in 1911. It is found in Nigeria and the Democratic Republic of the Congo. ==Biology== A known host plant is Terminalia catappa."Trabala lambourni Bethune-Baker, 1911". African Moths.
