Yumenornis is an extinct genus of basal ornithuromorph dinosaur known from the Early Cretaceous Xiagou Formation of Changma Basin, Gansu Province of northwestern China. Yumenornis was first named by Ya-Ming Wang, Jingmai K. O'Connor, Da-Qing Li and Hai-Lu You in 2013 and the type species is Yumenornis huangi. ==References== Category:Basal Aves Category:Bird genera Category:Early Cretaceous birds of Asia Category:Fossil taxa described in 2013 Category:Paleontology in Gansu
