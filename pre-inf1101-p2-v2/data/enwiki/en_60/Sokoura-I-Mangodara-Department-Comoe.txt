Sokoura is a village in the Mangodara Department of Comoe Province in south- western Burkina Faso. The village has a population of 1,220.Burkinabe government inforoute communale There is another village called Sokoura in Mangodara Department; the two are distinguished in government documentation by Roman numerals after their names. ==References== Category:Populated places in the Cascades Region Category:Comoe Province
