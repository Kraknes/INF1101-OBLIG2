Vaimoso is a Samoan football club based in Apia. It currently plays in the Samoa National League. ==History== The first recorded appearance of Vaimoso in the Samoan football league system was in 2010-11 when they finished first in the Samoan first division.Samoa 2010-11 at RSSSF It is unclear whether there were play-offs for promotion or indeed, whether the club were promoted at all, because details are missing for the next season.Samoa - List of Champions at RSSSF However, they finished sixth in 2012-13, below Adidas Soccer Club but above Apia Youth, with 35 points, winning 10 and drawing five of their 22 games.Samoa 2012-13 at RSSSF ==Honours== Samoa First Division: *Winners: 2010-11 ==References== Category:Football clubs in Samoa
