Vasily Yuryevich Golubev (; born 30 January 1957 in Ermakovskaya, Rostov Oblast) is the governor of Rostov Oblast of Russia since 14 June 2010. == Career and life == He was a member of Communist Party of USSR. He became a politician in Moscow and Moscow Oblast where he was an acting governor for several weeks. He moved to Rostov after Vladimir Chub left his post of Oblast head and was connected to United Russia.
