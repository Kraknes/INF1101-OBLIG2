{| |} The Scoville Stardust JS-2 is a homebuilt aircraft designed for air racing. ==Design and development== The Stardust is a single place midwing aircraft with a single engine and conventional landing gear. the fuselage is constructed of welded steel tubing with fabric covering. The wings are all wood construction. ==Variants== ;Stardust II :Initial versionPlane and Pilot: 1978 Aircraft Directory, page 154.
