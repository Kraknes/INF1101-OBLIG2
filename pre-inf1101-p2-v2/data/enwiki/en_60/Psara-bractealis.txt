Psara bractealis is a moth of the family Crambidae described by George Hamilton Kenrick in 1907. It is found in Papua New Guinea. It has a wingspan of 30 mm.Kenrick, 1907. On Pyralidae of British New Guinea. Proceedings of the Zoological Society of London. 1907 t.4:79 pl.IV ==References== Category:Spilomelinae Category:Moths described in 1907
