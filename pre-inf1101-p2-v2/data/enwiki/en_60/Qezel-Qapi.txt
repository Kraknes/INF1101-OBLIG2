Qezel Qapi (, also Romanized as Qezel Qapi and Qezel Qopi; also known as Qezelqupi) is a village in Mokriyan-e Gharbi Rural District, in the Central District of Mahabad County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,935, in 395 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Mahabad County
