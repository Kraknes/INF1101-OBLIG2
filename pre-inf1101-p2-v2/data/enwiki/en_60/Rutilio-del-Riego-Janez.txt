Rutilio del Riego Janez (born September 21, 1940) is a Spanish-born American Catholic bishop who served as the Auxiliary Bishop for the Roman Catholic Diocese of San Bernardino. ==Biography== Born in Valdesandinas, Spain, del Riego Janez was ordained a priest on June 5, 1965. On June 26, 2005, he was appointed titular bishop of Daimlaig and auxiliary bishop of the Roman Catholic Diocese of San Bernardino, California.
