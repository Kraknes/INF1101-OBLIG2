Shekar Bolaghi-ye Olya (, also Romanized as Shekar Bolaghi-ye 'Olya; also known as Shekar Bolaghi) is a village in Bastam Rural District, in the Central District of Chaypareh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 48, in 10 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaypareh County
