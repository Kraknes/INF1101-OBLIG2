Qarah Aghaj (, also Romanized as Qarah Aghaj and Qareh Aghaj; also known as Ghareh Aghaj, Karaagach, Qara Agach, and Qareh Aqach) is a village in Chelleh Khaneh Rural District, Sufian District, Shabestar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 470, in 118 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Shabestar County
