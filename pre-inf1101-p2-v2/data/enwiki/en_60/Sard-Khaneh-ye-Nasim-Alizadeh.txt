Sard Khaneh-ye Nasim Alizadeh (, also Romanized as Sard Khaneh-ye Nasim 'Alizadeh) is a village in Hasanlu Rural District, Mohammadyar District, Naqadeh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran.
