Zaviyeh (, also Romanized as Zaviyeh and Zavieh; also known as Zaviyeh-ye Sokmanabad, Zeiva, Zeyveh, and Ziveh) is a village in Sokmanabad Rural District, Safayyeh District, Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,417, in 293 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Khoy County
