Tokiori - Dobras do Tempo is a 2013 Brazilian documentary film directed by Paulo Pastorelo. The film follows the memory of five families of Japanese immigrants who settled in Brazil in the 1930s.http://www.cinemaemcena.com.br/plus/modulos/filme/ver.php?cdfilme=13130 == Plot == The destinies of five families of Japanese immigrants crossed, between 1927 and 1934, in the rural neighborhood of Graminha, 45 km of Marilia, Sao Paulo. The film follows the journey of memories of three generations of these families, creating a mosaic of different experiences.
