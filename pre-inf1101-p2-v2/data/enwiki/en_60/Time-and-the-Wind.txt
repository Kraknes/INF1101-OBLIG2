Time and the Wind () is a 2013 Brazilian epic drama film based on a series of novels written by the Brazilian author Erico Verissimo. The film was directed by Jayme Monjardim and starring Thiago Lacerda, Marjorie Estiano, Fernanda Montenegro, and Cleo Pires. == Plot == Based on the novel trilogy of the same name, by Erico Verissimo, Time and the Wind follows 150 years of family Terra Cambara and their opponent Amaral family. The history of struggles between the two families begins in the missions and runs until the end of the 19th century.
