Pam Eyking (nee Cullen; born 1962)Pam Eyking and the Optics of Oz - Frank Magazine Archive.org is a Canadian politician, who was elected to the Nova Scotia House of Assembly in the 2013 provincial election. A member of the Nova Scotia Liberal Party, she represented the electoral district of Victoria-The Lakes. She is married to Mark Eyking, the federal Member of Parliament for Sydney--Victoria.
