Super Subbarayan (born as P. Subbarayan) is a fight master/action choreographers in the Indian cinema, mainly in Kollywood. He has been working in the industry since 1980. Stunt masters like Rocky Rajesh, Thalapathy Dinesh, Miracle Michael, Kudrathur Babu, Indian Baskar, Rajasekhar, Dhilip Subbarayan and Dinesh Subbarayan have worked as fighters and assistants to him. His sons Dhilip Subbarayan and Dinesh Subbarayan are also a stunt masters..
