Qinar-e Olya (, also Romanized as Qinar-e 'Olya; also known as Qiz-e Bala) is a village in Sokmanabad Rural District, Safayyeh District, Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 184, in 30 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Khoy County
