Studio West, opened in September 2014, is a studio school situated in Newcastle upon Tyne in Tyne and Wear, England. Studio West opened on the old All Saints site in West Denton. Along with Kenton School, it is part of the Kenton Academy Multi-Trust. Its address is West Denton Way, Newcastle upon Tyne NE5 2SZ.
