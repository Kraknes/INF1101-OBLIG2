Qashqa Bolagh-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Qashqa Bolagh-e Sofla) is a village in Chaldoran-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 122, in 22 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
