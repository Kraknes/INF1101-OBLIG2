Tazeh Kand-e Poruch (, also Romanized as Tazeh Kand-e Poruch; also known as Tazeh Kand) is a village in Kandovan Rural District, Kandovan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 88, in 18 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
