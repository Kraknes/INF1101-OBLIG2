Serpong Station (SRP) is a train station located in Serpong, South Tangerang, Banten. It is located not far from Bumi Serpong Damai complex or commonly known as BSD City. This station used to be the terminus for trains in the green line, now the line has been extended to , and . This station mostly served the commuter in Jabodetabek area.
