Tak Aghaj (, also Romanized as Tak Aghaj; also known as Tak Aqach) is a village in Ajorluy-ye Gharbi Rural District, Baruq District, Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 386, in 89 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
