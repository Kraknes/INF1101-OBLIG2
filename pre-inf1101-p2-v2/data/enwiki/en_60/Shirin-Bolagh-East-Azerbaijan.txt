Shirin Bolagh (, also Romanized as Shirin Bolagh; also known as Shirin Bulag and Shirinbulaq) is a village in Kaghazkonan-e Markazi Rural District, Kaghazkonan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 133, in 33 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
