Radoslav Vanko (born in 1983, in Zilina, Slovakia), is a Slovak model. Vanko graduated from the faculty of physical education and sport with a master's degree as a teacher and personal trainer. ==Career== Vanko has modeled for Valentino, Dolce and Gabbana, Giorgio Armani, Gucci, Pierre Cardin, Louis Vuitton and Prada.
