Reyhanluy-e Olya (, also Romanized as Reyhanluy-e 'Olya; also known as Reyhanlu-ye 'Olya and Reyhanlu-ye 'Olya) is a village in Chaldoran-e Shomali Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 215, in 42 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
