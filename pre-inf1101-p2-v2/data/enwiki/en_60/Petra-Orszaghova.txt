Petra Orszaghova (born 7 April 1981 in Banska Bystrica, Czechoslovakia) is a Slovakian ice hockey defender. ==International career== Orszaghova was selected for the Slovakia national women's ice hockey team in the 2010 Winter Olympics. She averaged over twenty minutes of ice time in the five games, but did not record a point.IIHF - Team Slovakia Stats - 2010 Olympics She played in the 2010 Olympic qualifying campaign.IIHF - Team Slovakia Stats - 2010 Olympic Qualifier Orszaghova also appeared for Slovakia at five IIHF Women's World Championships, across three levels. Her first appearance came in 2007.
