"No Trespassing" is a song recorded by Canadian country music artist George Fox. Released in 1989 as the first single from his second studio album, With All My Might, it peaked at number 10 on the RPM Country Tracks chart in February 1990. "No Trespassing" was also the title of the B side of the Ventures second charted record, "Perfidia", in 1960 on Dolton Records (No. 28), as well as being a track on their first album.
