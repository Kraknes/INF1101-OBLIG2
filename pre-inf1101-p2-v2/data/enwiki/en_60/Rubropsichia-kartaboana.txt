Rubropsichia kartaboana is a species of moth of the family Tortricidae. It is found in Guyana. 2011: Descriptions of five new species of the Neotropical Mictopsichia group of genera (Lepidoptera: Tortricidae). Zootaxa, 3058: 63-68.
