Niroshan Mani (born 24 July 1988) is an Indian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Students Union in the Bangalore Super Division. ==Career== ===Early career=== Born in Bangalore, Karnataka, Mani is a product of the Tata Football Academy. After leaving Tata, Mani joined Sporting Clube de Goa for whom he mainly played with in the I-League 2nd Division and Goa Professional League. He was then sent on loan to South United of the Bangalore Super Division. ===Bengaluru FC=== On 11 September 2013 it was confirmed that Mani had signed with Bengaluru FC of the I-League for the 2013-14 season.
