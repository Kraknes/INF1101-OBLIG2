Qabaq Tappeh (, also Romanized as Qabaq Tappeh and Qabaq Tappeh) is a village in Owch Tappeh-ye Gharbi Rural District, Torkamanchay District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 509, in 95 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
