Paeniglutamicibacter antarcticus, its old name is Arthrobacter antarcticus, is a species of bacteria. It is Gram-positive, motile, aerobic and has a rod-coccus cycle. SPC26T (=LMG 24542T =NCCB 100228T) is the type strain. ==References== ==Further reading== *Whitman, William B., et al., eds. Bergey's manual(r) of systematic bacteriology. Vol. 5.
