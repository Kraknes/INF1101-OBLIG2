Yaqub Ali Kandi (, also Romanized as Ya'qub 'Ali Kandi and Ya'qub'ali Kandi) is a village in Chaybasar-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Maku County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 367, in 58 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Maku County
