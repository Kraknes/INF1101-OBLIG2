Sefid Marmar Plaster Company ( - Makan-e Mostoqol Karkhaneh Gech-e Sefid Marmar) is a village and company town in Hajjilar-e Jonubi Rural District, Hajjilar District, Chaypareh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 6, in 5 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaypareh County Category:Company towns in Iran
