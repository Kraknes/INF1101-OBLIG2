{| |} The Pancoast Pelican is an American twin-engine homebuilt aircraft. ==Design and development== The Pelican is a two place side-by-side configuration, high-wing, twin-engine, tricycle gear aircraft. The open cockpit aircraft is of all-wood construction with a plywood fuselage. The tail section is removable for ground transportation on a trailer. ==Specifications (Pelican) == ==References== Category:Homebuilt aircraft
