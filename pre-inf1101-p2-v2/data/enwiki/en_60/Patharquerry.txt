Patharquerry is a locality in Guwahati, surrounded by Lokhra and Barsapara localities. Situated in northeastern part of the city, this area is residential with a small commercial infrastructure. ==Transport== Locality is well connected to rest of the city with city buses and other modes of transportation. ==See also== * Paltan Bazaar * Rehabari ==References== Category:Neighbourhoods in Guwahati
