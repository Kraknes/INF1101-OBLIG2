Ydin (which means "nuclear, nucleus, core, crux", and other things in Finnish) is a bi-monthly political magazine published in Helsinki, Finland. ==Profile== Ydin was established in 1966. The magazine is headquartered in Helsinki and focuses on political and societal news. Arja Alho, former member of the Finnish parliament, is the editor-in-chief of the magazine. Asa Butcher, co- editor of Ovi Magazine, is one of the editors of Ydin.
