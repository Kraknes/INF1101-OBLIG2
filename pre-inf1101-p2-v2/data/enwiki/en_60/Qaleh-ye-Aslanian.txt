Qaleh-ye Aslanian (, also Romanized as Qal'eh-ye Aslanian; also known as Gala, Kalat, and Qal'eh) is a village in Kaghazkonan-e Shomali Rural District, Kaghazkonan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 32, in 11 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
