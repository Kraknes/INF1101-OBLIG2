Sefid Kamar (, also Romanized as Sefid Kamar; also known as Asem Kamar, Esmishamr, Ismichamr', and Ismishamar) is a village in Mishu-e Jonubi Rural District, Sufian District, Shabestar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 648, in 189 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Shabestar County
