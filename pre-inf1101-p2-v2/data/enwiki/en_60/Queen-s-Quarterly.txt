Queen's Quarterly is a Canadian quarterly peer-reviewed academic journal of cultural studies that was established in 1893 by, among others, George Munro Grant, Sanford Fleming, and John Watson, all of Queen's University in Kingston, Ontario."Queen's Quarterly: Fostering Canadian Cultural Identity" Historical Perspectives on Canadian Publishing, McMaster University. Retrieved 9 October 2013. The journal publishes articles, essays, reviews, short stories and poetry.
