Sriranjini is an Indian actress (born June 1, 1971) who has appeared in supporting roles in predominantly Tamil language films. ==Career== Sriranjini began her career in acting playing a role in the television series Kasalavu Nesam directed by K. Balachander, but made the switch to films after being offered a role in Mani Ratnam's Alaipayuthey (2000) as Madhavan's sister-in- law. Her next role which won acclaim was her depiction of an Iyengar wife in Shankar's Anniyan (2005).
