'Qaleh Bozorg (, also Romanized as Qal'eh Bozorg; also known as Qalat, 'Qal'eh, Qal'eh Kuchek, and Qal'eh-ye Kuchek) is a village in Marhemetabad-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 289 people, in 66 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
