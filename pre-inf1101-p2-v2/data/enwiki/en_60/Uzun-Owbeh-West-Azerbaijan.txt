Uzun Owbeh (, also Romanized as Uzun Owbeh; also known as Uzon Owbeh) is a village in Marhemetabad-e Shomali Rural District, Marhemetabad District, Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 788, in 173 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
