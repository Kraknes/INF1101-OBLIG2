Panda Oy is a Finnish confectionery company based in Vaajakoski, Jyvaskyla. The company was founded in 1920 by SOK (Suomen Osuuskauppojen Keskuskunta). Panda is known for its liquorice and chocolate products. In 2005, the company was sold to Felix Abba, which is part of the Norwegian Orkla Group.
