Pranas Mazeika (1917 in Beloretsk, Bashkortostan - 2007 in USA) was a Lithuanian basketball player. He won two gold medals with the Lithuania national basketball team during EuroBasket 1937 and EuroBasket 1939.European Championships for Men, Lithuanian Basketball Research. ==References== ==Further reading== * Vidas Maciulis, Vytautas Gudelis.
