Oleg Sudakov (; 4 May 1962), better known as Manager is a Russian musician, poet, artist, and publicist. The lead singer of the Manager & Rodina, Tsyganyata i Ya s Ilyicha, Anarchiya and Armiya Vlasova. He is also cofounder of band Kommunizm (with Yegor Letov and Konstantin Ryabinov).
