Seyf ol Din-e Olya (, also Romanized as Seyf ol Din-e 'Olya and Seyf od Din-e 'Olya) is a village in Akhtachi Rural District, in the Central District of Bukan County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 170, in 25 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Bukan County
