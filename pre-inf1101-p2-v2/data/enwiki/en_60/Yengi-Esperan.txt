Yengi Esperan (, also Romanized as Yengi Esperan and Yengi Asparan; also known as Angilspiran, Isbiran, Safidan-e Jadid, Sefidan-e Jadid, Sefidan Jadid, Yengi Esberan, Yengi Esiran, and Yengi-Isperan) is a village in Esperan Rural District, in the Central District of Tabriz County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2015 census, its population was 3,515, in 735 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Tabriz County
