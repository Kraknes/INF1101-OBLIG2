Evangelos "Vangelis" Margaritis (alternate spellings: Evaggelos, Vaggelis) (; born December 5, 1982) is a Greek professional basketball player who plays for and captains PAOK of the Greek Basket League. He is 6 ft 8 in (2.03 m) tall, and he can play at either the power forward or center positions. ==Professional career== Some of the clubs that Margaritis has played with during his career include: Iraklis, Ionikos Nikaias, Peristeri, KAOD, and PAOK. He renewed his contract with PAOK in 2013. In 2015, he eventually became PAOK's team captain.
