Tokhtamishlu (, also Romanized as Tokhtamishlu; also known as Tokhtameshlu, Tokhtamoshlu, Tokhtamush, and Tukhdamesh) is a village in Kaghazkonan-e Shomali Rural District, Kaghazkonan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 67, in 28 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
