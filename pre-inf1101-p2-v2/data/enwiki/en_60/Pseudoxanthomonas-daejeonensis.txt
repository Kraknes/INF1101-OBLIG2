Pseudoxanthomonas daejeonensis is a species of Gram-negative, non-spore- forming, rod-shaped bacteria, first isolated from ginseng fields. TR6-08(T) (=KCTC 12207(T)=IAM 15115(T)) is the type strain. ==References== ==Further reading== *Whitman, William B., et al., eds. Bergey's manual(r) of systematic bacteriology. Vol. 3.
