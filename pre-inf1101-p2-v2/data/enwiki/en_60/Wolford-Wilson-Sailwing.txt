{| |} The Wolford-Wilson Sailwing is an American single place homebuilt aircraft. ==Design and development== The Sailwing is a single place, open cockpit, twin engine pusher with an inverted V-tail and conventional landing gear. The aircraft uses an aluminum leading edge with wire support and Dacron covering, rather than a spar. Roll control is performed with wing-warping. ==Specifications (Sailwing) == ==See also== ==References== Category:Homebuilt aircraft
