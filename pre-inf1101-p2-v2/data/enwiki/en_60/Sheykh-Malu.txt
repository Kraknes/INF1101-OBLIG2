Sheykh Malu (, also Romanized as Sheykh Malu and Sheikh Maloo; also known as Shaikh 'Amli, Shekhamlu, Shekhemlu, Sheykhamlu, and Shikhamli) is a village in Ozomdel-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 253, in 51 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
