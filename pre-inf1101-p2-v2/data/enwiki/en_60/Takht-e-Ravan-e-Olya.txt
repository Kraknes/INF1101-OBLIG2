Takht-e Ravan-e Olya (, also Romanized as Takht-e Ravan-e 'Olya) is a village in Chaldoran-e Shomali Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 410, in 68 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
