The Honourable Robert Brooking was a judge of the Court of Appeal of Victoria from 1995, having been appointed a judge of the Supreme Court of Victoria in 1977. He was also an acting judge of the Supreme Court of the Northern Territory for 6 months in 2000-2001. Brooking was admitted to practice as a solicitor in 1954 and was appointed a Queen's Counsel in 1969. Brooking retired in 2002 after serving as a judge for 25 years. (2002, Winter) 121 Victorian Bar News 16.
