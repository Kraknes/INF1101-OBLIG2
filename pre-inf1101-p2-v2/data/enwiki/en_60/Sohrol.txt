Sohrol (, also Romanized as Sahrol; also known as Sohreqeh, Sokhrul, and Suhrul) is a village in Rudqat Rural District, Sufian District, Shabestar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 209, in 55 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Shabestar County
