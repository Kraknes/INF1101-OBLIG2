Paul Sturm (1 April 1859 - 21 December 1936) was a German art nouveau sculptor, medallist and designer. ==Life== Sturm was born in Leipzig, Saxony. He started his education with an apprenticeship as a wood carver. His tertiary education was gained at schools of Fine Art in Munich, Zurich, Lausanne, and Lyon. From 1884 he studied at the Hochschule fur Grafik und Buchkunst Leipzig .Staatliche Museen zu Berlin. German biography of artists, retrieved 10 October 2013 In 1908 he relocated to Berlin, where he had to accept an early retirement for health reasons in 1919 under difficult post war conditions.
