Stephen Kaye (born 1951) is a judge of the Supreme Court of Victoria. He was appointed to the bench in 2003, serving as a judge of the Trial Division until his appointment the Court of Appeal in 2015. Kaye is the son of William Kaye, who was also a Supreme Court judge. His paternal grandparents were Jewish immigrants from the Ukraine.
