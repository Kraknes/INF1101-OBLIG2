Qezkhachlu (, also Romanized as Qezkhachlu; also known as Ghiz Khachloo, Kizkish, Kizkishli, Qizkhachlu, Qiz Khajlu, Qiz Khashlu, and Qizqashli) is a village in Baba Jik Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 101, in 21 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
