Qarah Hajjilu (, also Romanized as Qarah Hajjilu and Qareh Hajjilu; also known as Qara Haji, Qarah Hajilu, and Qareh Hajjlu) is a village in Qaflankuh-e Sharqi Rural District, Kaghazkonan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 100, in 26 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
