Sardaraq (; also known as Sari Daraq, Sari Dareh, Saridarreh, and Saridarrekh) is a village in Kaghazkonan-e Markazi Rural District, Kaghazkonan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 69, in 23 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
