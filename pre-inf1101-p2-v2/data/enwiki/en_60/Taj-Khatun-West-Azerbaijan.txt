Taj Khatun (, also Romanized as Taj Khatun and Taj Khatoon; also known as Tash Khatun) is a village in Hajjilar-e Shomali Rural District, Hajjilar District, Chaypareh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 382, in 77 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaypareh County
