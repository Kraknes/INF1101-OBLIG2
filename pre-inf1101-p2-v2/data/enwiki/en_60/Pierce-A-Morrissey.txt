Pierce A. Morrissey was a member of the Wisconsin State Senate. ==Biography== Morrissey was born on April 15, 1870 in Nepeuskun, Wisconsin. He attended school in Waukau, Wisconsin. ==Career== Morrissey represented the 19th district in the Senate. Previously, he was Assessor of Nepeuskun from 1912 to 1917 and Chairman and a member of the Board of Supervisors in 1922.
