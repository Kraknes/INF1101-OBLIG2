Qareh Tappeh (; also known as Lak, Lak-e 'Olya, Lak-e Sofla, Liyek, Qara Tepe, Qareh Tappeh 'Olya va Sofla, Qareh Tappeh-ye Bala, Qareh Tappeh-ye 'Olya va Sofla, and Qareh Tappeh-ye Pa'in) is a village in Guney-ye Gharbi Rural District, Tasuj District, Shabestar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,768, in 496 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Shabestar County
