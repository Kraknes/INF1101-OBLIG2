Raul Gomez Ramirez (29 May 1964 - 10 December 2014) was a Mexican politician affiliated with the PAN. He served as a deputy in the LXII Legislature of the Mexican Congress, representing the tenth district of Guanajuato. On 16 November 2014 Gomez was involved in a traffic accident near Celaya, Guanajuato.
