Qareh Qayeh (, also Romanized as Qarah Qiyah; also known as Garkiyeh, Qareh Qiyah-e Moshk-e 'Anbar, and Qareh Tappeh) is a village in Sina Rural District, in the Central District of Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 54, in 8 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
