Patrizio Hofer (born April 2, 1980) is a compound archer from Switzerland.World Archery profile - Patrizio Hofer He regularly represents Switzerland at the highest level World Archery Federation competitions, including the FITA Archery World Cup and the World Archery Championships. In 2008 and 2009 he reached the World Cup final, finishing 2nd and 3rd respectively.
