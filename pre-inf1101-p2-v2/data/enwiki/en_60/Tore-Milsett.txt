Tore Milsett (born 23 April 1944) is a Norwegian cyclist. He was born in Lorenskog. He competed at the 1968 Summer Olympics in Mexico City, where he placed fifth in team trial with the Norwegian team, which consisted of Thorleif Andresen, Ornulf Andresen, Leif Yli and Milsett.
