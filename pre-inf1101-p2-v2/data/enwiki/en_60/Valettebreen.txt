Valettebreen is a glacier in Sorkapp Land on Spitsbergen, Svalbard. It is about two kilometers long, and is located between Guilbaudtoppen and Haitanna. The glacier is named after Emile Valette, the French radio operator on Amundsen's ill-fated rescue expedition for the lost Airship Italia. ==References== Category:Glaciers of Spitsbergen
