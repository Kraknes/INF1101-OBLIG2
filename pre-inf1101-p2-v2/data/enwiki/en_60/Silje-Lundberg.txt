Silje Ask Lundberg (born 13 April 1988 in Oslo) is a Norwegian environmentalist and chairman of Norwegian Society for the Conservation of Nature (Friends of the Earth Norway). She is a former leder of Nature and Youth. She grew up in Harstad in Northern Norway.
