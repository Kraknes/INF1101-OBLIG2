Sheykh Vali (, also Romanized as Sheykh Vali, Shaikhwali, Sheikh Vali, and Shikhvali) is a village in Guney-ye Gharbi Rural District, Tasuj District, Shabestar County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 763, in 193 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Shabestar County
