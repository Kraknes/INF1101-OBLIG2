Yusef Kandi (, also Romanized as Yusef Kandi, Yusefkandi, and Yusof Kandi; also known as Yusof Kand and Yusuf Kandi) is a village in Mokriyan-e Gharbi Rural District, in the Central District of Mahabad County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 2,345, in 488 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Mahabad County
