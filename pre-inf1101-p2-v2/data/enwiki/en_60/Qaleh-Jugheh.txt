Qaleh Jugheh (, also Romanized as Qal'eh Jugheh; also known as Qal'eh Juqeh) is a village in Akhtachi-ye Gharbi Rural District, in the Central District of Mahabad County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 162, in 33 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Mahabad County
