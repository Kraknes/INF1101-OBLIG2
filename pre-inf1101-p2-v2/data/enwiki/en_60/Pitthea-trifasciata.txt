Pitthea trifasciata is a moth of the family Geometridae first described by Hermann Dewitz in 1881. ==Distribution== It is found in Kenya, Niger, Uganda, Zimbabwe and Tanzania. ==References== *Dewitz (1881). "Afrikanische Nachtschmetterlinge". Nova Acta Leopoldina. Bd. 42, no. 2. ==External links== *"Pitthea trifasciata Dewitz, 1881". African Moths. With images and a distribution map. * With images.
