Southern Aeronautical Corporation was an American aircraft manufacturer, founded by Charles Lasher and based in Miami Lakes, Florida. The company specialized in the design of Formula V Air Racing aircraft for amateur construction.Plane and Pilot: 1978 Aircraft Directory, page 151. Werner & Werner Corp, Santa Monica CA, 1977. The company was formed as a Florida Domestic for Profit Corporation on 26 January 1968 and listed Charles W. Lasher as President and Director; John W. Lasher as Treasurer and Director and Helen B. Lasher as Director and Secretary.
