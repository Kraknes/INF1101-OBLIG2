Qand Kheli (, also Romanized as Qand Kheli; also known as Qand Qoli) is a village in Avajiq-e Jonubi Rural District, Dashtaki District, Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
