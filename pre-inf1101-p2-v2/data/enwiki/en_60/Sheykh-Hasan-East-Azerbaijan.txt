Sheykh Hasan (, also Romanized as Sheykh Hasan, Shaikh Hasan, and Sheikh Hasan; also known as Sheykh Khasan) is a village in Lahijan Rural District, Khosrowshahr District, Tabriz County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 2,240, in 552 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Tabriz County
