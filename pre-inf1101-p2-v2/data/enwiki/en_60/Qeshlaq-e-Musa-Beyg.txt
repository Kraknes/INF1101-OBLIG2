Qeshlaq-e Musa Beyg (, also Romanized as Qeshlaq-e Musa Beyg; also known as Qeshlaq-e Musa Beyglu) is a village in Tirchai Rural District, Kandovan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 288, in 63 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
