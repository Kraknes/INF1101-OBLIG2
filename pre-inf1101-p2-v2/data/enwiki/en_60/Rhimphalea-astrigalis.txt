Rhimphalea astrigalis is a small moth in the family Crambidae that is found in the mountains of Borneo and Thailand. The species was first described by George Hampson in 1898. ==References== * Hampson, G. F. (1899). "A revision of the moths of the subfamily Pyraustinae and family Pyralidae, part 1".
