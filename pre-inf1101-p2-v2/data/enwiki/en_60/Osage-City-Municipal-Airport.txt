Osage City Municipal Airport is a city-owned public-use airport located one nautical mile (1.6 km) east of the central business district of Osage City, a city in Osage County, Kansas, United States. == Facilities and aircraft == Osage City Municipal Airport covers an area of at an elevation of 1105 feet (337 m) above mean sea level. It has one runway: 17/35 is 2,560 by 40 feet (780 x 12 m) with an asphalt surface. For the 12-month period ending August 31, 2012, the airport had 2,700 aircraft operations, an average of 7 per day: 100% general aviation.
