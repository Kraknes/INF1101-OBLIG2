Takhteh Duz (, also Romanized as Takhteh Duz; also known as Takhteh Dozi and Takhteh Duzi) is a village in Qaleh Darrehsi Rural District, in the Central District of Maku County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 324, in 59 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Maku County
