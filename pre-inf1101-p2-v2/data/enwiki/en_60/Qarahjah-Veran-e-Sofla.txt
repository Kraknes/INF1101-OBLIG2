Qarahjah Veran-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Qarahjah Veran-e Sofla; also known as Qarah Chahveran-e Sofla) is a village in Chaldoran-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 679, in 113 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
