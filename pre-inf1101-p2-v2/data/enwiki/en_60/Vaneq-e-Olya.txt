Vaneq-e Olya (, also Romanized as Vaneq-e 'Olya; also known as Vaneq-e Bala) is a village in Molla Yaqub Rural District, in the Central District of Sarab County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 103, in 18 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Sarab County
