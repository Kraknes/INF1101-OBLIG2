Qeshlaq-e Barzaliq (, also Romanized as Qeshlaq-e Barzaliq) is a village in Barvanan-e Sharqi Rural District, Torkamanchay District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 262, in 58 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
