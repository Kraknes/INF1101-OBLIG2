Qusha Bolagh-e Olya (, also Romanized as Qusha Bolagh-e 'Olya; also known as Qusha Bolagh-e Bala) is a village in Baba Jik Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 351, in 62 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
