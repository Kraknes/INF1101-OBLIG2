Warsaw High School is a public four-year high school located in the Truman Lake/Lake of the Ozarks area. It is located out of town on Lane of Champions by the intersection Wildcat Drive and highway 83. It is the only high school in the Warsaw R-IX School district. The original building had a Middle school built on to it. Warsaw High School, or WHS, serves the communities of Warsaw, Edwards, Tightwad, Fristoe, and Cross timbers. Warsaw High School is home to a Taco Bell teacher finalist, Jowell Roellig.
