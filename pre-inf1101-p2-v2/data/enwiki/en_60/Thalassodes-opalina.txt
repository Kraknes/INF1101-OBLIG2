Thalassodes opalina is a species of moth of the family Geometridae. It is found in Asia, where it is known from India, Thailand, Taiwan, and China.Hongxiang Han & Dayong Xue, 2011.Thalassodes and related taxa of emerald moths in Chine (Geometridae, Geometrinae).
