"When Love Calls Your Name" is a song written by Richard Cocciante and Roxanne Seeman. It was recorded and released by Elhaida Dani as a single, in connection with The Voice of Italy television show. Dani was a contestant on the team of Riccardo Cocciante, Coach. The song was part of the voting process. Dani performed "When Love Calls Your Name" during the finale episode of The Voice of Italy, May 30, 2013.
