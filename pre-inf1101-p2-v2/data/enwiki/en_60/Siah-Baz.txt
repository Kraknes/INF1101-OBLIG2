Siah Baz (, also Romanized as Siah Baz and Siyah Baz; also known as Seyyed 'Abbas) is a village in Ivughli Rural District, Ivughli District, Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 2,867, in 694 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Khoy County
