Qarah Qush-e Olya (, also Romanized as Qarah Qush-e 'Olya and Qareh Qush-e 'Olya; also known as Qara Kush Yukari, Qareh Qush, Qarehqush Bala, and Qareh Qush-e Bala) is a village in Sokmanabad Rural District, Safayyeh District, Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 139, in 29 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Khoy County
