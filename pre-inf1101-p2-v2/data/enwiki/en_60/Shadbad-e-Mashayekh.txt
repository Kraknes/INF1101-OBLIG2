Shadbad-e Mashayekh (, also Romanized as Shadbad-e Mashayekh; also known as Panbeh Shalvar, Pina Shalvar, Pineh Shalvar, Shadabad-e Mashayekh, and Shah Abad-e-Mashayekh) is a village in Meydan Chay Rural District, in the Central District of Tabriz County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 4,317, in 1,086 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Tabriz County
