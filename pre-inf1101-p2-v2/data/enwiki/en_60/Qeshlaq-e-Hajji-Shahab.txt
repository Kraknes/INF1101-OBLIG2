Qeshlaq-e Hajji Shahab (, also Romanized as Qeshlaq-e Hajji Shahab; also known as Qeshlaq) is a village in Behi Dehbokri Rural District, Simmineh District, Bukan County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 177, in 26 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Bukan County
