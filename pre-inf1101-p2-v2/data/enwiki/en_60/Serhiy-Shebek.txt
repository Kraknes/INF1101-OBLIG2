Serhiy Shebek (born 14 June 1960, in the Ukrainian SSR of the Soviet Union) is a former Ukrainian football referee. He is a record holder for the most served football games in the Ukrainian Top Division. Shebek retired on December 31, 2008.Bryzh, Ye. Serhiy Shebek: one of the last games in his career. football.ua.
