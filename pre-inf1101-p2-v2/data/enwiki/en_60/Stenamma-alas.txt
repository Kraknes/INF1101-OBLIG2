Stenamma alas is a species of ant which can be found in wet forests of Costa Rica on an elevation of . ==Description== It pronotum is feeble and thin and have transverse stretch marks. The species legs are reddish-brown in colour while its head is dorsal on face and have a rugae which is transverse. Its propodeum is identical in brightness to promesonotum while its mesosoma is dark in colour. ==Habitat== The species is found on clay banks, along the streams, and on hillsides. ==References== ==External links== * *Stenamma alas Category:Myrmicinae Category:Insects described in 2005 Category:Hymenoptera of North America
