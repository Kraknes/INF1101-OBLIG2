YUL Condominiums is a large residential skyscraper complex in Montreal, Quebec, Canada. The towers are located on Rene-Levesque Boulevard at the corner of Bishop and Mackay, near E-Commerce Place and the Bell Centre. The towers have 38 floors and tall, and consist of 873 condos. YUL Condominiums also included the construction of 17 townhouses on Overdale Avenue.
