The Supercopa de Espana de Baloncesto Femenino (English: Spanish Women's Basketball Supercup) is an annual preseason Spanish professional women's basketball competition. ==Format== The teams that take part in this competition are: * The Liga Femenina winner of the previous season. * The Copa de la Reina winner of the previous season (or the Cup's runner-up if both teams coincide) ==Winners by
