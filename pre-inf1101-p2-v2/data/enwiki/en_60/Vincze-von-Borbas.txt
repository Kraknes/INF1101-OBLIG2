Borbas Vince Vincze von Borbas (28 July 1844 - 7 July 1905) was a Hungarian botanist. He was born in Ipoly-Litke, Hungary, and died in Kolozsvar (now Cluj-Napoca, Romania). A plaque in commemoration of Borbas in Budapest. Borbas named numerous plant species, most of which are taxonomic synonyms of currently accepted names.
