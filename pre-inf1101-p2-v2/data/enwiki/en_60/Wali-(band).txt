Wali is an Indonesian pop rock band formed in Ciputat, Indonesia in 1999. The band comprises vocalist Faank, drummer Tomi, keyboardist Ovie and guitarist Apoy. Wali are known for their hit singles, such as "Cari Jodoh", "Dik", "Si Udin Bertanya" and "Baik Baik Sayang".
