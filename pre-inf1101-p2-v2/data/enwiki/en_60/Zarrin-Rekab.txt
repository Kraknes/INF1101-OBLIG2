Zarrin Rekab (, also Romanized as Zarrin Rekab; also known as Zar Nekarab) is a village in Ozomdel-e Shomali Rural District, in the Central District of Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
