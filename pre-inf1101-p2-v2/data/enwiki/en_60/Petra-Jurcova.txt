Petra Jurcova (born 22 June 1987 in Kosice, Czechoslovakia) is a Slovakian ice hockey forward. Her younger brother Tomas Jurco is also a hockey player. ==International career== Jurcova was selected for the Slovakia national women's ice hockey team in the 2010 Winter Olympics. She played in all five games, recording one assist.IIHF - Team Slovakia Stats - 2010 Olympics She played in the qualifying campaigns for the 2010 and 2014 Olympics.IIHF - Team Slovakia Stats - 2010 Olympic QualifierIIHF - Team Slovakia Stats - 2014 Olympic Qualifier Jurcova has also appeared for Slovakia at six IIHF Women's World Championships, across three levels. Her first appearance came in 2005.
