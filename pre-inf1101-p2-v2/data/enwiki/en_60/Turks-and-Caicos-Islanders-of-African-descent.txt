Turks and Caicos Islanders of African descent or Black Turks and Caicos Islanders are Turks and Caicos Islanders who are of African descent. As of 2013, people of African descent are the majority ethnic group in the Turks and Caicos Islands accounting for around 87.6% of the territory's population. == References and footnotes == African Category:Ethnic groups in the Turks and Caicos Islands Category:People of African descent
