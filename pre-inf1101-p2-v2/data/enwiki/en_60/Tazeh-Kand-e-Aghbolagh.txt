Tazeh Kand-e Aghbolagh (, also Romanized as Tazeh Kand-e Aghbolagh; also known as Tazeh Kand and Tazeh Kand-e Aqbolagh) is a village in Ajorluy-ye Gharbi Rural District, Baruq District, Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
