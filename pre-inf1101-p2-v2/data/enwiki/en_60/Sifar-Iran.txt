Sifar (, also Romanized as Sifar, Seifar, and Seyfar; also known as Seyghar and Sighar) is a village in Sina Rural District, in the Central District of Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 321, in 61 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
