Sohran (, also Romanized as Sohran; also known as Sagran, Sagrun, Segryun, Sohrown, and Sohrun) is a village in Arzil Rural District, Kharvana District, Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 95, in 25 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
