Zangelan-e Olya (, also Romanized as Zangelan-e 'Olya; also known as Zangelan-e Bala) is a village in Hajjilar-e Jonubi Rural District, Hajjilar District, Chaypareh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 326, in 89 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaypareh County
