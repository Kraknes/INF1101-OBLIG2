Planet Bowl is a large tenpin bowling centre in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. It was the venue for 10 pin bowling for the 2015 Pan American Games after a change in site from Classic Bowl in Mississauga. The centre, located in the Etobicoke district, is one of the largest in Toronto area with 48 lanes.
