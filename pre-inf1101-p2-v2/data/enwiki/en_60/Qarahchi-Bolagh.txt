Qarahchi Bolagh (, also Romanized as Qarahchi Bolagh; also known as Qarahchah Bolagh) is a village in Chaldoran-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 112, in 19 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
