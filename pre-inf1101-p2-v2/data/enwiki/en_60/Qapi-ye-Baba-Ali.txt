Qapi-ye Baba Ali (, also Romanized as Qapi-ye Baba 'Ali; also known as Kopeh- ye Baba 'Ali, Kopi-Bab 'Ali, Koppeh Baba 'Ali, Koppeh-ye Baba 'Ali, Qopi Bab 'Ali, Qowbi-ye Baba 'Ali, and Qowpi-ye Baba 'Ali) is a village in Mokriyan-e Sharqi Rural District, in the Central District of Mahabad County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 597, in 118 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Mahabad County
