Tevin (, also Romanized as Tevin and Tovin; also known as Tiviun and Tivyun) is a village in Garmeh-ye Shomali Rural District, Kandovan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 594, in 118 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
