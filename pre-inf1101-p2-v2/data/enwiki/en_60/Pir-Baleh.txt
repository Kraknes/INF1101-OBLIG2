Pir Baleh (, also Romanized as Pir Baleh; also known as Mazra'eh and Pir Balen) is a village in Akhtachi-ye Gharbi Rural District, in the Central District of Mahabad County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported. == References == Category:Populated places in Mahabad County
