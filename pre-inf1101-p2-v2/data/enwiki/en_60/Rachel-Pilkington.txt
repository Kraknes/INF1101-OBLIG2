Rachel Carson Pilkington (born 28 November 1974) is an Irish actress. ==Career== In 2004, Pilkington received an IFTA nomination in the Best Actress in a Supporting Role - Film/TV category for her role in The Clinic. She has been playing the role of Jane Black in RTE soap opera Fair City since 2013. ==Personal life== She is the daughter of the actor Joe Pilkington who played Eamonn Maher in the television serial, The Riordans.
