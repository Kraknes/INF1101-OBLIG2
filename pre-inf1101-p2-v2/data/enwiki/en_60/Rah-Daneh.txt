Rah Daneh (, also Romanized as Rah Daneh; also known as Rah Dahaneh) is a village in Beygom Qaleh Rural District, in the Central District of Naqadeh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 681, in 177 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Naqadeh County
