Sefidan-e Atiq (, also Romanized as Sefidan-e 'Atiq and Safidan-Atiq; also known as Dash Esparan, Dash Esperan, Dash-Isperan, Dash Ispiran, and Sefidane Atigh) is a village in Esperan Rural District, in the Central District of Tabriz County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 251, in 51 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Tabriz County
