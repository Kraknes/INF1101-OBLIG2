Qareh Shaban (, also Romanized as Qareh Sha'ban; also known as Ghareh Sha'ban, Kara-Shaban, and Qara Shaban) is a village in Gowharan Rural District, in the Central District of Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 705, in 175 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Khoy County
