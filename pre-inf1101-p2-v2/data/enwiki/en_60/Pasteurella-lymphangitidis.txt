Pasteurella lymphangitidis is a bacterium; it causes bovine lymphangitis. Its reclassification to Yersinia has been proposed, given it poses a 99% sequence similarity to both Yersinia pseudotuberculosis and Yersinia pestis. ==References== ==Further reading== * * ==External links== * *LPSN *Type strain of Pasteurella lymphangitidis at BacDive - the Bacterial Diversity Metadatabase Category:Gammaproteobacteria Category:Bacteria described in 1990
