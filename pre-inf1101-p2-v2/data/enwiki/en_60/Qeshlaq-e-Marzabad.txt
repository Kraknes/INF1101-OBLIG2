Qeshlaq-e Marzabad (, also Romanized as Qeshlaq-e Marzabad) is a village in Dizmar-e Markazi Rural District, Kharvana District, Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
