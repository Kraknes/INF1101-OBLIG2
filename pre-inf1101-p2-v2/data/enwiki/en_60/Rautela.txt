Rautela is an upper caste of Hindu Rajputs from Garhwal and Kumaon divisions in the Indian state of Uttarakhand.They always celebrate Diwali one day prior to actual Diwali as they belong to the Parmar dynasty of Uttarakhand. == History == In 1786, king Mohan Singh Rautela defeated king Prakram Shah and at the battle of Pali Gaon defeated Harsh Deo with the help of his brother Lal Singh Rautela. == References == Category:Rajput clans of Uttarakhand
