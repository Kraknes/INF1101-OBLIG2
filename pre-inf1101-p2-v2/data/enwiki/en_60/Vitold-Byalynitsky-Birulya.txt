Post stamp from Belarus with the image of Vitold Byalynitsky-Birulya (1997). Vitold Kaetanovich Byalynitsky-Birulya (, ; in Krynki, Mogilev Region, Belarus - 18 June 1957 in Moscow) was a Belarusian Soviet landscape painter.
