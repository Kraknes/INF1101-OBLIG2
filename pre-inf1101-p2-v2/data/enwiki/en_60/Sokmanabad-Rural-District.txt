Sokmanabad Rural District () is a rural district (dehestan) in Safayyeh District, Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census (including Zurabad, which was subsequently detached from the rural district and promoted to city status), its population was 13,012, in 2,525 families; excluding Zurabad, its population (as of 2006) was 12,095, in 2,328 families.
