The 22651/22652 Palakkad Junction Superfast Express is a daily express train between Chennai Central (Code:MAS) and Palakkad Junction railway station (Code:PGT) by Southern Railways via Salem-Karur-Dindigul-Palani-Pollachi section. In the beginning it ran between Chennai Central and Palani. Then it had been extended up to Pollachi Junction as a special train between Palani and Pollachi. In the 2017 budget it had been extended up to Palakkad Junction by serving two states Tamil Nadu and Kerala.
