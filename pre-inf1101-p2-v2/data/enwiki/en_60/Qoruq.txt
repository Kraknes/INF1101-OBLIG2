Qoruq (, also Romanized as Qoruq; also known as Bazar-e Kargarud and Qoroq) is a village in Saheli-ye Jokandan Rural District, in the Central District of Talesh County, Gilan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,160, in 274 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Talesh County
