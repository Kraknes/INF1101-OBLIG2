Siaqul-e Olya (, also Romanized as Siaqul-e 'Olya; also known as Siah Gol-e Bala and Siaqul) is a village in Mangur-e Sharqi Rural District, Khalifan District, Mahabad County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 126, in 15 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Mahabad County
