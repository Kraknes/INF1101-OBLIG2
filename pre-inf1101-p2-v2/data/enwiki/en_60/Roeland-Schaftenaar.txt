Roeland Pieter Schaftenaar (born July 29, 1988) is a Dutch professional basketball player for Caceres of the Spanish 2nd division. ==College career== Scaftenaar played NCAA Division I college basketball at Oregon State, from 2006 to 2010. ==Professional career== Schaftenaar started his pro career by playing four years in the LEB Oro league, the Spanish second division. On October 11, 2014, he signed with the Greek Basket League club Rethymno.Rethymno Aegean signed Roeland Schaftenaar. On January 24, 2015, he extended his contract with the club for another two years.Roeland Schaftenaar extends with Rethymno. After temporarily leaving the team after the first 4 games of the 2016-17 season, he returned to Rethymno on January 23, 2017, in order to replace Brandon Edwards. On September 12, 2017, he joined Faros Larissas of the Greek Basket League.Roeland Schaftenaar (ex Baskonia) is a newcomer at G.Larissa-Faros. ==National team career== Schaftenaar is a member of the senior Dutch national basketball team.
