Trimma is a genus of fish in the family Gobiidae native to the Indian and Pacific Ocean. Together with members of the genus Eviota, they are known commonly as pygmygobies or dwarfgobies. ==Species== Trimma caudomaculatum Trimma habrum Trimma lantana Trimma nasa Trimma naudei female 23.3 mm, Nha Trang, Vietnam Trimma okinawae Trimma tevegae There are currently 98 recognized species in this genus: * Trimma abyssum G.
