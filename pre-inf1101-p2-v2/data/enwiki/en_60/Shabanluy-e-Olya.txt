Shabanluy-e Olya (, also Romanized as Sha'banluy-e 'Olya; also known as Bilehverdi and Pileh Verdi) is a village in Rahal Rural District, in the Central District of Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 267, in 70 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Khoy County
