Tazehkand (, also Romanized as Tazehkand; also known as Tazehkand-e Chaghal) is a village in Chaldoran-e Shomali Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 482, in 104 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
