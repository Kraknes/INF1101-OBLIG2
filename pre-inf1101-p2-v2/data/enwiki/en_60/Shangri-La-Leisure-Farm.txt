The Shangri-La Leisure Farm () is a tourist attraction farm in Dongshan Township, Yilan County, Taiwan.https://followmywanders.wordpress.com/2014/02/16/shangrila-leisure- farm-%E3%80%90%E9%A6%99%E6%A0%BC%E9%87%8C%E6%8B%89%E4%BC%91%E9%97%B2%E5%86%9C%E5%9C%BA%E3%80%91/ ==History== The resort originated back from a simple fruit orchard. ==Features== The leisure farm is located about 250 meters above sea level with temperature conditions of four seasons. It overlooks valleys, rivers, plains, farms, islands and ocean. It has several natural ecology such as macaques, tree frogs, firebugs, butterflies and a variety of plants. The farm also regularly hosts several activities, such as lantern activity, whirligig activity, matzo ball activity and other do-it-yourself activity. It also houses some accommodation services for overnight stay or venues for business meetings. ==Transportation== The farm is accessible by taxi or bus from Luodong Station of Taiwan Railways Administration. ==See also== * List of tourist attractions in Taiwan ==References== ==External links== * Category:Farms in Yilan County, Taiwan
