Sheykh Saluy-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Sheykh Saluy-e Sofla and Sheikh Salooy Sofla; also known as Shaikh Silu Ashaghi, Sheykh Salu-ye Pa"in, Sheykh Salu-ye Sofla, Sheykh Selu-ye Pa'in, Sheykh Solu, Sheykh Solu-ye Pa"in, and Sheykh Solu-ye Sofla) is a village in Chaldoran-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 387, in 69 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
