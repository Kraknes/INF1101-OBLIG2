Qarni Yarikh (, also Romanized as Qarni Yarikh; also known as Qarni Yareq) is a village in Hajjilar-e Jonubi Rural District, Hajjilar District, Chaypareh County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its existence was noted, but its population was not reported. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaypareh County
