Seyyed Mansur (, also Romanized as Seyyed Mansur; also known as Seyah Mansur and Shah Mansur) is a village in Garmeh-ye Shomali Rural District, Kandovan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 188, in 42 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
