Qarah Baraz (, also Romanized as Qarah Baraz; also known as Qareh Barar) is a village in Behi-e Feyzolah Beygi Rural District, in the Central District of Bukan County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 213, in 45 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Bukan County
