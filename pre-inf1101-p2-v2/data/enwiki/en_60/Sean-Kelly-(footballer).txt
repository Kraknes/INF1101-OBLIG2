Sean Kelly (born 1 November 1993) is a Scottish footballer who plays as a defender, who last played for Ross County. Kelly has previously played for St Mirren, East Stirlingshire and AFC Wimbledon. ==Career== Kelly began his career with St Mirren and made his professional debut on 3 August 2013 in a 3-0 defeat against Inverness Caledonian Thistle. On 22 November 2013 it was announced that Kelly had signed a two-year contract extension with Saints, tying him to the club until the summer of 2016. On 5 March 2014, Kelly appeared for the Scotland under-21 side in a 2-2 draw against Hungary at Tannadice. Kelly was released by St Mirren at the end of the 2015-16 season. Kelly went on trial with AFC Wimbledon during the 2016-17 pre-season, and permanently signed for the club on 1 August 2016. Kelly scored his first goal for the Dons on 14 January, in a 2-1 win against Oxford United.
