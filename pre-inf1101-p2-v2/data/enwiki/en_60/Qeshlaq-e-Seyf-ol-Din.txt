Qeshlaq-e Seyf ol Din (, also Romanized as Qeshlaq-e Seyf ol Din and Qeshlaq-e Seyf ed Din; also known as Qeshlaq) is a village in Kandovan Rural District, Kandovan District, Meyaneh County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 306, in 54 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Meyaneh County
