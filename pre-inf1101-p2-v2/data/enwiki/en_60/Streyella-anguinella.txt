Streyella anguinella is a moth of the family Gelechiidae. It is found from south-western Europe to the southern part of the Ural Mountains and the Lower Volga. Outside of Europe, it is found in Turkey, the Near East and North Africa. et al. 2010: The gelechiid fauna of the southern Ural Mountains, part II: list of recorded species with taxonomic notes (Lepidoptera: Gelechiidae).
