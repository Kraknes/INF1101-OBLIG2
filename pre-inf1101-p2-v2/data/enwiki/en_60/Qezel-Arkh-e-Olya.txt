Qezel Arkh-e Olya (, also Romanized as Qezel Arkh-e 'Olya; also known as Qezel Arkh) is a village in Chaybasar-e Shomali Rural District, Bazargan District, Maku County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 43, in 8 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Maku County
