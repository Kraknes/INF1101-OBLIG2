Stanislas Dombeck (26 September 1931 - 18 September 2013) was a French footballer who played at both professional and international levels as a midfielder and forward. ==Career== Born in Crisenoy, Dombeck played professionally for Amiens, Stade Francais and Rennes. After playing amateur football, he became manager of Tours.
