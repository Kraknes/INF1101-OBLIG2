Senziq (, also Romanized as Senziq, Sanaziq, and Sanziq; also known as Sanzigh and Sinzik) is a village in Aghmiyun Rural District, in the Central District of Sarab County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 719, in 163 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Sarab County
