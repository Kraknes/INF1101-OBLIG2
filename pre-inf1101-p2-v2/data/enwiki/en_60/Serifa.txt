Serifa is a slab serif typeface family created by Adrian Frutiger in 1967. The typeface is based on the Univers family. ==Usage== It was most prominently featured in the logo of Montgomery Ward from 1982-92 and again in the revived online store. It was also used in the campaign ads for Ross Perot's 1992 Presidential campaignhttps://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DVGlVg81fow and from 1982 to late 1987 on the graphics of various CBS News programming. In 2014 Jake Tilson used it in the NT signage to complement the NT logo.http://www.jaketilson.com/design/national/index.html Serifa is also used in nearly all of the College Board's communications and exams. == References == Category:Typefaces and fonts introduced in 1964 Category:Typefaces designed by Adrian Frutiger Category:Serif typefaces Category:Slab serif typefaces
