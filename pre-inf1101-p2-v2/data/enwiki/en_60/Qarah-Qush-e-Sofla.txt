Qarah Qush-e Sofla (, also Romanized as Qarah Qush-e Sofla and Qareh Qush-e Sofla; also known as Qara Kush Ashaghi, Qareh Qush-e Pa"in, and Qarehqush Pa"in) is a village in Sokmanabad Rural District, Safayyeh District, Khoy County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 102, in 28 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Khoy County
