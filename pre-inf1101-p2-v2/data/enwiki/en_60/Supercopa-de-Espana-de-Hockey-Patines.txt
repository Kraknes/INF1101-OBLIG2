The Supercopa de Espana de Hockey Patines is an annual Spanish rink hockey tournament played by the winners and runners-up of both OK Liga and Copa del Rey. It's managed by Federacion Espanola de Patinaje. Until 2012, if a team won both OK Liga and Copa del Rey, its Supercopa opponent was the Copa del Rey runners-up.
