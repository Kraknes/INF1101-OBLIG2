Special National Site is the status given to sites of exceptional natural, historical, cultural and archaeological interest in Vietnam. They are national sites proposed by the Ministry of Culture, Sports and Tourism and approved by the Prime Minister. Currently there are 48 Special National Sites, seven of which are UNESCO World Heritage Sites. ==Classification== The Special National Sites of Vietnam include: *Four sites of scenic beauty: Ha Long Bay, Ba Be Lake, Cat Tien National Park and the Trang An-Tam Coc-Bich Dong Complex. *Cultural heritages: But Thap Temple, Dau Pagoda, Citadel of the Ho Dynasty, Hoi An
