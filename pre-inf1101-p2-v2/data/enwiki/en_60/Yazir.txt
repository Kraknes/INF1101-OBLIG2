Yazir were a past tribe of Oghuz Turks. Today, the name refers to: * Yazir, Cubuk, a village in the district of Cubuk, Ankara Province, Turkey * Yazir, Finike, a village in the district of Finike, Antalya Province, Turkey * Yazir, Karacasu, a village in the district of Karacasu, Aydin Province, Turkey * Yazir, Korkuteli, a village in the district of Korkuteli, Antalya Province, Turkey * Yazir, Kumluca, a village in the district of Kumluca, Antalya Province, Turkey
