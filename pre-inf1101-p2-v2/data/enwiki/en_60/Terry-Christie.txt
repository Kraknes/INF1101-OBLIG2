Terry Christie (born 1942/1943) is a Scottish former football player and manager. ==Career== Christie played as a full back and winger for Dundee, Raith Rovers, Hawick Royal Albert, Stirling Albion and Newtongrange Star. He later became a manager and was in charge of Newtongrange Star, Meadowbank Thistle, Stenhousemuir and Alloa Athletic. Christie won the 1995 Scottish Challenge Cup Final while manager of Stenhousemuir.
