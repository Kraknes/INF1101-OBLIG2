Yengi Kandi (, also Romanized as Yengi Kandi; also known as Yangi Kand, Yengi Kand, Yengikend, and Yeyin Kand) is a village in Lahijan Rural District, Khosrowshahr District, Tabriz County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,411, in 337 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Tabriz County
