Wakaf Bharu-Kota Bharu-Kubang Kerian Highway or WKK Highway (Lebuhraya WKK), Federal Route 3, is a major highway in Kota Bharu, Kelantan, Malaysia. Kota Bharu flyover is the first flyover built in Kota Bharu.
