Qezel Bolagh (, also Romanized as Qezel Bolagh; also known as Ghezel Bolagh, Kizil Bulak, and Qizil Bualaq) is a village in Avajiq-e Shomali Rural District, Dashtaki District, Chaldoran County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 360, in 87 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Chaldoran County
