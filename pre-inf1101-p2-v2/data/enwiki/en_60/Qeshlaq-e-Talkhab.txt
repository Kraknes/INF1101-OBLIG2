Qeshlaq-e Talkhab (, also Romanized as Qeshlaq-e Talkhab) is a village in Mokriyan-e Shomali Rural District, in the Central District of Miandoab County, West Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 250, in 47 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Miandoab County
