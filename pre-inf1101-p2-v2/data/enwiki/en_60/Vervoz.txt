La chapelle Saint-Hubert. Vervoz is a Belgian hamlet of the municipality of Clavier in the Walloon region, in the province of Liege. It is close to the borders of the three provinces of Liege, Namur and Luxembourg and lies near the village of Ocquier. The hamlet is on the ancient Roman road Tongres-Arlon. It is registered on the list of exceptional heritage of Wallonia. ==Places of interest== Tornaco Castle, the small chapel nearby as well as the surrounding farms all have the same building style, constructed in the grey stone characteristic of the region.
